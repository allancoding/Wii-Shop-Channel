Purchasing Add-On Content

Add-on content for this game (referred to as "stories") can be bought by selecting "Purchase Add-On Content" from the title menu. Purchasing add-on content requires Wii Points (must be purchased separately).

● The Purchase Add-On Content Screen

Here, you can choose the story you want to buy from a list of those available. You can view details like the description of the selected story as well as your current Wii Points, the number of Wii Points needed to purchase the selected story, and the number of blocks it requires. Choose "Yes" to finalize your purchase.

When your Wii console doesn't have enough free space...

In order to save any of the stories you will need to have the appropriate amount of blocks available on your Wii console (number of blocks required per story can be located below). If you do not have the required space available, delete or copy data to an SD Card from the Save data management screen (refer to the Wii Operations Manual: Channels and Settings for more details).

 

When you don't have enough Wii Points...

Please visit the Wii Shop Channel or the Nintendo homepage for details on how to purchase Wii Points.

 

● Delete Downloaded Stories

You may remove purchased stories from your Wii system memory by deleting it within the Save data management screen (refer to your Wii Operations Manual Channels and Settings for more details).

● Story Outline

These stories are add-on content for use with "FINAL FANTASY IV: The After Years" and cannot be played on their own.

 

 

Rydia's Tale "The Eidolons Shackled"

 

Wii Points: 300 Wii Points
Blocks required: 5 Blocks

Available: 6/01/09

Rydia, returning to her second homeland in the Feymarch, finds its queen cold and unwelcome. Suddenly, the Eidolon King and all his subjects freeze, as if turned to stone. Together with Luca, daughter of the dwarven king, Rydia heads to the Dwarven Castle to investigate.

Yang's Tale "The Master of Fabul"

 

Wii Points: 300 Wii Points
Blocks required: 5 Blocks

Available: 7/06/09

Yang serves as king of Fabul, but still he trains on a daily basis with his loyal monks. One day his daughter, Princess Ursula, slips away from the castle. Traveling to Mount Hobs to retrieve
her, Yang is shocked to find the Red Wings in the sky, ready to invade his country...

Palom's Tale "The Mage's Voyage"

 

Wii Points: 300 Wii Points
Blocks required: 4 Blocks

Available: 7/06/09

Palom the black mage has sworn to follow in the late Tellah's footsteps and become a sage. His elder sends him to Troia to train Leonora, a novice Epopt, in black magic. What does a peaceful kingdom need such powerful magic for? The upheavals rocking the entire world are about to land on Troia's doorstep...

Edge's Tale "The Pulse of Babil"

 

Wii Points: 300 Wii Points
Blocks required: 7 Blocks

Available: 7/06/09

Edge, honoring his parents' dying wish, is now king of Eblan. His kingdom is prosperous once more, but the Tower of Babil's ominous glow has returned. To investigate this dour omen, Edge sends four of his most faithful ninja on covert missions spanning the entire globe.

Edward's Tale "Star-Crossed Damcyan"
