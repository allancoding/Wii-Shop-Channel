Magic List

This section outlines some of the white and black magic available to your party. There are also various summons and other types of magic for you to discover.

 

White Magic

Cure

(MP: 3)

Restores a small amount of HP. Damages undead enemies.

Cura

(MP: 9)

Restores more HP than Cure. Damages undead enemies.

Curaga

(MP: 18)

Restores more HP than Cura. Damages undead enemies.

Esuna

(MP: 20)

Cures all status ailments except KO.

Raise

(MP: 8)

Cures the KO status ailment.

Blink

(MP: 8)

Creates a copy of the caster that defends against physical attack.

Protect
(MP: 9)

Raises defense against physical attack.

Shell

(MP: 10)

Raises defense against magical attack.

Float

(MP: 8)

Levitates selected member or members of the party into the air, avoiding ground-based damage.

Hold

(MP: 5)

Keeps the target from moving for a period of time.

Silence

(MP: 6)

Inflicts the target with the Silence status ailment.

Confuse

(MP: 10)

Inflicts the target with the Confuse status ailment.

Slow

(MP: 14)

Slows down the target.

Libra

(MP: 1)

Reveals the target's HP, max HP, and weaknesses.

Berserk

(MP: 18)

Turns an ally or an enemy into a berserker, raising his or her attack power.

Teleport

(MP: 10)

Teleport away from battles and dungeons.

Mini

(MP: 6)

Inflicts the target with the Mini status ailment, or restores him or her back to normal.

Sight

(MP: 2)

View a map of the world while traveling.

Black Magic

Fire

(MP: 5)

Damages the target with fireballs.

Fira

(MP: 15)

A flame attack more powerful than Fire.

Blizzard
(MP: 5)

Damages the target with balls of ice.

Blizzara

(MP: 15)

An ice attack more powerful than Blizzard.

Thunder
(MP: 5)

Damages the target with a bolt of lightning.

Thundara
(MP: 15)

A lightning attack more powerful than Thunder.

Poison

(MP: 2)

Poisons the target.

Bio

(MP: 20)

Damages the target with giant bacteria. The damage continues each turn afterward.

Warp

(MP: 4)

Returns you to the previous floor of the current dungeon.

Toad

(MP: 7)

Turns the target into a toad, or turns a toad back to normal.

Pig

(MP: 1)

Turns the target into a pig, or turns a pig back to normal.

Sleep

(MP: 12)

Puts the target to sleep.

Stop

(MP: 15)

Stops the target from moving for a period of time.

Drain

(MP: 18)

Absorbs the target's HP and uses it to replenish your own.

Osmose
(MP: 0)

Absorbs the target's MP and uses it to replenish your own.
