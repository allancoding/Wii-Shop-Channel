Commands

Some battle commands can be executed by every character, while other commands (called "abilities") are unique to certain party members. This section outlines the common commands as well as some abilities.

Press right to access the "Band," "Defend," and "Swap Rows" commands.

● Attack (common)

Execute a physical attack with the character's currently equipped weapon. You will have to choose the target to attack.

 

● Items (common)

Use an item from your inventory. Select an item, then the target to use it on.

 

Changing Equipment

If you press up beyond the upper edge of the item window, the items a character has equipped in their hands will be displayed. To change this equipment, first select the equipped item and then the new item that will replace it.

● Band (common)

Execute a previously learned Band, or try to learn a new Band.

 

What are Bands?

A Band is a combination attack carried out by two or more characters simultaneously. You can learn new Bands as the story progresses. They can also be learned by searching for them during battle.

 

Searching for Bands

To search for a Band, select the "Band" command, and then "Search for Bands." You will be asked to choose the characters to team up with and the battle command you wish to use for each member of the Band. After inputting commands for all the participating characters, press to execute the Band; alternatively, if there are no more available characters to select to search for a Band, you can press to execute the Band with the current members. If you have found a correct combination of characters and commands, you will unleash a Band move; the Band will also be added permanently to your list of Bands. Otherwise, the Band is a failure and you will learn no new moves.

 

Using Bands

Once you learn a Band, you can execute it simply by selecting its name from the menu. Bands consume MP from all the characters involved.

 

● Defend (common)

Protect yourself, reducing the amount of damage enemy attacks cause.

● Swap Rows (common)

This command swaps characters between the front and back rows. Since a character's choice of row affects the damage taken by enemy attacks (→ P. 13), it's a good idea to swap rows quickly if enemies attack from behind (a "back attack").

● White Magic (ability)

Cast any white magic you've learned (→ P. 23).

● Black Magic (ability)

Cast any black magic you've learned (→ P. 23).

● Awaken

(Ceodore only)

This move maximizes your HP and gives you stat bonuses for a limited time. The effect runs out after executing a certain amount of actions, reducing your HP to single digits.

 

● Cover (Cecil only)

Take physical damage in the place of other characters.

 

 

 

● Blessing (Rosa only)

Restore a small amount of HP and MP to all allies. Be aware that this occasionally fails.

 

● When using magic on an entire group

Some magic can be used to affect the entire party or all enemies on screen. Press left or right in the direction of the desired targets. This will select all possible targets in the group.
