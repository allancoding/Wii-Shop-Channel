About the Adventure

This game is broadly divided into three sections: the field map, the menu, and the battle screen.

● Field map (→ P. 12)

On the field map, you will travel around the world, talk to people, and carry out the main part of your adventure. In addition to the main world map, you will also travel through towns and dungeons during your explorations.

 

● Menu screen

  (→ P. 13-19)

Press on the field map to bring up the menu screen, allowing you to use items, cast magic, and check the status of your characters.

 

● Battle screen

  (→ P. 20-23)

You will see this screen when you run into an enemy, either on the field map or as part of the story. You fight the enemy by giving battle commands to each character in your party.

 

● Time and Moon Phases

The passage of time is depicted in this game using the phases of the moon. The moon's phase changes every 30 minutes of real time, or when you rest at an inn or use a tent or cottage. The current moon phase affects the effectiveness of your battle commands (→ P. 20).
