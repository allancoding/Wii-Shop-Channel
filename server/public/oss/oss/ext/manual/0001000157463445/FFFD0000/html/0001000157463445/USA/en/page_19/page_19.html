Settings

Here you will find various settings for the game. Press up or down to select an option and left or right to adjust individual settings. If you press while selecting "Adjust Screen" a specialized window for screen adjustment will appear. Press to return to the previous screen once you've finished making adjustments.

 

Battle Mode

Choose between "Wait"—which stops the passage of time during battle when you are selecting commands—and "Active," where time passes normally during all parts of battle.

Battle Speed

Choose between 6 different speed levels for how fast time flows during battle.
Battle Messages

 

Choose between 6 different speed levels for how long messages are displayed during battle.

Window Color

Change the color of the windows by adjusting the red, green, and blue levels.

Adjust Screen

Adjust how the game is displayed on your television screen.

Language

Switch the language used for the game between English and French.

Controller

Assignment

 

Assign the characters controlled in battle to each controller when playing with multiple players.

Return to Title

Return to the title screen without saving.
