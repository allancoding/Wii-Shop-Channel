Controls

Control the cursor with the Wii Remote and look for groups of three or more identical objects. Point at one of these objects with the Wii Remote and press the A Button to destroy it. Your score depends on the number of objects in the group; the higher the number of objects, the higher the score. You may find a yellow tile underneath an object; hit it with your hammer to make it disappear. You have finished the level when you have destroyed all the colored tiles.


There may be only ONE object in your way to prevent you from achieving that really big score, so use the hand to get rid of it. You can do this a maximum of five times. Point with the cursor at the object you want to throw away. Use the B Button on the Wii Remote to pick the object up and swing the Wii Remote to throw the object out of the play area.


Your hammer gauge will fill up a little way if you discard at least four objects simultaneously. You can activate the BIG hammer when the gauge reaches 25%. Point the Wii Remote at the spot you want to target, hold it steady for a few moments and swing the Wii Remote up and down. You will clear a large area of the game field at one stroke; the higher level on the gauge, the mightier the hammer blow will be.
