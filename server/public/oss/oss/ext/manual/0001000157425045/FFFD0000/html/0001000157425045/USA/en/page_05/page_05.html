The Story

In 7 billion years our sun is on the climax of its life. Being a red giant, it shines brighter than ever.

 


 

It is the birthplace of ZeLeLi.

 

Unifying the essence of time, life and light in them, these gearwheels are born inside of the enormous star. The ZeLeLi swarm to all directions and spread the seed of life and light everywhere and in all time.



But where there is light, there also is shadow.


From a dark part of the universe, lured by the bright shine of the sun and the ZeLeLi, gathers an evil force in our solar system in order to seal away this power forever.
 


Black Plättchen enclose the ZeLeLi to absorb them and take all of their energy. The strong light of the fighting gearwheels passes their outer barrier and makes the Plättchen shine in all colors of the rainbow. 

Between adjacent Plättchen of the same color arises such a huge power that the ZeLeLi with their united forces become able to destroy the Shadow Plättchen from inside and regain their freedom.
Those that are not able to do that in time lose all of their power and the Plättchen become pure black.

 


Being part of a group of highly advanced beings that guard the earth for aeons, you have to use your fantasy to help the ZeLeLi to destroy the evil, black Shadow Plättchen in all sectors of space and to save us all.
