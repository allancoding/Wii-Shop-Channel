Level Preview

The Level Preview shows you the repeating, fixed sequence of Plättchen and Items that fall in from all four directions. On the right, you can see the sequence of gravity changes, on the left there is the Color Wheel with available colors.



 

Move the cursor up and down on this screen to see the changes between the different Energy Levels.

 

In some levels could be hidden items and Plättchen outside of the preview range.

 

Press during gameplay to display a simple form of the Level Preview.
