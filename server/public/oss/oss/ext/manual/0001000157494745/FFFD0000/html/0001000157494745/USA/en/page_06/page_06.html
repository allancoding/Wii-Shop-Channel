How to Play
The goal of Incoming is to defeat your opponent by  destroying  their tanks using the  arsenal you have at your disposal. Advanced arsenal becomes available at different levels of the game.

 

Tank

The goal of the tank is to blast your opponents by lobbing mortar shells at them.  The higher the shot, the more damage the mortar shell will cause.

Shoot: Using the Wii Remote, point the arrow cursor at the tank.  While pressing and holding the A Button, move the arrow upward at an angle.  The farther you stretch it, the farther it’ll shoot.  Releasing the A Button will fire your shot.

Move: To move your tank, point the arrow cursor at the tank.  While pressing and holding the A Button, move the arrow left or right.  Releasing the A Button will move the tank to that location.

 

Missiles

The missiles are an excellent offensive weapon and never miss their target but they can be shot down by the satellites.

Shoot:To shoot a missile, select a launch button on the screen.  Depending on the button you press, your missile comes from the row that it will attack on your opponent’s side.

 

Satellite

The satellite is used to shoot down your opponent’s missiles and collect power-ups. 

Shoot: Moving the arrow cursor into the sky area will turn it into a crosshair.  When in this mode, the satellite will fire when the A Button is pressed.  Try to lead your shot as it takes time for it to intercept the target.
