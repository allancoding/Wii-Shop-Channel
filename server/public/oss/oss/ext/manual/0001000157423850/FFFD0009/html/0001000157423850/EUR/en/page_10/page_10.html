In-Game
Wii Remote: Before releasing ball: Swing to throw.
After releasing ball: Tilt to the right or to the left to control after the ball touches down.
 
+Control Pad:
Set ANGLE or POSITION before throwing.
A Button:
Switch functions of the +Control Pad between ANGLE and POSITION.
B Button:
Before releasing the ball: Hold the B Button and swing the Wii Remote to throw the ball.
After releasing ball: Hold the B Button then tilt the Wii Remote to direct the ball after it touches down.
1 Button: Display the pause menu.
In-game, after throwing the ball:
When the ball is rolling on the lane:
After throwing, hold and tilt the Wii Remote to the left or to the right to control the direction in which the ball moves.
Caution:
1. When the ball has reached the end of the lane, you cannot control it anymore.
2. Be careful! If you hold the B Button for too long, the ball may go into the gutter!
