How to play
  • Game mechanics

    Your goal is to change the color of all the grey cells of the game field into gold cells within the time limit. You can swap 2 adjacent stones, as long as this results in making 3 or more of a kind in a row or column. When this occurs, those stones disappear and cells contained those stones turn into gold. When the board turns entirely into gold, you progress to the next level. Additionally, stones can be cleared using the magic stones.
