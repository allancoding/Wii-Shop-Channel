Magic Stones

If you clear 4 or more stones horizontally, they turn into a magic stone that eliminates all stones in a row when activated.

If you clear 4 or more stones vertically they turn into one magic stone that eliminates all stones in a column, when activated.

If you clear 5 stones that form a cross (3 cells horizontally that cross 3 cells vertically), they turn into a magic stone that eliminates all adjacent stones when activated.
