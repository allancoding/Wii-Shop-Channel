Main Menu
  • Story mode
    This is the main game mode, where you can progress into the game, and unlock the pieces of ancient manuscript. According to the ancient prophecy, the manuscript should provide you with help from the Island Spirits.

  • Free Play
    Here you can choose to play in any level that was already completed in Story Mode. However, you will be unable to progress to the new levels.

  • Options
    Here you can adjust Music and Sound options and manage your Profiles.

  • High Score
    Here you can check High Scores.
