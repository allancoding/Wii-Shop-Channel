Game Overview
Gameloft’s Ghost Mansion Party is a board game that is sure to bring hours of fun to all players and encourages playing with family and friends. Four characters, controlled by a combination of human players and computer-controlled players (if human players are not available), enter a mysterious ghost mansion and work together to find a huge treasure. The players need to cooperate or compete in On-Board Challenges and a wide range of mini-games.

Story

 Four people (a boy, a girl, a butler, and an old man) arrive at a decrepit and terrifying mansion. At the gate, they are startled by a kind but clumsy ghost named Fazzy who appears out of thin air to greet the living. Fazzy tells them this mansion used to be his home, but some evil ghosts locked his treasure in a hidden chamber that can only be accessed with a secret password. Fazzy asks the newcomers to help him get rid of these ghosts and promises to give them his treasure as a reward. They agree to help Fazzy and immediately embark on an adventure in the ghost mansion, where they will face many challenges as they struggle to find the magic scrolls that will provide clues for them to figure out the password.

So, let’s hurry and begin our quest to find the treasure!
