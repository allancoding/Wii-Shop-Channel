Receiving New Fish
You will receive a new fish a couple times a week. When you feed them frequently (several times a day), the probability that you will get a new fish may go up.

● Receive Fish Over Time
You will receive a new fish a couple times a week.

 

* If you do not view your aquarium regularly, it may take longer to receive a new fish.

● Receive Fish from Friends' Aquariums
If there are any new fish in an aquarium you receive from a friend, during an exchange, one random fish from it will be added to your Aqua Center.

 

* Once a fish is added from a received aquarium, it will take more time to receive a new fish.
