Helix: How to Play


Your job is to watch the robot and move your arms as he does. The robot does a move and then you repeat that move.
In the example above, you would do the move with your left hand (not right!) after the robot does this move.



The Rhythm Track on top of the screen tells you when it is time for you to do a move. When the robot does a move, a Move Bar appears on the right end of the Rhythm Track.



The Move Bar starts traveling left on the Rhythm Track after it appears.




You start to perform the move when the Move Bar reaches the vertical line in center of the Rhythm Track.

 

Your move should be halfway performed when the Move Bar is halfway across (when the sphere reaches the center vertical line)

 

Your move should finish when the Move Bar is no longer on the center vertical line.

 

Example:

  • Begin to move your arm out when the Move Bar first touches the center vertical line.
  • Your arm should be fully extended when the sphere reaches the center and the Move Bar is halfway through the vertical line.
  • Your arm should be fully retracted back to your body when the Move Bar is no longer on the center vertical line.



If the Move Bar turns green, you did it right. If it becomes red, you did it wrong.
