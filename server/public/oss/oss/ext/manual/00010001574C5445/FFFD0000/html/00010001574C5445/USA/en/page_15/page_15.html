Answering Phone Calls

In 12 of the puzzle rooms, a phone will begin ringing shortly after the level starts. This is Rachael making a phone call to Jake. The player can answer these phone calls by walking Jake up to the flashing red phone and pressing the A Button while it’s still ringing. Each time Rachael calls Jake, the phone will ring 6 times before she hangs up. If the player is able to reach the phone before the final ring, Rachael’s call will be answered. If the player reaches the phone after that, Rachael’s call will be missed.

 

If the player is able to answer all 12 phone calls while progressing through the game, they will receive the game’s Good Ending, as well as an extra unlockable. If the player answers less than all 12 phone calls, they will receive the game’s Bad Ending, and will not receive the extra unlockable. In order for a phone call to count, it must be answered on the same attempt that the exit door is reached. Answering a phone call, failing, and then proceeding to the exit door on the following attempt will not count as having successfully answered that phone call.
