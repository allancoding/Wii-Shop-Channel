Ranking List
There are two divisions for sumo wrestlers: juryo (lower) and makuuchi (higher). Within the makuuchi division, there are several different levels: maegashira, komusubi, sekiwake, ozeki, and the top--yokozuna.

Each sumo wrestler starts out as a juryo at the beginning of the game. Your first goal is to be promoted to makuuchi. Once promoted to makuuchi, you will start off as a 16th rank maegashira.

You will be promoted when you win a set number of matches in a tournament.

If you win more than 8 matches in one tournament, you will advance to the next tournament.

Aim to become a yokozuna by fighting in tournaments throughout the world and gaining as many promotions as you can.

*The criteria for this ranking list apply only to this game, not real sumo.
