Chanko
Controls
Hold the Wii Remote so that it points to the screen. Point to an area where 2 or more of the same ingredients are placed side-by-side. Press the A Button to get those ingredients.

How to Play
Make chanko by collecting "dishes," "meat," and "vegetables." New ingredients will be added once the sumo wrestler finishes eating the chanko you made. The amount of ingredients that you collect affects the size of the chanko that you can make. The bigger the chanko, the more calories you take in.

If you collect enough ingredients while the sumo wrestler is eating, you'll get to have a refill. Having refills will add bonus points to the number of calories you take in.

There is a limit to the number of times you can collect ingredients. Regardless of whether or not you were able to make chanko, the game ends if you reach this limit with one of the ingredients.

Parameters that Increase
Health and Weight
CAUTION
The Wii Remote is held pointing towards the screen during this mini-game only. Please keep this in mind when playing.
