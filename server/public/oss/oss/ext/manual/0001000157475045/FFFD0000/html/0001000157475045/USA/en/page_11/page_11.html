Switching the Camera

If you point to Change Camera on the aquarium menu and press , you can switch the camera and view the aquarium from different angles. If you press or , you can switch to, respectively, Autocamera 1 or Autocamera 2. Press either button again to switch to the center camera.

● Camera Types 
There are five camera types. If you point to Camera and press , the camera type will switch.

Center

The entire aquarium will be displayed.
Camera 1

This camera shows the aquarium from the left or the top.
Camera 2

This camera shows the aquarium from the right.
Autocamera 1

This camera’s angle and position automatically change.
Autocamera 2

This camera’s angle and position automatically change. The background is black regardless of the aquarium settings (→P.13).

*If 10 minutes pass without any control input, the camera will automatically switch to Autocamera 1. However, when fish or Mii characters (→P.12) are selected and swimming automatically in the aquarium, the camera will not switch.
