Getting started

The Strap Usage screen will be displayed first. Once you have properly placed your hand through the wrist strap, press to proceed to the title screen (shown on the right).

 

In this game the Wii Remote is only used in the vertical position.

At the title screen press to proceed to the icon selection screen (shown on the right). Here you can either choose one of the four default icons or one of the Miis stored in your Wii system memory. When you achieve a new high score the chosen icon will be displayed in the score table. After you finish selecting your icon the game will start.

 

For more information about creating Miis, please see your Wii Operations Manual.

 

Potpourrii can also be played by two players simultaneously. In order to join, the second player has to press on their Wii Remote at the icon selection screen. After that the second player will be able to choose an icon just like the first player.

 

By pressing either player can cancel their participation in the game.
