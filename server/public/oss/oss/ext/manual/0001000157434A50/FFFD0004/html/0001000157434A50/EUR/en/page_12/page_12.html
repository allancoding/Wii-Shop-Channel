Race Mode

This mode is for 2 to 4 players. Get ready for a crazy race with your friends and be the first to reach the top of the course!

 

Starting a race

In the main menu, select "Race" to start a race. Then select your character with the +Control Pad or the Nunchuk. The same character can be selected by two players at most. In this case, they will appear in a different colour.

 

Game principle

At the beginning of the game each player has 3 life points. The aim is to reach the top of the course. The player who advances the most regulates the game! Make sure to always be visible on the screen or you will lose a life! In this case, if you have at least one life left, you have a few seconds to decide if you want to resume the race by pressing the A Button.
