Battle Mode

In this mode exclusively for two players you must confront your opponent and win the battle by eliminating all the energy points first.

 

Starting a battle

In the main menu, select "Battle" to start a battle. Then select your character with the +Control Pad or the Nunchuk. The same character can be selected by both players. In this case they will appear in a different colour.

 

Game principle

You must get energy and fill the gauge on the lower part of the screen. This will allow you to use your weapons (arches and pitchforks). In this mode, teleporters can take you from one end of the level to the other which can be useful to escape from your opponent... or fall into his trap! Attention: you only have 5 life points!

 

Gauge

You must fill it to be able to use the weapons.

Bonuses

Use them to fill the gauge.

Cup

Number of victories.
