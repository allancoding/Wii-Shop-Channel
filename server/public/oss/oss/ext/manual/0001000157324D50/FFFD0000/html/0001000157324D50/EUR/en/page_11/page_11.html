GAME SIDE VIEW①

BLASTER MASTER™ OVERDRIVE features two modes of gameplay, Side View and Top View.
SIDE VIEW MODE
In Side View mode, the player can explore the world by controlling the movement and action of S.O.P.H.I.A. from a side perspective.
　

●S.O.P.H.I.A. CONTROL SCREEN

　


① HP (Hit Point) Gauge
This gauge measures the health of S.O.P.H.I.A. and decreases when S.O.P.H.I.A is hit.


② EN (Energy) Gauge
The Energy Gauge decreases when add-on Special Equipment for S.O.P.H.I.A. is used.


③ Equipment
This indicates equipment available for use. Equipment can be changed by using the A Button.
