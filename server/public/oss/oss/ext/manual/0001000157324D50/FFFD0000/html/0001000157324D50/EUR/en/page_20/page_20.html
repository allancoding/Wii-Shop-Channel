TOP VIEW ACTION②

●MAIN WEAPONS
Three different types of main weapons can be equipped by Alex. The player can switch between them at will.
GUN
Standard　projectile-issuing weapon to attack enemies.
   
 
HOMING MISSILE
A Projectile that seeks out　and　destroys enemies.
   
 
GRENADE
Although supplies of this are short, it’s a powerful way to take out enemies in a larger area.

　
There are five power levels.
Every GP (Gun Power) Generator obtained increases the level by one. Damage received decreases the level by one.

●BLAST BOMB
A Blast Bomb that can damage enemies over a wider area than the gun is also available to Alex. The size of the blast area depends on the Main Weapon equipped. The Blast Bomb can be obtained on the map.
