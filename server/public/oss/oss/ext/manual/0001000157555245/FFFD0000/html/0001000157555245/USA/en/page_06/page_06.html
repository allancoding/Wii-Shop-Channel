Enemies
  • Landrunners

    Landrunners hunt you, while you are trying to develop new chunks of land. If a Landrunner bumps into you or your trail, you’ll lose a life. However, Landrunners are too heavy to move on the paved roads and can only travel off-road. If you are on a paved road, you are safe.

  • Helisharks

    Helisharks are watching you from the sky. When an opportunity arises, they will try to eat you. Urbanix is too hard for their teeth though, so it takes some time for them to chew it up.

    Try to avoid their attacks, but if you have been caught, shake the Wii Remote quickly to escape from the Helishark’s jaws.



  • Housecrashers

    Housecrashers are malicious, but cowardly creatures. They cannot damage Urbanix itself, however they can destroy houses that have already been built, while you are developing other areas in other parts of the level. If the house is completely destroyed, this area will be deducted from your developed areas and will become unsuitable for further building. The Housecrashers can travel on paved roads only. If you want to scare Houscrashers, move closer to them. Once they see you - they run away in fear and leave the houses alone.
