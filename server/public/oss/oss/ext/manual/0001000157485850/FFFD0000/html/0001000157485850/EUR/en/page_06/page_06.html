Helix: What is it?

Helix is a music rhythm game. By moving Wii Remotes through the air, you do several different moves to the beat of the music.


The game senses whether you are doing the moves correctly or not using the Wii Remotesâ€™ motion sensors.

You perform moves based on the robot you see on the screen. He demonstrates moves, and you perform them to the beat of the music.

There are dozens of songs to play. Songs come in 3 difficulties - easy, medium, and hard. As you complete songs, new ones are unlocked. Try to unlock all of the songs!

 

Note: 2 Wii Remotes are strongly recommended when playing this game!
