What Is Pokémon Rumble?

Pokémon™ Rumble is an action game in which you control Toy Pokémon to defeat wave after wave of Pokémon while befriending some of them. Your goal is to be the last Pokémon standing!

 

・ Befriend Strong Pokémon

Sometimes when you defeat a Toy Pokémon (referred to as “Pokémon”), you can befriend it. As you play, you can switch freely among Pokémon you have befriended. As you progress through the game, stronger Pokémon will appear, so be sure to befriend as many as you can!

 

・ Aim to Be the Winner of the Battle Royale

Once you’ve befriended a Pokémon with a certain level of Power or higher, you may compete in the Battle Royale, where many Pokémon fight all at once.

 

・ Cooperate with Friends

Up to 4 players can play together at the same time. Players can combine their strengths and work together to take down strong Bosses and fight to the end of the Battle Royale.
