Game Progression

Pokémon Rumble is an action game in which you control Toy Pokémon to defeat wave after wave of Pokémon while befriending some of them. Your goal is to be the last Pokémon standing!

The Terminal is where you prepare to challenge the Stages and the Battle Royale.

 

Your Pokémon

Game Progression

This game is divided into four Ranks (levels): C, B, A, and S. You advance through the Ranks in this order: C→B→A→S. Each Rank has a Terminal, Stages, and a Battle Royale. Once you’ve cleared the Battle Royale, you can advance to the next Rank.

 

Terminal (See p. 11: Terminal)

This is the hub of the game. From here, you can go to the Stages or the Battle Royale, or use any of the facilities.

 

 

 

Stages (See p. 12: Stages)

The Stages are divided into several areas and are populated with different types of Pokémon. If you defeat a Pokémon, you might get a chance to befriend it. You can switch out the Pokémon you are controlling for other Pokémon that you have befriended. At the end of each Stage, you’ll meet a Boss.

 

Battle Royale (See p. 13: Battle Royale)

Once you have befriended a Pokémon that meets the minimum Power requirement for the Battle Royale, you can take this challenge. Fight the multitude of Pokémon in the arena and defeat them all in order to win.
