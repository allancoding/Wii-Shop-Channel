Battle Royale

How to Begin the Battle Royale

When advancing to the Battle Royale from the Terminal, you will arrive near the Battle Royale arena.

Once you are prepared, use the green Launch Pad in the back of the area to advance to the Battle Royale. Use the red Launch Pad out front to return to the Terminal.

 

・ Battle Royale Opening Screen

Before the Battle Royale begins, information about the Prize ( you’ll receive if you win) and Rivals (powerful opponents that will appear in the Battle Royale) will be displayed.

 

Battle Royale Progress and Rules

In the Battle Royale, many Pokémon gather inside the arena to battle it out among themselves.

Defeat all opponents to clear the Battle Royale. However, if you run out of Keys or time, your battle is lost and you will be returned to the Terminal.

 

* You cannot befriend Pokémon during the Battle Royale. 
* During Co-op Play, Keys are shared by all players.

 

・ Switching Pokémon

In the Battle Royale, you can’t switch Pokémon until the HP of the Pokémon you’re controlling reaches 0.

When your Pokémon’s HP reaches 0, you will lose a Key, and the Switch Menu will be displayed. Select a Pokémon from your collection to continue your battle.

 

* Unlike in Stages, time will pause while switching Pokémon.
* Attention: If there are no Pokémon available to switch and your Pokémon’s HP reaches 0, you lose the game regardless of how many Keys you have left.

 

・ Rivals

In the middle of the Battle Royale, Powerful Rivals will appear.

 

* The number of Rivals that appear differs depending on the Rank.

 

Battle Royale Clear

When the Battle Royale has been cleared, the Prize will appear in the center of the arena. Pick up this Prize to advance to the Clear Screen.

In the Clear Screen, you can confirm how many Pokémon each player defeated. Press to return to the Terminal.

 

 

 

・ Reaching a New Rank

Once you’ve cleared the Battle Royale, a Launch Pad to the next Rank will appear in the upper-right corner of the Terminal.

Use this Launch Pad to jump to the Terminal for the next Rank, and aim to befriend stronger Pokémon and compete in that Rank’s Battle Royale.

 

* If you would like to return to the previous Rank, use the Launch Pad in the upper-left corner of the Terminal.
