Basic Rules
Lives Rules

Under Lives Rules, you lose one life every time the Energy Ball touches the bottom of the screen. The game ends when you lose all lives.

However, you can increase your lives by reaching certain scores and acquiring certain items.

In 2 player mode, each player loses a life when the Ball touches the bottom of the screen. Points for busting blocks and enemies are awarded to the last player who bounced the Energy Ball.

Barrier Rules

Under Barrier Rules, you start with a protective barrier that weakens every time the Energy Ball hits it until it disappears. The game ends when the Energy Ball touches the bottom of the screen.
The barrier returns to full strength at the beginning of each Round.

The rules are the same for 2 player mode.
