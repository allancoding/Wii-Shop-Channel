Loop Packs

Mix Superstar initially comes with three exciting loop packs - Dance, Hip Hop and Techno. Additional packs can be downloaded from the Loop Store.

To cycle through your current loop packs, move your pointer to pack listings in the top left corner of the browser, then press up or down on the +Control Pad or select by pressing the A button and dragging in the same directions.
