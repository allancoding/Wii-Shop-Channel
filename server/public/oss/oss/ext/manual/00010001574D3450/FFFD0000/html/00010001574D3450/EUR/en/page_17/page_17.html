Favourites

As you are creating your mix, you may find there are certain loops that you really like. Instead of having to search through the 'Loop Browser' each time, you can favourite individual loops.

 

To favourite a loop, place the loop you want to favorite in the Arranger. Then press the B button on the desired loop and select the star icon on the bottom left corner of the Volume/Pan controls.

 

To view your favourite loops go to the 'Loop Browser' and scroll through the loop packs on the top left until you get to 'My Favourites'. All of your favourite loops will be in this section. To use the favourite, select it by pressing the A button and dragging it into the Arranger just like you would a regular loop.

 

To preview or delete a favourite, press the B button on the loop and select from the options on the drop down menu.
