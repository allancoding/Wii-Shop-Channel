Loop Browser

The Loop Browser allows you to preview and select loops from the various loop packs available to you. To access the Loop Browser, move your cursor to the bottom center of the screen and the browser will automatically pop up.


To preview a loop from the list, hover over it and press the B button. The loop will automatically begin playing. If you don’t like the loop you’re listening to, just press the B button on a new loop. The previous one will stop playing and the new one will start.

When you've heard a loop you want to add to your mix, press the A button to press and drag it from the 'Loop Browser' above to the arranger. The browser window will automatically close.
