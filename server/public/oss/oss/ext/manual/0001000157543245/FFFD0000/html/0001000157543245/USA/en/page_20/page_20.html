Shot Type

Use to change your Shot Type.


Flop (default): A "flop" shot sticks to the box when it lands. This is great for getting on the box but makes it tougher to get in the hole.





Slide: A "slide" shot moves up the box after it lands. Use this for three-point hole shots but be careful not to slide off!
