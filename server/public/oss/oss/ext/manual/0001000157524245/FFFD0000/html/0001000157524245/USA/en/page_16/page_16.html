Hero Types

Heroes are the most powerful robots on the battlefield, with each having advanced weapons and special abilities. Pick one of these moving-towers and prepare for the enemy invasion!
Joe Commando

Joe is a powerful, elite fighter who's all red, white, and green. Equipped with Dual Chain Guns, when he really wants to turn up the juice, Joe goes into Rampage Mode.

Sergeant Payne

The Sarge is a Leader Hero, which means he can form a Squad of basic soldiers. His second Ability is his War Shout, which temporarily increases the damage inflicted by his squad.
Spyder

Spyder is equipped with a Converter Cannon, which turns robots into refrigerators for a short time. He can also place a Fake HQ on the terrain, which distracts the enemy waves temporarily.
Pyro

Pyro's main weapon is a flamethrower - the Melter. When he performs his Siege Mode Ability, his flamethrower gets a power and range boost, and the marshmallows all get well-done.
Warlord General

The General is a Leader Hero (forming Squads) – but with a Chainsaw! His secondary Ability is the Demoralizing Shout, which temporarily decreases the enemy's ability to attack... and can often send them to therapy.
Viper the Sniper

Viper lives up to his name, picking off enemies with his long-range Sniper Rifle. His favorite toy is the WMD. Just when your enemy thinks they're safe, an anvil drops from the sky!
