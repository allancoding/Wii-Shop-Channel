The Basics

Enemy robot soldiers come in waves, heading straight for your HQ building to destroy it. The goal of the game is to protect your HQ using your defenses. If they destroy it, your game is over.


The HQ normally appears as a building, however in later levels it may appear in different forms. Check the Level Title Screen image to see your HQ's positioning and where enemies will come from.
The Enemy

Enemy Units can enter from more than 1 location on a map, and will attack in various ways. Some enemies attack Towers, Basic Units, even Heroes, as they advance towards your HQ. Each successive wave of enemies is tougher, so choose and position your defenses wisely!
Defenses

You have 2 main types of defenses – Towers and a Hero robot.


Use the Towers to funnel enemy soldiers across the battlefield. Use your Hero robot to reinforce your defenses, or to cover areas without Towers.


Basic troops will provide you with support, but they are not under your control. These builders, medics, basic and heavy soldiers will protect your HQ, Towers, and Hero until they perish! (And are rebuilt!)
The Beaver

The Beaver pops up out of the ground in most levels to taunt you. The more you see the Beaver, the worse you are probably doing. Stupid beaver!
