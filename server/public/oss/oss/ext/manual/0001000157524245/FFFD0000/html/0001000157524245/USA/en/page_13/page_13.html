Tower Types

What would a tower defense game be without towers? Choose from 4 types, each with varying strengths and costs.

 

In Story Mode, Towers are unlocked as you progress. All Towers are available in Survival Mode.
Basic Cannon

The basic Thermidoom model shoots robot-piercing bullets and can be upgraded into either an Auto Cannon (fires faster), or a Sniper Cannon (increased range, slower to fire).
EMP Cannon

The EMP shoots a blast of energy that can melt even the toughest Beaver-bot. It can be upgraded into a stronger blast, or into a DEAR device, which temporarily turns targets into appliances - like toasters!
Missile Launcher

The basic launcher shoots a powerful, self-guided missile. The SMART upgrade can track more than one enemy, while the SWARMs shoot multiple missiles!
Laser Beam

The most powerful (and expensive) weapon is the Laser tower. Lasers work great on robot-metal, allowing targets to be chain-linked together. Laser blasts lose their damage the further its target gets.
