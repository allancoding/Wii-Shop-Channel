Controls
  • Use the Wii Remote to direct the cursor. Use the cursor to select and place things in-game.
  • Press the A Button to select / place Towers.
  • Press the A Button to select / direct your Hero.
  • Press the B Button to deselect a menu, or go back.
  • Use the +Control Pad, or the Nunchuk, to scroll on large maps.
  • Press the + Button to display the Pause Menu.
  • Press the – Button to toggle the Tower Menu on / off.
  • Press the 1 Button to center the screen on your HQ.
  • Press the 2 Button to center the screen on your Hero.
