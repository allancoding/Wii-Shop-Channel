Ranking System

Three kinds of rankings are available.

*Select the information you want to view by moving the cursor over any the RANKINGS and tilting left or right. Viewable info includes: TOP 10, NEAR YOU, and FRIEND.

  • VS Ranking
    •
    Rank is decided according to number of points gained in Nintendo WFC Mode VS Battle mode. Player initially starts out with 5000 VS POINTS.

  • BATTLE ROYALE Ranking

    • Rank is decided according to number of points gained in Nintendo WFC Mode BATTLE ROYALE battles. Player initially starts out with 5000 BR POINTS.

  • SURVIVAL Ranking

    • Rankings reflect your performance in the Survival Challenge played during the STORY Mode. Player must play through the STORY Mode's Survival Challenge at least once before a rank is assigned.

    VS and BATTLE ROYALE rankings will automatically be deleted if neither mode has been played at least once in the last 30 days.

    While perusing the rankings, you can view battle records of those you have battled against over the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. Move the cursor over the player's name and press to view their rank.
