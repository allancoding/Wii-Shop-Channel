Nintendo WFC Mode

Description of Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Modes.

WITH ANYONE
This is a VS mode with player rankings.
Find a random opponent to battle against. Select to play either VS or BATTLE ROYALE. Also, rating points are separate for each mode. As your points increase or decrease, they will be reflected in the rankings.
WITH FRIEND
Battle against friends whose Friend Code is registered. Select to play either VS or BATTLE ROYALE. Results of battles WITH FRIENDs will have no effect on the player rankings.

*Only friends who have registered one another's names can battle against each other.
