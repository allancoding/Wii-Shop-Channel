What is PictureBook Games: Pop-Up Pursuit?
PictureBook Games: Pop-Up Pursuit is a family board game set in the world of a pop-up picture book. Instead of rolling dice to move along the map, you use cards and coins. The cards have various functions that help you reach your goal.
・
Strategically Move Ahead Using Coins
If you have coins, you can use certain cards to add to the number of spaces you can move along the map. Also, if you stop on a Card Bazaar space, you can use your coins to buy more cards.
・
Something Happens on the Book of Magick Space
You might land on a space called the Book of Magick. If this happens, the book will open up and mysterious events will take place, depending on what page remains open...
・
A Sudden Switch at the End
Players are given a Rank Bonus according to their order at the time the map is cleared. In addition, a Coin Bonus may be given that changes the number of coins players have into points, and the combination of Rank Bonus and Coin Bonus determines the final rankings.
Even if you do not reach the end of the map in first place, if you have saved up plenty of coins, you may be able to come up from behind and win, depending on the Coin Bonus results.
