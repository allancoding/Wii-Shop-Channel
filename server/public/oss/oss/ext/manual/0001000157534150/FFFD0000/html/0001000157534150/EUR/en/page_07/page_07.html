Overview
Onslaught is a first-person shooting game that pits you against overwhelming hordes of alien creatures in a thrilling, fast-paced battle.
The game is set in the near future on a distant planet where packs of hostile alien creatures attack in overwhelming numbers.
Story Mode follows you though a number of missions as you battle the alien onslaught using a wide range of weapons. Onslaught also allows you to compete against up to 4 players worldwide using Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.
