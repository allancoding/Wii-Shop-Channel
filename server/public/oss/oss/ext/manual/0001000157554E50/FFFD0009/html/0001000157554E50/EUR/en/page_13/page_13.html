Custom Rules


The Custom Rules menu is called up in Free Play and Multiplayer mode. Select the arrow icon to scroll through all the rules, so you can fully customise UNO™ matches.

Players
Choose 4 or 6 players to play the game.

Difficulty
Choose Easy, Normal or Hard difficulty.

Score
You can select the score that you must reach from a selection of 250, 500 or 1000.

Challenge
When toggled “On,” players will get points for the cards remaining in their hands after one player has gotten rid of his or her cards. Whenever a player reaches a designated score, the Exit Score, that player must leave the game. When toggled “Off,” whoever gets rid of his or her cards first receives points for the remaining cards left in opponents’ hands. The winner is the player who has reached the Target Score first.

Penalties
When the previous player plays a Draw Two card or Wild Draw Four card, you can put the same card down if you have it and you will not be penalized. However, the player after you will get double the penalty. If he or she has the same card, the effect is transferred to the next player.

Draw Type
There are three Draw Types: Type 1) Draw one card and play if the card is playable 2) Draw one card but don’t play 3) Draw cards until a card can be played.

Force Play
If you have a playable card, you must use it. Play then passes to the next player. You can toggle this rule on or off.

UNO™ 7-0
You can toggle this rule on or off: Every time that a 0 is played, all players must give their cards to the next player. Every time a 7 is played, that player must exchange cards with a player of their choice.

Jump-In
You can toggle this rule on or off: If you have exactly the same card that is on the top of the discard pile, you can play it immediately without waiting for your turn.

Brag
When toggled on, Wild Draw Four cards can be challenged by the next player. When toggled off they cannot be challenged.

Default
Returns game rules to default settings.

Start
Start a new game.
