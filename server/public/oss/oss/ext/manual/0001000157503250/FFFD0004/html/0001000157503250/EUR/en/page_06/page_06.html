Welcome!

Hey, you! Fortunately, you are still in time. The magic forest is in danger!
An evil villain is draining the life essence from our trees.
Please take control of our magic cannon and help us restore the balance in the Potpourrii.

The Potpourrii is the pool where the season spirits reside. For centuries they have been maintaining the natural progression of seasons and providing life essence to our trees.

By disturbing the order of the season spirits within the Potpourrii, this villain tries to stop the progression of seasons and essence supply for our trees, to ultimately destroy our forest.

"Where do I fit in this story?" I hear you think! Well... you will be our new magic cannon operator! Using our magic cannon you can restore the balance by ordering the season spirits and releasing their essences.

The essence possessed by a spirit can be released by hitting it with a type of spirit from its successive season.
For instance, a spring season spirit will be able to release a winter season spirit's essence.

It is our task to intercept the incoming spirits and load the cannon at your request. Watch out though, we can only hold one spirit of each season at a time! If another spirit is sent our way, we will have to throw it straight into the Potpourrii.
