Fenimore, Rhiannon, William

Fenimore Fillmore, the main character
Age: twenty something
Occupation: cowboy
This guy is a hopeless case. He is always in trouble, but he solves it with some work and some luck, or a lot of both.

 

Rhiannon Starek, the girl
Age: twenty and a bit
Occupation: vocational school teacher
She is not the typical girl who needs to be saved from the bad boys. She is a determined and liberated woman who decides who will help her when and how.

 

William Starek, the baddy
Age: About half a century
Occupation: cattle rancher
He is the law and order; he is the lord and master in all the land to the horizon and beyond. He is very rich, very powerful and very very bad.
