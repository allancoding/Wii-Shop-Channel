Extras I

FROM ONE PLACE TO ANOTHER


In order to change from one scenario to another, Fenimore has to ride his horse. Then, he goes to a map in which you have to choose the destination by pointing with the Wii Remote and pressing the A Button.


THE REVOLVER


The different fire weapons are of very easy use. You only have to point with the Wii Remote and shoot by pressing the B Button.


RECOMENDATIONS


It is highly recommendable to talk to all the characters in order to have as much information as you can to progress in the game, and back to talk to them over and over again just in case they know something new.

 

SHOPPING


A Far West Town worth its salt must have at least four buildings: the saloon, the jail, the bank and, above all, a general store. Here you can buy a lot of very useful items. So it is highly recommendable that, when you need something, you go to the town store in order to see if they have it.(On occasions it might be cheaper, but more complicated, to get it elsewhere though).
