Ki

In addition to normal attacks, Celia can use three different Ki techniques to defeat her enemies.

Illusion Tears
Holding down the 1 Button while Celia is standing still will trigger a powerful Ki slash that hits all nearby enemies. It uses a quarter of the Ki Bar. With the Classic Controller, Illusion Tears is done with the y Button.

World Ender
Pressing down on the
+Control Pad and pressing the
1 Button during a jump will cause Celia to charge energy and perform a dive attack that, upon impact, will launch all surrounding enemies into the air. It uses a quarter of the Ki Bar. With the Classic Controller, World Ender is done with the y Button.

Neokinesis
When Celia’s Ki bar is full you can press the
A Button to activate Neokinesis. Celia´s Ki will begin to drop rapidly, but all her attacks cause additional damage and affect all the enemies that they impact, even if they are grouped. With the Classic Controller, Neokinesis is activated with the a Button.
