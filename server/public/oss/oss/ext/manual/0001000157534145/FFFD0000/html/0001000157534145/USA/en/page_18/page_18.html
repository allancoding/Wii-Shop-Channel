Nintendo
Wi-Fi Connection Ranking
Results from Story Mode missions and Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking Battle are recorded on Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Rankings, allowing you to compete for high scores with players around the world.

There are 3 types of rankings: World, Region, and Friends. Rankings allow you to view not only worldwide player rankings, but also what position your own score is.

* You must create a save data file in order to register to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking.

Registering to
Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking
When you select "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking" from "Nintendo Wi-Fi
Connection", your results will automatically be recorded to the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Rankings.

*You may be unable to update the Nintendo Wi-Fi
Connection Rankings in some cases.

Viewing Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking
Select "Nintendo Wi-Fi
Connection Ranking" from "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection", then choose the parameters for the rankings you want to view. Finally, select "High Scores" or "Player Score" to display the rankings.

*You may be unable to update the Nintendo Wi-Fi
Connection Rankings in some cases.

*It may take time to display the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Rankings in some cases.

Ranking Information

● Hudson WiiWare Portal Website
Ranking/leaderboard information including your game registration name and territory for Onslaught, may be publicly displayed on the Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.

● Operation Policy
Operations may be altered depending on frequency of use. If any changes occur, details will be posted on Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.
