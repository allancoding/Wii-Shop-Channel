● What is Rock N’ Roll Climber?
Rock N’ Roll Climber is a game that simulates the increasingly popular sport of rock climbing (also called "bouldering").
● Game Features
Rock N' Roll Climber features realistic rock climbing with a tactile immediacy made possible by the Wii Remote™ and Nunchuk™ controllers. Even better, if you have a Wii Balance Board™ accessory (sold with Wii Fit) you can complete the immersive experience and climb with your whole body!
・
Enjoy the most realistic rock climbing possible in your own living room
When you release your grip and reach your right arm toward the next hold, so will your on-screen character. When you grab the next hold and pull yourself up, so will your on-screen character. It's the next best thing to actually climbing up the side of a boulder!
・
Get further into the game with a Wii Balance Board
If you use a Wii Balance Board, you'll also be able to control your character by raising or lowering your legs and maintaining balance. It'll make the game as much of a workout as the real thing!
What is a hold?
The handlelike protrusions and cracks in the climbing surface are called "holds." There are many holds available on each course, but selecting the best ones to climb as efficiently as possible is what separates the novice from the pro.
