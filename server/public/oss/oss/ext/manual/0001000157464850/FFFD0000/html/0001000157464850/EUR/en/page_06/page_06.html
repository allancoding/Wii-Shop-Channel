Welcome!

Keep the skies safe on your Wii console with the insanely addictive WiiWare download Flight Control™!

In this action-packed game, you will take on the role of an air traffic controller at an increasingly busy airport. In complete control of the airspace, you must direct aircraft to their appropriate landing zones while avoiding collisions. You’ll travel to airports set among green fields (Classic), a beachside resort (Beach), the Australian Outback (Outback), a winter wonderland (Windy) and even an aircraft carrier (Carrier). Learn how to land ten different kinds of aircraft, including jets, seaplanes and helicopters.

To direct aircraft, simply use your Wii Remote to select and drag them to their landing zones without letting them collide. Sounds easy? As planes begin to arrive thick and fast, you’ll need smart strategy, keen eyes and a smooth touch to keep the chaos under control! Fly solo or play collaboratively with friends in a party-style, drop-in, drop-out multiplayer mode for up to four people.

Flight Control only takes a minute to learn – just don’t expect to put it down as quickly as you’ll pick it up!

Featuring:

  • Innovative and insanely addictive 'one more go' gameplay, perfect for the Wii console – guide ten types of aircraft safely to the runway
  • Only takes a minute to learn – just use your Wii Remote to touch the aircraft and draw a path to its landing zone
  • 5 unique airfields, each with their own challenging twist
  • Drop-in, drop-out co-operative multiplayer mode – up to four players can share landing duties
  • Personalized Hi Score tables – make your mark in aviation history
  • Track your performance with detailed statistics for each airfield
  • Popular fast forward mode so that experts can progress through the game faster. Plus 'safe' fast forward mode, which turns off when collisions may be imminent
  • Save your game any time so you can resume exactly where you left off when you are ready
  • Supports both 4:3 & 16:9 display

So welcome aboard! Please sit back, fasten your seatbelt and enjoy the mayhem!
