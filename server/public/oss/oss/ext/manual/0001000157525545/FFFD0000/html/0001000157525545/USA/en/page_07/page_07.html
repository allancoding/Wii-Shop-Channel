Controls

BIT.TRIP RUNNER is played with the Wii Remote held in a horizontal position.

 

 

Use the +Control Pad and buttons on the Wii Remote to control CommanderVideo and navigate him through the environment, avoiding and thwarting obstacles as he goes. CommanderVideo’s suite of moves is detailed below.

 

Jump!
(2 Button)
Tap the 2 Button to make CommanderVideo jump. Jumping helps CommanderVideo to avoid obstacles and traverse the environment.

 

Glide!
(2 Button)

Press and hold the 2 Button to make CommanderVideo glide in the air longer during a jump. This can help him get over wider gaps and bigger obstacles.

 

Slide!
(+Control Pad Down)

Press and hold Down on the +Control Pad to make CommanderVideo slide along the ground. This helps CommanderVideo sneak under obstacles in his path.

 

Kick!

(1 Button)

Tap the 1 Button to make CommanderVideo kick obstacles that block his path entirely.

 

Spring!
(+Control Pad Up)

Press Up on the +Control Pad while on top of a purple springboard to make CommanderVideo spring into the air for a super jump.

 

Block!

(+Control Pad Right)

Press and hold Right on the +Control Pad to make CommanderVideo use his Paddle from BIT.TRIP BEAT to block incoming Beats.
