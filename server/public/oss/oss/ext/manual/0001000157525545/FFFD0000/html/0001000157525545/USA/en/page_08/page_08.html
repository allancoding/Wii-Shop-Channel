Get CommanderVideo to the end of each Challenge and defeat the boss at the end of each Zone by running, jumping, sliding, and kicking your way to victory!


 

Each Zone is made up of multiple Challenges and Bonus Challenges. Use all of CommanderVideo’s skills to make it to the finish line.

 

If you collect enough Gold Bars along the way, you’ll enter a difficult but rewarding Bonus Challenge.


 

Challenges and Zones for which you’ve collected all the Gold Bars will have a “!” next to them on the Main Menu.


 

Earn two “!” per Challenge! One for the main Challenge, and one for the Bonus Challenge!
