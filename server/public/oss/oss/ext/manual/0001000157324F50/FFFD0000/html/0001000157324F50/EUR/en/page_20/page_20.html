Receiving New Fish
You will receive a new fish a couple times a week. When you feed them frequently (several times a day), the chances that you will get a new fish may go up.
You will also receive fish in various other ways.
Receive Fish Over Time

You will receive a new fish a couple times a week.
* If you do not view your aquarium regularly, it may take longer to receive a new fish.

Receive Fish from Wii Friends' Aquariums

If there are any new fish in an aquarium you receive from a Wii Friend during an exchange, one random fish from it will be added to your Aqua Center.
* Once a fish is added from a received aquarium, it will take more time to receive a new fish.

Receive from "My Aquarium" Data

You will receive certain fish if you have "My Aquarium" software or its save data.

* These fish can be obtained through normal play even without data from "My Aquarium".

Purchase Add-On Content

Select "Purchase Add-On Content" from the Mode Selection Screen to add new content.

 

You can purchase certain fish and objects as Add-On Content.

Wii Points, purchased separately, are required to purchase Add-On Content.

 

Once at the Add-On Content shop, select the content you wish to buy.
