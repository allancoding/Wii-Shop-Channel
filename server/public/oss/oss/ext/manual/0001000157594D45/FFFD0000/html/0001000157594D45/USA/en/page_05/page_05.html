Introduction

 

 

Have you ever wondered what it would feel like to be around the stoves of the best restaurants in the world? Do you want to become the best chef there is, preparing delicious dishes? Jump right in and enter the world of Yummy Yummy and its mix of flavors and aromas.

 

In Yummy Yummy, you'll become a great cook in an entertaining game where you have to satisfy the hunger of the most diverse, colorful set of hungry diners: vampires, extraterrestrials, mafia dons, Vikings, and so on. Prepare hot dogs, hamburgers, pizzas, and Mexican burritos as quickly as you can before your customers get tired of waiting and decide to leave the restaurant without giving you a good tip.

 

Roll up your sleeves, put on your chef's hat and have a great time being the best of cooks.
