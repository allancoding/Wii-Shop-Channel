Hamburger Restaurant

 

 

Prepare the most delicious hamburgers with grilled meat, ketchup, lettuce, and cheese slices. Round out the menu by frying crispy fries.


To make a wonderful hamburger, first place the bun on the central plate. Put the meat on the grill and then flip it over with the spatula so it doesn't burn. When the meat is done, put it on the hamburger bun and add other ingredients such as ketchup, lettuce, and the cheese slices.

 

To accompany this, you can fry some tasty potatoes, putting them into the deep fryer located on your left. But be careful! Don't let them burn.
