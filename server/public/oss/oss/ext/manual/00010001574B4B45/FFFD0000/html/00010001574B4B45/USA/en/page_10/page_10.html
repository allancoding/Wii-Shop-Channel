The Captain's Challenge

You may suddenly find yourself facing the Captain's Challenge while playing in Pop-Up Party mode. This unavoidable event occurs between players' turns.
As each player thrusts a sword into the barrel, he or she will be challenged to a mini-game against the Captain. Succeed and it's the next player's turn, but if you fail, you'll have only 3 seconds to thrust your sword.
The Captain's Challenge mini-game goes like this:

1. "Look"
The screen zooms in on the Captain's face, and a Wii Remote icon appears in the center. The word "Look" is displayed on screen.

↓

2. "that"
Next the word "that" is displayed. Watch your timing and move the Wii Remote icon up, down, left, or right just before the Captain's face moves.

↓

3. "way!"
Finally, the word "way!" appears on the screen as the Captain's face moves. If you move the remote in the direction the Captain is facing, you win... but if you've picked the wrong direction, you lose. After displaying the result, the game automatically proceeds to the next player's turn.
Once each player has had a turn, the mini-game will end.
