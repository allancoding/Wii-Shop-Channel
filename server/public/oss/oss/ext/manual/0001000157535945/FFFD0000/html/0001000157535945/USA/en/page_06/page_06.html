Playing Modes

Adventure
This standard playing mode takes Turbo through 50 delivery routes across 10 galaxies.

Co-Op Adventure
Play Adventure Mode with a friend. When one friend dies, the other can complete the delivery route.

Time Trial
Improve your skills and test your speed in Time Trial. Select any delivery route that you have played and try to beat your best completion time.

Challenge Mode
Challenge Mode is packed with an infinite number of randomly-generated levels, and enables you to select a difficulty and racing speed. Raising the level of difficulty lengthens all delivery routes and increases the number of enemies and obstacles.

Multiplayer
Play with up to 3 friends in Multiplayer Mode. Like Challenge Mode, Multiplayer enables you to select a difficulty and racing speed. It also allows you to choose how the winner will be selected -- Death Match, race, Score, or Time Trial -- and up to 5 tracks.
