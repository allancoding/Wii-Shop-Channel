Introduction
What is "The Magic Obelisk"?

This is an action game in which you become Popo, a light spirit. It will be your mission to guide Lukus, a tree spirit, to each stage's goal while moving only within the shadow. You can help him do this by shining your light near the mysterious Magic Obelisk, allowing you to create and connect shadows.


  • Popo, light spirit
    •  Popo can move around stages freely, without worrying about light and shadow. Popo also has the ability to call Lukus. Once called, Lukus will attempt to move to where Popo called him.
    (See Page 9: Basic Rules, Part One)

  • Lukus, tree spirit
    •  Lukus can only move around while in the shadow. If he goes into the sunlight, it will end your current attempt, and you'll lose one Spirit Meter. Lukus will then go back to the beginning of the stage and you'll have to start over. When you run out of Spirit Meter, the game is over.
    (See Page 10: Basic Rules, Part Two)

  • Magic Obelisks
    •  Throughout the stages, you will find structures decorated with mysterious crests. These structures are known as "Magic Obelisks." When Popo shines his light near one of these obelisks, he can create shadows, and connect two or more shadows together. Lukus can use these connected shadows to move through the level.
    (See Page 11: Magic Obelisks)
