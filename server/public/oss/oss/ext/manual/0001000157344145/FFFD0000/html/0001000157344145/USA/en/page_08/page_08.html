Controls
 

FRONTEND

+Control Pad
Cycle through options

A Button
Accept

B Button
Back / Cancel

 

 

SNOOKER (ALL TYPES)

Wii Remote
Move to position ball
Select a ball to nominate

When holding the B Button push forward to strike the white ball (advanced only)

+Control Pad
Move the camera left and right

A Button
Select position to place cue ball
Select position on ball to place cue
Accept to nominate ball

B Button
Hold and pull back to increase power
When holding the B Button release to strike the white ball (basic only)

1 Button
Switch between Overhead and POV camera

2 Button
Open the in-game menu (to change cue elevation and ball nominations)

+ Button
Zoom camera in

-  Button
Zoom camera out

Select position on ball to place cue

AIR HOCKEY

Wii Remote
Move mallet
Move puck (if you are in possession)

BOWLING

Wii Remote
Pull back and swing forward to launch ball (after position and angle states have been completed)

+Control Pad
Move ball left and right

A Button
Accept and move onto next stage

B Button
Cancel and go back to one stage

2 Button
Open the in-game menu
