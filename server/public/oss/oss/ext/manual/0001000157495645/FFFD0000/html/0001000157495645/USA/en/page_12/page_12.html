Attack Mode and Transport Mode
When is pressed, your Invaders will return to your UFO, initiating “Transport Mode” in which your UFO can travel at high speeds. You will remain in Transport Mode for as long as you hold down .  You will switch back to normal Attack Mode when is released.

 

In Transport Mode, you can more easily avoid enemy attacks and can get to your destination faster, but it’s important to remember that you will not be able to attack, and are not invulnerable to enemy attacks.
