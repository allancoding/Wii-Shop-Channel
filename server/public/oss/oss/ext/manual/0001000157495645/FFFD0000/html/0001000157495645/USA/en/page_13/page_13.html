Enemies
An introduction to some of the Earthlings’ Weapons
Tanks

These move at and fire from ground level.
Small tanks use only 1 type of ammunition.
Large tanks use 2 types of ammunition.
Fighter Planes

These move and attack quickly.

Be careful as you can sustain damage by colliding with them.

 
Helicopters

These will actively follow your UFO.

Although they don’t inflict much damage, you should shoot them down as soon as possible.

 
Battleships

These formidable enemies launch missiles with deadly accuracy.

Watch out when they attack as a fleet.

 
Submarines

These fire missiles from underwater.

You cannot attack them when they are hiding beneath the waves.

 
Fortified Buildings

These form an important part of Earth’s defense system, and are designed to protect the Earthlings’ cities.
They utilize a variety of different attacks.

 
Gas Tanks

These explode when attacked.
The fragments flung from these explosions can inflict serious damage.

 
Capture Fields

Special barriers that the Earthlings have created to capture UFOs.

Destroy the power-generating towers to escape.
