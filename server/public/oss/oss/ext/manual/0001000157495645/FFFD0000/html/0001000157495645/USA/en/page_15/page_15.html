Techniques

“SPACE INVADERS GET EVEN” has many techniques that

you can use to attain high scores.

This is an introduction to some of those techniques.
Cows

Position your UFO so that its shadow is directly on top of a roaming cow to automatically abduct it.  Doing so will recover some Time.

Cows in pastures do not move and are easy to capture, but if you destroy the pasture, they will escape, so be careful.

 
Technical Bonus

If you use specific attacks for certain enemies, you will be rewarded with Technical Bonus time.
You will need to maintain your Technical Bonus time to earn high scores.

 
Stonehenge

If you are running out of Time, head for the Stonehenges located in different areas of the map.
Hover right over the Stone Circle to recover some Time.

Doing this will cause your Technical Bonus to decrease, however, making it difficult to earn high scores, so be careful.
