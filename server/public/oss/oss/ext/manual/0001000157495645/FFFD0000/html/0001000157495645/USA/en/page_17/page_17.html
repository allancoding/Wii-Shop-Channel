About Add-on Content

By purchasing add-on content, you can play extra missions.
There are three different Add-on Mission Packs that can be
purchased from the shopping screen.

You do not need to acquire Mission Packs in order, so feel free to buy any Mission Pack you like.
You will need 500 Wii Points to buy each item of add-on content.

Visit the Wii Shop Channel or the Nintendo website for instructions regarding the purchase of Wii Points.

 

Warning!
The add-on content described in this section is data used by the game SPACE INVADERS GET EVEN.
You cannot play using the add-on content alone.
Mission Pack A

Available: 12/1/08

500 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 117

 

These 2 missions offer 6 areas featuring powerful battleships stationed at the "Port City," as well as craftly Earthlings lurking in the undergrowth around "Woodland City."
Mission Pack B

Available: 12/1/08

500 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 123

 

These 2 missions offer 6 areas featuring gas tanks and searchlights in the "City of Industry," and the manic attacks of Earthlings in the "Desert City."
Mission Pack C

Available: 12/1/08

500 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 128

 

These 2 missions offer 6 areas featuring wild blizzards in "Arctic City," as well as blazing attacks on the "core" in the area of "Volcanic City."
