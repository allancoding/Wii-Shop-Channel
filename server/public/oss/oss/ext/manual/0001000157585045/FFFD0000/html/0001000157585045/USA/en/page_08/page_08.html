Saving Paintings

In both Paint Mode and Splash Mode it is possible to save your paintings to your SD Card.

Press the 1 Button while you are painting to bring up the save prompt, then press the B Button to confirm that you would like to save (or press any other button to cancel). The painting will automatically be given an unused file name, so nothing will be overwritten when saving a painting to the SD Card. The painting will be saved as a regular JPEG image, so you can insert the SD Card into your computer afterwards and email the painting to your family or use it as desktop background.

Pictures can only be saved to the SD card, and can be accessed using a PC in the following directory: /private/wii/app/WXPE
