Introduction

 

Have you ever wondered what it would feel like to be among the stoves of the best restaurants in the world? Do you want to become the best chef there is, preparing scrumptious dishes? Jump right in and enter the world of Yummy Yummy and its mixture of flavours and aromas.

 

In Yummy Yummy, you'll become the best of cooks in an entertaining game where you have to satisfy the hunger of the most diverse, colourful set of hungry diners: vampires, extraterrestrials, mafia dons, Vikings, and so forth. Prepare hot dogs, hamburgers, pizzas, and Mexican burritos as quickly as you can before your customers get tired of waiting and decide to leave the restaurant without giving you a good tip.

 

Roll up your sleeves, clap your chef's hat on and have a great time being the best of cooks.
