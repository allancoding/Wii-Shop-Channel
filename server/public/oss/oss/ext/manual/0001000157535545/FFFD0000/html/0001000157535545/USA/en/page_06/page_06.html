Rules & Screens

Move your character and the crosshairs around the screen, and shoot down the targets while dodging enemy attacks.

 

Getting hit by an enemy projectile or touching an enemy close to you will count as a miss. With each miss, one heart will disappear from the bottom of the screen, and incurring a miss with no hearts left will end the game.
After aiming the crosshairs at the desired target, press the B Button to fire a shot.

Move your character by moving the Control Stick to the left and right.
Press the A Button to fire your subweapon.

You can no longer use your subweapon when the Subweapon Icon at the bottom of the screen disappears.

While holding the Control Stick to the left or the right, press the C Button to perform a Sudden Dodge. Alternatively, a Sudden Dodge can also be performed if the Nunchuk is swung to the left or the right.

You will become invincible while dodging.

Controls
Control Stick Move Character
Pointer Move Crosshairs
B Button Shoot
A Button Subweapon
C Button Sudden Dodge
Z Button Telescope
+ Button Pause & Controls
