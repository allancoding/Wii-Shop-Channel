Amusement Park Environments

Each amusement park has four games with the similar goal of shooting the targets as quickly and accurately as possible.





ARCADIA
  • Knock 'Em Over – Knock over the sets of furry cats but don't hit the fur or those cats won't fly.

  • Cluster Buster – Group colored balloons together before shooting for the highest score.

  • Shooting Gallery – Shoot the targets on the fish, ducks, foxes, squirrels, rabbits, skunks, bears, birds and owls.

  • Shoot the Star – Shoot the star as it moves along the track. Zero points for shredding the paper.

DREAMLAND
  • Saloon Shoot – Knock over the bottles on the conveyor belts as they appear from left and right doors.

  • Haunted Hunt – Shoot spiders, pumpkins, shadows, ghosts, werewolves, witches, skulls and bats for a spooky high score.

  • Stop the Robots – Shoot the blue, green and red waves of colored robots before they land on the pads in front of you and steal your time.

  • Balloon Wheel – Shoot the colored balloons in the order shown on the signs to the right and left of the wheel.

LAGOON PARK
  • Mouse Hunt – Shoot the targets on the mice for highest score. Hit a mouse for lower points.

  • Bullseye Canyon – Hit the targets - not the transportation - by land, air and sea.

  • Animal Match – Shoot the animals in correct pairs for the highest score.

  • Quick Draw – Shoot the red bullseye in the center of the targets before they disappear for the highest score.
