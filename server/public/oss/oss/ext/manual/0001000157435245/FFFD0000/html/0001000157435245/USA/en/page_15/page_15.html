Round Recaps
At the end of Round 1 the Round Recap displays the score and the Game Timer. The Game Timer starts at 15 seconds and the Bonus Time earned during Round 1 is added to the time available for Round 2. After Round 2 the total score for both rounds is tallied along with the accuracy percentage (the percentage does not figure in the total score).


The Games Tally Board lists the score for each game and the total points for all four games. The Bonus Round Meter tracks how close a player is getting to 15,000 points, which unlocks the Bonus Game. After completing the final round of the last game in a park or the Bonus Game, the Final Score appears along with a prize and a title, such as a crown for Carnival King.
