Scoring

Round 1
Starts with 20 seconds and counts down to zero. Shooting Bonus Clocks increases the time allowed for that round only. Each target has a preset value, which displays before the beginning of each game. Earn Bonus Time for Round 2 based on how well you score.


Round 2
Starts with 15 seconds plus any Bonus Time earned during Round 1. The targets move faster and Bonus Targets randomly appear allowing players to pump up their scores.


Perfect Bonus
Each game has a Perfect Bonus, such as shooting 3-out-of 3 bottles in Saloon Shoot. In matching games, such as Balloon Wheel, the colors have to be matched in order to get the Perfect Bonus. In other games, such as Bullseye Canyon, players must hit the bullseye with the first shot for the Perfect Bonus. When a player hits the Perfect Bonus, the game displays a Perfect Bonus graphic.


Bonus Round
Each amusement park has a different Bonus Round: Bonus Wheel at Arcadia, Clown Drown at Dreamland and Dunk Tank at Lagoon Park. The player must score at least 15,000 total points for all four games in one park to reach the Bonus Round.
