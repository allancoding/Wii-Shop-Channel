Story

The Meerkat pack used to live a happy and peaceful life in the lush, green savanna but everything changed one year during the rainy season. An invading pack of meerkats arrived from the desert in the middle of a thunderstorm. The meerkat pack fled in terror from their territory as the dark colored rivals attacked their burrows.

One young meerkat female got separated from the rest of the fleeing pack. She stumbled to a river that flows through the savanna at this time of the year. The stream was strong as water rushed down from the near mountain tops and filled the dried riverbed with force. The unlucky meerkat got dragged under the water and quickly lost consciousness.

The next morning the weather was nice and the meerkat woke up on the riverbank. She had drifted to the other side of the river where the living conditions are harder and the desert tries to overtake the savanna. To survive the meerkat must find a partner and start a new meerkat pack. Perhaps some day she can cross the dried riverbed with her new family and take back the land that belonged to her ancestors.
