Story

 

There exists a rainbow-colored comet known as Holly's Comet that can grant anyone a single wish. Once in a blue moon, the comet can be found visiting a tower located on Rainbow Island.


While the comet resides there, humans are able to borrow her powers and use Rainbow Magic to help them scale the colossal tower.

 

One day, two brothers living on Rainbow Island, Bubby and Bobby, overhear an interesting conversation about the comet.

 

"If you can find Holly's Comet before she flies off and takes away your magic, she'll grant you any wish you want!"

 

"Wow!" Bubby says. "We've got to look for this Holly's Comet!"
"Yeah!" Bobby replies. "Think of all the great stuff we can ask for!"

 

Alas, Holly's Comet happens to possess a bit of a mean streak, and decides to make life difficult for the intrepid brothers by creating monsters out of the shards of the stars and having them attack them.

 

You see, Holly's Comet has become completely bored from spending so much time cooped up in the tower that she has decided to have some fun with the boys. Not very nice of her, is it?

 

Ah, but more trouble awaits the adventurous duo, for another man has heard the rumors and come to seek the comet as well. He is Dr. Crescent, a scientist who is out to prove that science is superior to magic and intends to test the Rainbow Magic that Bubby and Bobby have acquired against his diabolical inventions!

 

Will Bubby and Bobby successfully scale Rainbow Tower and have Holly's Comet grant their wishes?
