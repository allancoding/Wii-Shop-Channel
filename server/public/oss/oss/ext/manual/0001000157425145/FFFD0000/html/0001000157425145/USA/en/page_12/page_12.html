Techniques
Technique is essential to racking up as many points as possible within the time limit when going for a high score. There are several ways to improve your score in Star Soldier R, and a few of them are explained below.
Material Units

2,000 points will be added to your score each time you obtain a Material Unit. Try not to leave any behind.

Combos

Continuously destroying enemy ships and surface objects will increase your combo count, which is eventually factored into your score. Once the combo meter runs out, your combo count will return to zero, so be careful.

Delilah

Mysterious surface objects which appear in the latter part of certain levels.
Destroy them simultaneously for 80,000 bonus points.
