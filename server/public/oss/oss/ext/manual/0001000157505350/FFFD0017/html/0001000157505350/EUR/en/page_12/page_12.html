Things You Can Do in the Terminal

In addition to using the Launch Pads to travel to Stages and to take part in the Battle Royale, there are also several facilities in the Terminal that you can use.

 

Traveling to Stages

There are six Stages in each Rank. Use the Launch Pads in the Terminal to reach each Stage. When you’ve cleared a Stage, information will appear near its Launch Pad—the number of times the Stage has been cleared, Pokémon encountered, and Pokémon befriended. Befriended Pokémon will appear in colour. Pokémon that you have not yet encountered will appear as .

 

Challenging the Battle Royale

In order to attempt the Battle Royale, you must first befriend a Pokémon that has a Power (see p. 11: Selected Pokémon Details) over the required level. Once you have met this requirement, the gate will open, and you can reach the Battle Royale from the Launch Pad on the other side.

To learn the Power level required for your Pokémon to reach the Battle Royale, approach the gate.
