Magazine Stand

The Magazine Stand can be accessed from the Main Menu. Follow the
on-screen prompts to view and download these additional puzzles available for Word Searcher Deluxe and take your searching to a new level.

Word Packs are separated by issue and volume. Each Volume has 10 issues and each issue has 10 puzzles.

Wii Points are required to download any available Word Packs. (This involves a fee). Free space is required in Wii system memory to download Word Packs. If there is not enough free space, either delete unnecessary data saved in Wii system memory or move it to an SD Card.

All downloaded Word Packs can only be used with Word Searcher Deluxe.
