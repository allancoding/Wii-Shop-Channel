Options
Here you can adjust the options of the game.

Audio

From here, you can adjust:

VOICE
The volume of Jerry’s voice.

SOUND FX
The volume of the game’s sound effects, such as coin drops, crowd cheers, etc.

MUSIC
The volume of the music.

Select the DEFAULT icon to reset volumes to their default settings.


About

See the game’s credits.


Other Options

You can also choose if you want to see the tutorials, spin the wheel between rounds and use downloaded questions.

For beginners, we suggest keeping the tutorials so that you don’t feel lost when playing a new mini-game or a new quiz mode.

You can decide to not use the wheel in the game. This will significantly reduce the luck factor in determining a winner.

If you downloaded questions, you can temporarily disable the use of these questions by unchecking the option.
