Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Mode
When selecting "Nintendo WFC Mode" from the Main Menu, you will be connected to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. There you can battle with players from afar, and see how you rank in against others.

Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Match and Ranking can only be performed when the Wii console is connected to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.

About Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Match
Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Match is divided into 2 different match types.

● Friend Match
Battle players you've exchanged friend codes with.

 *You must go into "Nintendo WFC Mode" and "Friends" to register a friend code.

Friend Registration Note

Once a friend has been registered to your Friend Roster, your own information (such as ranking information and online status) will be sent to him or her until both of you have deleted one another's friend codes.

● Free Match
Battle with players who aren't registered as friends.

About Ranking
You can go into "Ranking" from "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Match" to view your achievements in ranking form. Results from Friend Match and Free Match will be registered in Ranking Mode.

*Continue to play in matches, and earn titles for your accomplishments.

● World Ranking
View the top 30 players in the world, along with your own ranking.

● National Ranking
View the top 30 players in your nation, along with your own ranking.

Ranking Information

● Hudson WiiWare Portal Website
Ranking/leaderboard information including your game registration name and territory for Water Warfare, may be publicly displayed on the Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.

● Operation Policy
Operations may be altered depending on frequency of use. If any changes occur, details will be posted on the Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.
