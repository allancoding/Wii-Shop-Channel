Weapons
Weapons are placed in certain positions throughout each map. Aim your sight at one and press the fire button to obtain it.
Each weapon has its own unique capabilities, so choose wisely to best deal with the situation at hand.

Machine Gun
This weapon boasts the fastest repeat fire speed, which makes up for its lack of power.
Water Submachine Gun
This weapon boasts an impressive repeat fire speed, and is easy to handle. One can be held in each hand for dual action!
Water Shotgun
Discharges multiple water blasts at once, and is top of the line in close-range combat. The downside being that, since its bursts are somewhat scattered, it doesn't serve well as a long-range weapon.
Water Bazooka
Launches powerful bursts of water that also cover the surrounding area.
Its power makes up for a slow repeat fire speed.
You could end up drenching yourself when attacking from too close with this weapon, so be careful.
Water Launcher
Launches powerful bursts of water that also cover the surrounding area. Its power and fast repeat fire speed make up for a short blast range.
You could end up drenching yourself when attacking from too close with this weapon, so be careful.
Water Rifle
This weapon is the most powerful, and boasts the farthest range of attack. Use its zoom function to close in on targets from afar. The downside is its slow repeat fire and poor close range performance.
 

Dual Action

When using Water Pistols or Water Submachine Guns, one can be held in each hand for dual action.
To do this, simply pick up the same type of weapon you are already holding.
