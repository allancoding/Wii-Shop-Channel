Screens
 Item Status
  Shows the item currently in your possession. Since item effects are only temporary, an icon is displayed showing how much time is left before an effect wears off.

 Status Abnormalities
  An icon is displayed to show when you are using an item, or to indicate effects brought about by status abnormalities.

 Sight
  Aim this sight when shooting your water gun at a target, picking up weapons, etc. Simply pointing the sight at items on the ground or other players will reveal names and other information.

 Message Window
  Messages needed to progress through the game are shown here.

 Weapon Status
  Shows which weapon you are using, and how much water is left in it. A Weapon Icon is only displayed when "Weapon Display" is turned off.

 Game Info
  Your score and remaining time are shown here.
Displayed content will vary depending on the rules.

 Character Status
  Shows how wet your shirt is, how many shirts are left, and the name of your team during a Team Match.

 Radar
  The white mark shows your position, and targets are shown too for convenience.

 Team Display
  An icon showing which team a player represents will be displayed above his or her head during a Team Match. Remember which color represents your own team.

Shirts

When getting attacked your shirt will get wet, and you'll see the color gradually start to change. If your entire shirt turns blue, you are KO'ed, and your total number of shirts decreases by one. Once you run out of shirts, the game will end, or you'll have to watch the rest of the match from the sidelines.

Match Info Display

Press to switch between Match Info and Game Info displays.
