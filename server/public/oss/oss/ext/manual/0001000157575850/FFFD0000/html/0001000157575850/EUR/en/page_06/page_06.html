Getting Started

Basic rules

You control a tanks cannon while trying to stop the army of the enemy. The object of the game is to defeat the army of the enemy, and to keep the number of soldiers that gets through your line of defense as little as possible. You have a variety of 'bonuses' at your disposal that will help you with the task.

Victory/Defeat

There are two game modes: 'Survival' and 'Campaign'. Each of the modes is divided into three levels of difficulty. In 'Survival mode', the goal is to repeal enemy's army - The longer you succeed in doing it, the higher the score you get! The 'Campaign' mode consists of three different subcampagins: 'Classic Campaign', 'Winter Assault', 'Cyber Wars'. Each of them has 28 different missions. Missions have different goals : killing a fixed number of enemy soldiers, eleminating specyfic type of the enemys, holding the line of defence for some time or to kill hostile units in the right way. Player loses if too many enemy soldiers gets through the line of defence.

Multiplayer

The 'Survival' mode allows to play up to four players at the same time. The more players the stronger the enemies are. All 'bonuses' work on all players at the same time, so like on a real battlefield you will need good cooperation.
