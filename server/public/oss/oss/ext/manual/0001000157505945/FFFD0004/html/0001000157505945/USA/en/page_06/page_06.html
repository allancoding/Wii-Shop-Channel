Basic Controls

Adjust the trajectory pressing the A button on the Wii Remote and release it to shoot your Pallurikio.

You may use any of two types of throws to start off Pallurikio: the hurl and the touch throw. The hurl could be used at any time Pallurikio is resting on the ground. When Pallurikio is already in the air, you can instead make use of a touch throw to put Pallurikio on course. Take care, however, you may only use the touch throw once, after which you would have to touch an object or the ground surface to be able to shoot again!
