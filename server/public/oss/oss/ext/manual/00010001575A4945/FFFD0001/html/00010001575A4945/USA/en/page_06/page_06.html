Rush: How to play

Your cubes keep bumping into each other and they really need your guidance! The goal is to make each cube reach a same-colored exit point without letting them collide with each other or fall into the abyss.

To do this, you can place these signs on the level to instruct the cubes:

Changes the direction of the cubes
Slows down the cubes
Speeds up the cubes
Makes the cubes pause for a short while
Moves the cubes without changing their direction
Consecutively turns the cubes left and right

The environment can be used to solve puzzles too! If the cubes bump into walls, they will shortly pause and then turn right. When cubes enter a warp they instantly teleport to the connected warp point.

Can you make sure they reach the exit safely?
