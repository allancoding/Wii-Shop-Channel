Downloads

●Downloads
There are additional maps and costumes available to purchase for "Family Pirate Party". To obtain these maps and costumes, you need Wii Points, which can be purchased for a fee from the Wii Shop Channel.

●Purchasing add-on content and re-downloading

Select the add-on content you would like to purchase or re-download and click on the "download" or "re-download" button. Once you purchase the add-on content, you can re-download for free, even if you delete it.

●Deleting add-on content you downloaded

You can delete the add-on content that is saved on the Wii console.
The items deleted here can be re-downloaded for free.
