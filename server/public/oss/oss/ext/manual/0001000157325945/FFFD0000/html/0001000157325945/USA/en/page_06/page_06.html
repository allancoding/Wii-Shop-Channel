How do you play?

‘Fireplacing’ is going to be completely configurable by every kind of user.

That means you’re going to be able to do all of the necessary steps, from preparing the wood, through lighting the fire, and always remembering to stoke it. But, if you prefer, you’re also going to have the option of choosing the ‘automatic’ mode to have the fireplace on your screen like a latest-generation decoration.

Either way, when you finish, you’ll never have to worry about cleaning it.

You’ll have a fireplace ready for every occasion, since you can choose between 3 pre-set designs according to the needs of the moment...
