Keyhole

You will need to connect a Nunchuk to use this control method. Use the Nunchuk to control the character and the Wii Remote to rotate the world.
Move the Control Stick on the Nunchuk left or right to make the character run. Press either the Z or C Button on the Nunchuk to make him jump.

You will use the Wii Remote’s Motion Sensor to rotate the world. This time imagine the Wii Remote is a key, and by turning it, you rotate the world accordingly.

First you have to press and hold the A Button to freeze the world. While the A Button is held down - and the world is frozen - turn the Wii Remote like a key to rotate the world.
Nothing bad will happen while the A Button is held down, so take your time and rotate the Wii Remote until the world is oriented how you want it.
Release the A Button to continue playing.

The world’s rotation will follow the rotation of the Wii Remote. Try to always hold the Wii Remote straight before you press the A Button, this way you will have more rotational freedom.
