Cloud Form

The final form—but by no means the least powerful—is the cloud form. No longer restricted by gravity, the cloud can soar into the sky and reach areas previously unattainable. The following abilities are available when in cloud form:

● Controlling the Cloud
The cloud can be moved to the left and right in the same way that you move the water and ice forms: through tilting the controller in the appropriate direction.

To make the cloud ascend, tilt the controller toward yourself. To make the cloud descend, tilt the controller away from yourself.

*These controls can be reversed so that tilting the controller toward yourself makes the cloud descend, and vice versa—by changing the Cloud up & down controls setting to Inverted in the Options menu. (→ P. 7)

·Rain
Simply shake the Wii Remote to "rain" your cloud into a puddle of water—but watch where it goes!

·Suction
By holding , you can suck balloons inside the cloud to carry them with you. It will also make the cloud slower, which helps when you need to navigate carefully through an area.

·Gust
If you press a direction on while suctioning, you will expel a gust of wind in that direction. Gusting has a number of uses:

Boosting

Gust in the opposite direction to the direction you’re moving in to get a nice speed boost. So, if you’re traveling to the right, gust to the left and you’ll be there in no time.
Moving
objects

You can use gust to make small objects move, even if you can’t reach them.
Goop
mist

You’ll discover areas throughout the Aquaticus where the Influence has formed into clouds that block your progress—but they won’t stand up to a good old gust!

·Lightning
Fancy giving something a bit of a shock? Hold down to charge up a lightning strike, and then release to fire it beneath you. You can use this to get rid of enemies and also to charge up machinery.
