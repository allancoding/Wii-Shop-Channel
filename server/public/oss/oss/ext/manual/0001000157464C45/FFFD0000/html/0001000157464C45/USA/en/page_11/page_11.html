Ice Form

When water just won’t do the job, consider trying out the ice form: it can help you push buttons, smash walls, and slide around like a pro. The following abilities are available when in ice form:

·Jump
Just like the water form, quickly lift the Wii Remote upward to make the ice block jump into the air. Again, this can be used at any time—not just when you’re on the ground.

·Stick
Once you’ve unlocked the stick ability, simply press and hold to stick onto any surface you’re currently touching. But be careful—you can only stick for a short amount of time before falling off!

By combining the stick ability with jumps, it’s possible to scale high walls and reach previously unattainable areas. You can also freeze enemies to death by sticking tight to them.
