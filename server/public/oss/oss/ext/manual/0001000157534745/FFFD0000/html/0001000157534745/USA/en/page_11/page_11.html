What Are Colorblocks?

Colorblocks temporarily prevent your opponent(s) from clearing a certain color block.
A colorblock is triggered when you clear 10+ blocks in a 3-color game or 5+ blocks in a 4-color game. An onscreen icon will indicate which color blocks cannot be cleared while the colorblock is in effect.
