Camera
Point at on the Aquarium Screen and press to change the camera and view the aquarium from another angle.

Press or to change to one of the automatic cameras (see below).

  • Camera Type
There are five camera types. Point at and press to change between them.
Centre

View the aquarium from the front.
Camera 1

View the aquarium from the left or top depending on the aquarium.
Camera 2

View the aquarium from the right.
Auto Camera 1

The camera will automatically change angle and position.
Auto Camera 2

The camera will automatically change angle and position.

Note: Regardless of the aquarium settings, the background will be black.

Note: If the Aquarium Screen is left idle for ten minutes, the camera will automatically change to Auto Camera 1. The camera will not change when focused on a fish or a Mii (see “14. Swimming with a Mii”).

Moving the Camera and Zooming
Use on the Aquarium Screen to change the camera angle. Press to zoom in and press to zoom out. Press to reset the camera.
Note: Some cameras are fixed and cannot be adjusted.
