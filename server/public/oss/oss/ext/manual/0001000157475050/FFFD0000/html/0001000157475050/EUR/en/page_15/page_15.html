Aquarium Settings

Point at on the Aquarium Screen and press to change the aquarium settings. Point at an option and press to select it.

FISH

Change the fish in the aquarium.
ORNAMENTS

Change the ornaments in the aquarium.
WALLPAPER

Change the wallpaper of the aquarium.

MUSIC

Change the background music. You can turn on water sounds and music. There are six tracks available for each aquarium theme (see “16. Track Titles”).
Change Fish

Change the number and types of fish in your aquarium. The amount of fish you can stock is limited and is dependent on the size of the fish. When you are happy with your settings, select OK to confirm.

  • Adding or Changing Fish
Point at a block with a fish and press to increase or decrease the number of that fish in the aquarium.

Point at and press to view a list of fish which can be added to the current aquarium. Available fish will be dependent on the aquarium theme.

Ornaments

Move and change ornaments, such as driftwood and rocks. When you are happy with the ornaments and their placement, select OK to confirm. Aquarium plants will be placed automatically for you.
  • Repositioning Ornaments
Point at an ornament and press to grab it. Point where you would like to place the ornament and press to place it. Ornaments can also be rotated using .

Note: When ornaments are highlighted red, they cannot be placed in that location.

  • Changing the Ornaments

Select to add ornaments. Select to remove ornaments.
