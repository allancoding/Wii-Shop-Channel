Rattle-a-Kiss

Shaking the Wii Remote will give  a playful pat, causing him to jump towards  who is waiting for a soppy smooch. If you use too little force he won't travel far enough, but put too much power into it and he could overshoot the target. Get the strength of your Wii Remote rattling just right to set-up the perfect kiss and a great score.

 

Space Battle Rattle

Press  to fire beams to hit the coming from above. Just to complicate things,  will appear from the side to block the beams, so rattle the Wii Remote to get rid of the obstructions and maintain a clear line of fire.  The more targets you hit, the higher your score.

 

Rattle Blast

Keep shaking the Wii Remote to unleash a concentrated beam of pure energy. Adjust your shaking depending on the strength of the rival beam released by
and try to resist their onslaught while maintaining your balance in the centre for as long as you can.

 

Rattle Rocket (Scores Recordable on Nintendo WFC Ranking)

Rattle the Wii Remote with all your might for 3 seconds to charge up energy, then rapidly mash the Wii Remote buttons at lift off. The more times you manage to hit the buttons, the higher your score.

Rattle Battle Fever

In this game, a high score is known as Rattle Battle Fever. If you manage to reach the level of Rattle Battle Fever, a solid gold-plated Rattle Hero will join you to celebrate your achievement. Your Rattle Battle Fever high score will be recorded in Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking.
