Mini-Games (1)

Rattle Fever (Scores Recordable on Nintendo WFC Ranking)

This mini-game is a high-octane rattle speed trial. Shake the Wii Remote as fast as you can to rack up an unbeatable score. The player with the highest score after an intense 3 second shakedown will be declared the winner!

 

Rattle In Time

Pay careful attention to the second count displayed beneath and match your shakes of the Wii Remote to the rhythm.
The more in tune your rattling is with the rhythm, the higher your score. Aim to get the highest total score from 4 rounds.

 

Rattle Reflex

Pay attention to the speech bubble above and when a matching appears on screen, it's time to shake, rattle and roll! Time it right and you'll get extra time. But if you rattle at the wrong time or miss a matching pair, time will be deducted. Keep rattling in response to the speech bubbles to get a rattling good score!

 


Ray-Gun Rattle

Shake the Wii Remote to fire a laser beam from the bottom of the screen. Be sure to wait until your enemies are lined up on screen before launching your attack as you’ll get a higher score by zapping multiple enemies simultaneously. You have 10 laser beams to do as much damage to the enemy as you can.


Rattle With Caution

Rattle the Wii Remote rapidly and you’ll approach.
But beware – if you rattle the Wii Remote while  is facing you, it's all over! Start rattling just as turns back, and keep moving forward to get a top score!

 

 

Tight-Rope Rattle

Shake the Wii Remote from side to side to get to edge his way along the tight-rope. Use a large shaking motion and  will increase in both size and speed. Take care up on the high-wire – get hit by an oncoming arrow or fall and it’s all over.  Adjust the size and strength of your motions with the Wii Remote to progress safely to the other side. The quicker you make the crossing, the higher you’ll score!
