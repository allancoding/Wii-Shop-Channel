In-Game Display Guide
Main Characters

●Rattle Hero

 

Rattle Hero is as close as this game gets to a leading man and  you’ll be seeing plenty of him throughout. Depending on the mini-game, you’ll even get the chance to send him flying across the screen.  Rattle Hero boasts a whole bunch of brothers who come in a variety of colors and sizes. He weighs in at around 1kg.

●Rattlelette

 

Rattle Hero’s sweetheart, Rattlelette enjoys dressing up and appearing in a variety of guises. In fact, it's rare to catch sight of her when she’s not sporting one of her unique outfits. Just don't ask her what her weight is - that's highly classified information...

●Rattle Meister

 

Never one to think small, Rattle Meister regards the world as his own personal property. Despite his huge ambitions, he is a relaxed soul who is usually to be found enjoying himself in the park. But take one look at his eyes when they light up, and you'll realize that he is not to be trifled with.  Frankly, his weight is the last thing on his mind...

●Kid Rattle

 

A shy soul by nature, Kid Rattle is sometimes overcome by the desire to show off. This explains why this mysterious character might be hiding one minute and appearing boldly on screen the next. Despite appearances, Kid Rattle is both older and heavier than you’d think...
