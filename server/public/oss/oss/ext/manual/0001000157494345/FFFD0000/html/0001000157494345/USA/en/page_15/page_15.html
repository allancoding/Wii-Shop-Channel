The Story

NyxQuest: Kindred Spirits takes place in ancient Greece.

 

A mythic land governed by capricious gods. Our story begins when a young man, Icarus, decides to build some wax wings. He thus flies off to the clouds where he finds something he could have never dreamed of, a palace inhabited by Nyx, a beautiful winged girl. Nyx, herself is amazed to see for the first time in her life a being similar to her. A mortal with wings!

 

Thus a beautiful friendship develops between them.

 

But destiny is always in the hands of the gods and of the ancient titans. One day a wave of fire sweeps and destroys the earth entirely. Icarus tried to escape flying high but his wings melted and fell off. Nyx realizing Icarus is not coming back, decides to come down from the heavens and starts looking for this great friend, in a world that was, so far, unknown to her.
