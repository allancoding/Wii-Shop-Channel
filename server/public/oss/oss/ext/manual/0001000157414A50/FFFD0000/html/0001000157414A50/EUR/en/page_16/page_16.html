16
Power-Ups


The power-ups, collectibles, and other goodies you will find in MotoHeroz.

Sticky Wheels

Activate this power-up and your buggy can stick to any slope.
Ghost Essence

This special item lets your buggy pass through other cars.
Spring Jump

Use this item to propel your buggy forward and upward.
Nitro Burst

This power-up gives your buggy a speed boost.
Jet Pack

Turn on this jetpack to soar through the sky.
Parachute

Activate this item for a gentle landing.
Homing Beetle

Use the beetle to flutter towards the leader.
Shockwave

Blast obstacles and bounce your opponents.
Coins

Gather coins from levels to spend in the garage.
Ancestor Spirits

Unlock their wisdom to assist your journey.
