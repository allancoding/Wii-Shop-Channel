Training Room
 
You can play unlocked games in the training room freely. There are two modes: Brain Training and Stress Training.
 
Brain Training ⇨ Brain Training is the training area for brain games. There are 25 mini-games sorted into five categories: Logic, Math, Memory, Visual, and Focus. For each mini-game, there are 3 difficulties to select from. You can always try for a higher score in each difficulty. At first, not all mini-games can be played, but you will unlock them as you progress through the game.
 
Stress Training ⇨ Stress Training is the training area for stress games. There are 5 mini-games sorted into two categories: Multitask and Physical. For each mini-game, there are 3 difficulties to select from. You can always try for a higher score in each difficulty.
