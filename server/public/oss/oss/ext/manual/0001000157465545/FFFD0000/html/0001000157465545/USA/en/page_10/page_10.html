Furballs

In Furry Legends, you control up to three different fluffy heroes.


Use the +Control Pad on your Wii Remote to switch hero.
Each Furball has a different FurPower, a special ability developed over the years to help them live in the environment they have chosen.

 

Name: Furrzie
Color: Green
FurPower: Super Speed

 

Furrzie is the leader of the tribe that inhabits the Fluffy Coast. He and his fellow Furballs love rolling around the lush and colorful lands. To make rolling over vast plateaus easier for them, they have developed a skill which temporarily increases their speed.

The Super Speed FurPower can accelerate Furrzie to achieve great speeds, allowing him to roll up the half pipes, and perform some of the longest jumps in Furland.

 

Name: Furryflow
Color: Blue
FurPower: Time Lapse

 

This Furball has chosen to live the life of a hermit, worshiping the Creator in his own temple built in a secret place miles away from any other Furballs. Through years spent in constant meditation, he has developed a power that makes time flow more slowly.

Furryflow’s Time Lapse will slow down the whole world significantly, leaving our hero only slightly affected. His FurPower is not only essential to enable him to beat some of the fast-moving obstacles, but can also come in handy when faced with a tricky jumping sequence or multiple enemies.

 

Name: Smashfurr
Color: Yellow
FurPower: Crushing Strength

 

Smashfurr and his tribe live in caves. They have developed the power to smash through hard objects like rocks to make their travels through their chosen land much easier. Their path is often blocked by rubble from collapsing caves.

Smashfurr’s FurPower not only makes his attacks twice as powerful against the Squaries, but also enables him to destroy weakened stone walls.
