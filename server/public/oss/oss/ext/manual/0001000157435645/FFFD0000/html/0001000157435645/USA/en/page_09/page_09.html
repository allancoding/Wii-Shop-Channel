Game Screen
Vital information is located at the top-left portion of the game screen. Here you will find vital data such as the currently equipped weapon, ammunition, current health and more. Keep track of all this information regularly if you don't want to restart often.

1 Displays currently equipped weapon and amount of ammunition available (if applicable).
2 Displays currently equipped weapon's level. The maximum level possible for any weapon is Level 3.
3 Displays your total health as a numerical value and meter as well.
4 Doors and teleportation devices are located through Mimiga Island. Press Down on the +Control Pad while standing in front of these and other devices to enter or activate.
5 Collect Weapon Crystals to increase the potency of most weapons.
6 You!
