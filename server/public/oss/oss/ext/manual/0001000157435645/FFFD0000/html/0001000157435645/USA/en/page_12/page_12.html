Items
Items are located in random areas, inside chests, hidden in the landscape or in the possession of characters who may or may not want to give them up.


Some items will be necessary to advance while others are optional. Typically you'll find them within chests throughout the caves, corridors and areas.


A few items, such as Health Containers will increase your overall health, but won't be maintained in the Sub-Screen. Others, such as the Map System or Silver Locket will be inventoried for later use.


Here are a few of items that you will probably discover:

Map System

The Map System will provide a full read-out of your current location. It will be incredibly useful when trying to find new and secret locations.

Arthur's Key

The key to Arthur's house. But it's hidden somewhere in Mimiga Village. Where can it be? Find it to enter Arthur's house.

Silver Locket

A silver locket in the shape of a fish. From the condition it's in, it looks like it's been treasured for years. It also appears to have been submerged in water.

ID Card

It's an ID Card. Certainly, it'll help you access something important. What it is, you'll just have to figure out. That is, assuming you can find the ID Card.
