Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Mode
Select "Nintendo WFC" from the Main Menu.
When you have successfully been connected, the Nintendo WFC Mode Selection screen will be displayed.
Select the Nintendo WFC mode you'd like to play in, and press .

● REGIONAL RACE
Battle complete strangers from within the
"Americas" region.

● WORLDWIDE RACE
Battle complete strangers from around the world.

● FRIEND RACE
Compete with friends.

● RANKING
You can view the rankings for BATTLE, STOIC, and TIME ATTACK modes.

Selecting REGIONAL RACE
or WORLDWIDE RACE
There are two modes to choose from. Select the mode you want to participate in, then press .

● BATTLE
The first player to complete 3 laps on the course is the winner. Use various items on the course to interfere with your rivals and defend against attacks.

● STOIC
Make use of techniques in a race for first place while avoiding obstacles in your path. The first player to complete 3 laps is the winner. Using Boost Devices on the course will make you go faster, but items cannot be used.

Select a character and equipment, then choose a course to start racing.
The course will be selected randomly from among those chosen by all members.
When a race ends, your rating will go up or down depending on what place you finish in.
Selecting FRIEND RACE

● FRIEND ROOM
Hold a race with friends. Select a mode to play and a character/equipment to use, and then recruit players from the friends with whom you have exchanged friend codes.

● Join a Friend
Join a friend-hosted race.
Each friend's connection conditions and race results will be displayed when opening the Friend Roster.

After joining a friend, the routine is the same for both REGIONAL RACE and WORLDWIDE RACE modes.

If the race was with friends, your race results with each friend will then be updated.

Friend Race Note

Players other than the friend selected may be participating in the race you join.

● Register a Friend
Register a friend by entering his or her friend code. You can also view registered content.

Friend Registration Note

Once a friend has been registered to your Friend Roster, your own information (such as ranking information and online status) will be sent to him or her until both of you have deleted one another's friend codes.
RANKING
Your ranking is determined by sending and receiving Rating Points in BATTLE and STOIC modes, or race times in TIME ATTACK mode.
When selecting the rank type you want to see, you will be shown the top 10 ranks for any category. You can also check how well you and your friends rank in. Ranking will be updated automatically when selecting a rank type.

 *In some cases, ranking will not be updated, or it may take time for information to be displayed.

Ranking Information

● Hudson WiiWare Portal Website
Ranking/leaderboard information including your game registration name and territory for Snowboard Riot, may be publicly displayed on the Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.

● Operation Policy
Operations may be altered depending on frequency of use. If any changes occur, details will be posted on Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.
