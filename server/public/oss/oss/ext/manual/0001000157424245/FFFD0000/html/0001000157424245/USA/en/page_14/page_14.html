Items
When passing through an Item Box on the course, an item will be mounted to your board. You won't know what kind of item is in the Item Box until you pass through it.
Shot Items
These can be obtained from red boxes.
Attacks are released from equipment attached to the front of your board. Attacks will differ depending on if you just tap the button or hold it down.
Freeze

Quick Press : A freezing bullet is launched when hit.

Long Press : Freeze time gets a little longer, and the effect gets a little better.
Power Disc

Quick Press : Launch a bullet wrapped in a whirlwind, which deflects off of walls and obstacles.

Long Press : Launch a bullet wrapped in a whirlwind behind you. It will deflect off of walls and other obstacles.
Homing

Quick Press : Launch a bullet with a highly sensitive homing function.

Long Press : Launch a faster bullet with a stronger homing function.
Lock On

Quick Press : Launch a bullet aimed at the player in first place.

Long Press : Two bullets will warp, aiming directly at the player in first place. Each bullet makes contact at different times.

* If used when you are in first place, the bullets will be aimed at you. Players who have already finished the race will not be targeted.
Napalm

Quick Press : A bullet launches forward at an arc, generating a large explosion on contact.

Long Press : This bullet splits up in midair for a wide range of attack.
Equip Items
These can be obtained from blue boxes.
When equipped to the back of your board, these items can be used to improve your own skills or weaken your rivals.
Turbo

Speeds you up for a certain amount of time.
Speed Down

Players ahead of you will slow down.

* If used when you are in first place, power is stolen from the player in second place.
Mine

Set a mine with a wide-range explosion.
Barrier

Temporarily shields you from enemy attacks.
Gauge Down

Steal SP Gauge power from the player in first place.

* If used when you are in first place, power is stolen from the player in second place.
High Jump

Using this before a jump enables you to jump higher than normal, allowing you to perform a lot of tricks at once, or to easily dodge rival attacks.
Icicle

Distracts players ahead of you by generating obstacles in front of them.

* Using this while in first place will generate an obstacle in front of your rival in second place.
