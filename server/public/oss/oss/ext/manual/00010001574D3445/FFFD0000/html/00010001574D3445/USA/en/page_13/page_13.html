Making Music

Now that you know how to preview all the loops that come with Mix Superstar, you will probably want to start putting them to use. In the browser, select a loop with the A Button and drag it anywhere you wish on the arranger screen. Dragging loops off the top of the screen will remove them.

Copying loops is extremely simple. Hold a loop by pressing the A Button. If you press the B Button, a copy of that loop will be stamped on the arranger.

If you want to copy multiple loops, draw a range around the desired loops by pressing and holding the A Button over an empty spot on arranger and dragging the pointer to make a rectangle. You can now pick up multiple loops with the A Button and stamp copies with the B Button, just like before.

To edit multiple loops at once, highlight a range of loops as above and then press the B Button while over the highlighted area to show the multiple loop options. Select 'Clear' to remove the loops from the song leaving the original space where the loops were intact. To remove the loops and the highlighted space, select 'Delete'. If you wish to insert additional space on the highlighted tracks, select 'Insert' and all of the current loops within the highlighted track space will push ahead.

Scrolling and Zooming
To zoom in or out of your current mix, press the + Button or – Button and you'll be able to fine-tune the timing of the loops in your mix. You can also scroll left and right through your mix by using the +Control Pad or by pressing the B Button on an empty area and dragging left or right.
