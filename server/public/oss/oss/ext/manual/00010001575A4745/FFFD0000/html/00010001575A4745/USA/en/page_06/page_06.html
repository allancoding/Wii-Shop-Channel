Character/Course Selection

To Select A Character:

  • Note: The Wii Remote with Player LED 1 must be active for both single player and multiplayer games. In this online manual the person using the Wii Remote with Player LED 1 lit is called Player 1 or designated P1.
  • Player 1’s cage (P1) is open when the Character Selection screen appears. Player 1 uses the left and right arrows on the Wii Remote’s +Control Pad to cycle through the available characters: Rockhopper Penguin, Kangaroo, Panda, Meerkat.
  • When the desired character is in the cage, Player 1 presses the A Button. The Select Handedness window appears. The player must select the hand he or she will use to swing the Wii Remote. (Be aware that the animated character always uses its right appendage to throw the disc, but the Wii Remote responds to the location and orientation of the hand selected here.) Place the cursor on the desired option and press the A Button. The GO button lights up.

To Play as Single Player:

  • Player 1 places the cursor on GO and presses the A Button to advance to the course selection screen.

To Play in Multiplayer Mode:

  • Up to three additional players may join the game by selecting a character using separate Wii Remotes. Every Wii Remote to be used in the game must be synchronized with the Wii console.
  • Press the B Button to return to the previous screen or step.
  • Each additional player should press the A Button on his or her Wii Remote to open a cage and make a character selection following the steps described for Player 1 above.
  • Once all players have selected a character, Player 1 places the cursor on the GO button and presses the A Button to advance to the Course Selection Screen. The GO button is not lit until all additional players have selected a character.

Course Selection:

  • The Zoo Disc Golf Course is made up of 18 holes.
  • A set of three holes, nine holes, or all eighteen holes of the course are available. Selecting "3" (holes) or "9" (holes) places additional options on the Zoo Disc Golf Course Map.
  • Player 1 selects the desired set of holes then selects Start Game.
