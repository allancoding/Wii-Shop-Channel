Album

You can view the pictures you took at the Ranch or post pictures to the Wii Message Board. You can save up to 20 pictures in the Album. You can also save your pictures to an SD Card.
* If you take more than 20 pictures, the oldest pictures will automatically be deleted.

Album

When you select "Album" under the main menu, an Album screen will be displayed. When you want to move to a different picture, point to the green arrow, press (left or right), , or .

Back

Exit the Album screen.

Save to SD Card

Save the pictures from My Pokémon Ranch to an SD Card (saves as jpegs).
* Pictures you save to an SD Card cannot be restored in the Album, but you can view them in the Wii Photo Channel or on your computer.
* The copyright of all pictures taken in My Pokémon Ranch belong to The Pokémon Company and other parties. Please do not use them outside of copyright law.

Delete all pictures on SD Card

Delete all of the My Pokémon Ranch pictures saved on the SD Card.

Post on the Wii Message Board

You can post and look at pictures on the Wii Message Board.

Secure / Cancel Secure

You can secure or cancel secure on a picture. When you secure a picture, that picture won't be deleted. You can secure up to 19 pictures.

Delete

You can delete the picture that is currently displayed.

Notes about an SD Card

  • Nintendo recommends the use of an officially licensed SD Card. While any SD Card (up to 2 GB) can be used, Nintendo can only guarantee the functionality of officially licensed Nintendo products.
  • When you save pictures from My Pokémon Ranch to an SD Card, don't change the name of the directory or the file names when you transfer those pictures to a computer. When you copy pictures from an SD Card to the Wii, copy the exact directory to the Wii console. Changing the name of the directory or the file names might cause unreadable data.
