Pokémon Introduction Screen

Ranch Screen

Main Menu

When you point at the screen, the main menu will be displayed. The buttons you can select at this point will be displayed. (→p.10)

Name Display
The name of the Pokémon or Mii will be displayed when you Focus on it. If you mark your favorite Pokémon, you can bring only those Pokémon to the Ranch by selecting Favorites in the Pokémon on the Ranch menu. (→p.17)
  • This feature becomes available when your Ranch reaches a certain level.
Modes (→p.10)

When the mode is set to "Free Mode," the words "Free Mode" are displayed on the screen. If nothing is shown, it means the mode is set to "Auto Mode."
* "Free Mode" becomes available when your Ranch reaches a certain level.

  • "Auto Mode"
    This mode automatically changes the Pokémon and Mii focused on.
  • "Free Mode"
    This mode allows you to view the Ranch freely. It becomes available when your Ranch reaches a certain level.
Number of Pokémon

How many Pokémon you have on your Ranch.

Focus Lock

When you have used Focus Lock on a Pokémon or Mii, "Focus Lock" will be displayed on the screen. When you press on a Pokémon or Mii, you can set the Focus Lock. By pressing again, you can cancel the Focus Lock.
