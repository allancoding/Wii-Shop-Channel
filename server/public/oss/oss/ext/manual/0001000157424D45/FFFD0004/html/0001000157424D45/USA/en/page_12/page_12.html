Connect to DS

You can deposit or withdraw Pokémon you caught in Pokémon Diamond or Pokémon Pearl using the Nintendo DS.

Please Note:

  • You can only withdraw Pokémon that you deposited to the Ranch from a DS Game Card back to that same Game Card.
  • If you deposited Pokémon to the Ranch and started a new game on the Nintendo DS Pokémon game, you can't withdraw the Pokémon to that Game Card.
  • You can't deposit Pokémon that you have in your party. You must deposit it to Bebe's PC before connecting.
  • You can deposit a maximum of 1,000 Pokémon from up to eight different Game Cards (up to eight Trainers). If you want to deposit additional Pokémon from a new Pokémon Diamond or Pokémon Pearl Game Card, withdraw all of the Pokémon deposited from any one Game Card.
  • If you use an external device or software to modify the game save data, you might not be able to play the game, cause corrupted data, or lose the data. Once a game is corrupted, the save data cannot be restored.
  • My Pokémon Ranch save data, including Pokémon data, cannot be saved to an SD Card.
  • My Pokémon Ranch can connect only to English-language versions Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl Game Cards.

Wii screen

When you connect Pokémon Diamond or Pokémon Pearl to My Pokémon Ranch, you will find it is easier to connect if you select "Connect to DS" in the My Pokémon Ranch main menu first, and then select "Connect to Wii" in the Pokémon Diamond or Pokémon Pearl main menu.

If you successfully connect to the Nintendo DS, the screens below will be displayed on both sides (Wii side & DS side). From here, you'll operate the game with your DS.

However, the first time you connect your Nintendo DS, you will need to choose a Caretaker on the Wii side (My Pokémon Ranch). (→p.17)
*You can change the Caretaker at any time.
