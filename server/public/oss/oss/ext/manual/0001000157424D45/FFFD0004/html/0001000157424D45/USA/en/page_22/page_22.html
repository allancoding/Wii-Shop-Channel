Preparation for WiiConnect24

Your pictures and Ranch-status info will automatically be sent to your Wii Friends via WiiConnect24.
* Please read the Wii Operations Manual Channels and Settings and set WiiConnect24 to ON.

Register a Wii Friend

When you want to communicate with your friend, you need to register him or her to your Wii Friends.
* Please refer to the Wii Operations Manual Channels and Settings (Wii Message Board).

* Once both you and your friend register each other, the nickname color on the Address Book in the Wii Message Board changes from light gray to black.

Caution!

  • If you have a problem connecting to the Internet, please read the "Error Code" and "Error Message" and refer to the "What to do if this happens?" section in the Wii Operations Manual Channels and Settings.
  • If you change the time on the Wii console, it could affect the game. We recommend not changing the time once you set up the game.
