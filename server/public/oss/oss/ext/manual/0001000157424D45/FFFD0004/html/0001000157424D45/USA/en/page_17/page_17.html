Deposit Details

You can check Pokémon on the Ranch, see Mii info, and view the Pokémon Parade.

You can also change the selection of Pokémon you want to bring to the Ranch by pointing at the Pokémon on the Ranch Button and pressing . You can change selection to "All," "Trainer," or "Favorites."

What Is a Pokémon Caretaker?

It's a Mii who takes care of Pokémon brought to the Ranch by a Trainer. When you set up a Caretaker, the assigned Caretaker and Pokémon will be friendly to each other.

Change the Caretaker of Pokémon

On the Deposit Details screen, point at the Mii you want to change and press .

  • Select a Mii Already on the Ranch as a Caretaker

When the Setup-Caretaker screen is displayed, point at the Mii that you want to change and press . The background of the Mii changes to blue.
* A Mii that has a below its face is a Mii who is already assigned as a Caretaker on the Ranch.

  • Select a Mii as a Caretaker Who Is Not on the Ranch

If you want to select a different Mii that you have on the Setup-Caretaker screen, point at "Select a different Mii" and press . If you have any other Miis in the Mii Channel, those Miis will be displayed. Point at the Mii you want to select and press . The background of the Mii changes to blue.
* You cannot choose "Select a different Mii" if you have the maximum number of Miis on the Ranch.

Once setup is complete, point at "Okay and Return" and press to return to the "Deposit Details" screen.
*You cannot change the Caretaker status for Hayley.

  • If You Want to Erase a Mii

When you erase a Mii on the Mii Channel, that Mii will disappear from the Ranch, and a new Mii will be automatically assigned as a Caretaker.

Add a Pokémon to Favorites

This feature becomes available when you bring a certain number of Pokémon to the Ranch. Once you add Pokémon to Favorites, you have the option of bringing only those Pokémon to the Ranch. To add to Favorites, point at next to the Pokémon Name and press . You'll see a crown next to the Pokémon Name. When you want to remove it from Favorites, point at the crown and press .

Parade

When you point at the Go to a Parade Button and press , the Pokémon Parade begins. In the Pokémon Parade, the Pokémon Name, Level, Capture Date, and Location info will be displayed. Point at the door when you want to exit the Parade.
