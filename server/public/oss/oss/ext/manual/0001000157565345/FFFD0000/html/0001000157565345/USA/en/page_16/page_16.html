The Gods
General information

In this game, you will embody twelve Gods (three Gods in each Mythology).

These Gods' Powers are broken down as follows:

  • Five Powers are common to all Gods in all Mythologies
  • Two Powers are specific to each Mythology
  • One Power is specific to each God
The Egyptian Gods

OSIRIS

Osiris is the embodiment of good, the God of the Dead, of flora, of farming and resurrection.

Osiris' element is Fire.

SETH

Seth is Osiris' brother, and the embodiment of evil. He is the God of violence, crime, chaos and destruction.

Seth's element is Lightning.

RA

Ra (or Re) is the God of the sun.

Ra's element is Fire.

The Greek & Roman Gods

ZEUS / JUPITER

Zeus (or Jupiter) is the king of all Gods, the God of the skies, lightning, weather and all divine manifestations.

Zeus's element is Lightning.

POSEIDON / NEPTUNE

Poseidon (or Neptune) is Zeus' brother. He reigns over the oceans, rivers great and small, sea storms, sea monsters and earthquakes.

Poseidon's element is Water.

ATHENA / MINERVA

Athena (or Minerva) is Zeus' daughter. She is the goddess of wisdom, war, craftsmen and artists.

Athena's element is Wind.

The Norse Gods

ODIN

Odin is the God of war, warriors, poetry, magic, knowledge and wisdom.

Odin's element is Fire.

THOR

Thor is the God of thunder, warriors, law, order and justice; he is also the protector of the Gods.

Thor's element is Lightning.

NJORD

Njord is the God of the seas, of winds, fire and sea creatures.

Njord's element is Water.

The Japanese Kami

AMATERASU

The female Kami of sun, light, growth and fertility. She is Susanoo's sister.

Her element is Fire.

SAKUYA

The female Kami of flora.

Her element is Wind.

SUSANOO

The male Kami of storms and thunder. Amaterasu's brother.

His elements are Lightning and Water.
