The Tower's construction

The Tower is built by the Humans. It comprises Foundations, 10 Levels and a Summit.

Each Level features its own health points, which vary according to the Level type and consolidation status.

Slots

Each Level features a number of slots reserved for Human Units and Powers. Their number varies according to the type of Level.

The different types of slots are the following:

Resources: this is where Carriers store the resources required to build the Tower. There is usually only one Resources slot per Level, except in the Warehouse.

Defensive Powers: this is where you can use your Defensive Powers. Usually, only one of these slots is available on each Level, and each slot can only house one Power at a time (except the Evil Temple, which doesn't feature any slots at all).

Construction Supervisor: this is where the Construction Supervisor is placed; his role is to watch over the other Units. There is only one Construction Supervisor on each Level.

Builders: this is where Builders are placed; each Level features four of these slots, each placed before a Pillar.

Priests: this is where Good or Evil Priests stand and preach.

Resources

Resources are placed on each Level by Carriers. Then, the Builders use these Resources to build the Tower's Levels. If the Builders run out of Resources, they cannot work.

The construction stages

Each Level goes through four construction stages:

First stage: this is the base of the Level.

Second stage: the Level now has one third of its health points.

Third stage: the Level now has two thirds of its health points.

Fourth stage: the Level is fully constructed.

The Level base cannot be destroyed until the Level's construction is completed, i.e. until the Level has reached the fourth construction stage.

Consolidation

Humans can consolidate a Level when they deem fit to do so. The Level’s health points then increase significantly.

However, Humans cannot consolidate a Level unless its construction is completed, the Level has at least 75% of its health points and the Level located below has already been consolidated.

During the consolidation process, the Builders cannot repair the Level if it has taken damage. Once the consolidation is over, however, all the Level's health points are restored.

Pillars

Levels are built on four Pillars, which each contain a share of the Levels' health points. Your objective is to destroy the Pillars using your Offensive Powers.

Repairs

If they have enough Resources available, the Builders can repair the damaged Pillars. Once repaired, the Pillars regain all their health points.

Foundations

These form the Tower's base; you can destroy them to win the game.

They have a very large number of health points. If you spend your time attacking them without dealing with the Humans, they will reach the Tower's Summit before you even have time to destroy its Foundations.

The Summit

The Summit connects the Tower to the Portal leading to your realm.

It cannot be destroyed; you must destroy a Level located below it to make it disappear.

The Portal to the Gods’ realm

The Portal is the Gods' only line of defense. If the Humans succeed in destroying it, the game is over!

The Gods’ Portal has few health points. The Humans' Battering Ram is designed to destroy it.
