Levels

Five different types of Levels are available.

Each different type of Level features specific strong points and weak points, for Humans and Gods alike.

A Level's specificity only becomes apparent once the Level is fully built.

However, during the Level's construction, a sign shows what type of Level is being constructed.

Neutral Levels

Beneficial to: no one.

The neutral Level is the most basic Level available. It provides no benefits to anyone.

Specificity: none.

The Warehouse

Beneficial to: Humans.

The Warehouse enables Carriers to store Resources and later carry them to another Level without having to ever leave the Tower.

Specificity: it can contain four times as many resources as any other Level.

The Forge

Beneficial to: Humans.

The Forge is essential for Humans. This is where the Blacksmith will produce the Battering Ram Unit, i.e. the weapon with which the Gods' Portal will be destroyed. A Tower can only contain one Forge.

Specificity: the Battering Ram Unit is bound to this Level. If this Level is destroyed, the Battering Ram will leave the Tower.

The Evil Temple

Beneficial to: Humans.

An Evil Priest lives on this Level and draws Humans to the dark side with his sermons. This Level is difficult to destroy.

Specificity: very solid; Defensive Powers cannot be placed on this Level.

The Good Temple

Beneficial to: Gods.

The Good Temple is a Level dedicated solely to a God. It is built when the number of Respect points gathered during a game reaches a certain level. A Good Priest always lives on this Level.

Specificity: this Level can be destroyed by the player if necessary. It has very few health points.
