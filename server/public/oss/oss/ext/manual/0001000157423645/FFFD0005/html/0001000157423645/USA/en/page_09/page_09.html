Wheel

At the end of each round of the Classic, Light, and Scratch Quiz, Jerry will give you a chance to bet your winnings on the wheel.
Jerry will ask you if you want to spin the wheel. You can accept or refuse to spin the wheel.
The wheel sequence will appear once per round. Depending on whether you chose three, six, or nine Quiz Rounds in the Main Menu, you will be offered the chance to spin the wheel three, six, or nine times.

A) Basic Controls

After you accept to spin the wheel, the first player will appear in front of the wheel. Press the A Button to grab the wheel.
Swing your Wii Remote to the left or right to spin the wheel. Wait for the wheel to stop on a section to show how lucky you are.
The wheel sequence starts with Player 1 and will continue in consecutive order for all the contestants who accepted to spin the wheel.
 
B) Bonus-Penalty

The wheel will make you lose cash if it stops on a Penalty section.

The wheel will make you win cash if it stops on a Bonus section.

You will lose all your money if you land on the Bankrupt section!

If the wheel stops on a Player Gift section, you will receive cash from a contestant of your choice.

If the wheel stops on a Player Steal section, you must give the indicated sum of cash to a contestant of your choice.

If the wheel stops on a Player Swap section, you must exchange your total winnings with the total winnings of another contestant! Fortunes can change drastically in a fraction of a second...
