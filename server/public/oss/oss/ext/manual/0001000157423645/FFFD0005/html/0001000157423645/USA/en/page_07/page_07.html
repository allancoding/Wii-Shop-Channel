7
Quiz

A) Classic Quiz

Use your Wii Remote to choose the correct answer from a selection of possible answers. Point at the answer that you think is correct and press the A Button to confirm.
You can change your selection as often as you like, but the faster you answer, the more cash you will get.
You have 15 seconds to answer each question.
Correctly answer as many questions as you can in each round of seven questions. As you progress further through the quiz, the amount of cash you can win will increase.


Hint!

⇨ Try to answer correctly as fast as possible to win more cash than the other contestants with the same answer.
 
B) Light Quiz

Use your Wii Remote as a flashlight to illuminate shadowed blocks of answers. Move your flashlight across the darkened answer boxes to reveal answers. Point at the answer that you think is correct and press the A Button to confirm.
You can change your selection as often as you like, but the faster you answer, the more cash you will get.
You have 15 seconds to answer each question.
Correctly answer as many questions as you can in each round of seven questions. As you progress further through the quiz, the amount of cash you can win will increase.

Hint!

⇨ Try to pay attention to all the revealed texts as you move the flashlight.
⇨ You don't need to reveal all of the text to choose an answer.
 
C) Scratch Quiz

Use your Wii Remote to scratch the surface of the covered answer boxes. Move your cursor across the answer boxes to reveal the covered answers. Point at the answer that you think is correct and press the A Button to confirm.
You can change your selection as often as you like, but the faster you answer, the more cash you will get.
You have 15 seconds to answer each question.
Correctly answer as many questions as you can in each round of seven questions. As you progress further through the quiz, the amount of cash you can win will increase.

Hint!

⇨ Try to pay attention to all the revealed texts as you scratch the surface of the covered boxes.
⇨ You don't need to scratch each box completely to choose an answer.
⇨ You can also choose an answer scratched by another player.
