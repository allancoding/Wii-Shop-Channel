8
Kung Fu Technique

Do you have what it takes to be a Kung Fu Master? Well time to prove it. Pass through a series of wacky trials designed to test your strength, agility, accuracy and funkiness!

Chopsticks:
Using the Wii Remote.
Swing the Wii Remote towards the buzzing flies to catch them with your chopsticks.

Using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk.
Swing either controller towards the buzzing flies to catch them with your chopsticks.

Chopping:
Using the Wii Remote.
Swing the Wii Remote repetitively in one direction to charge your chop power.

When the avatar changes his action, change your swings to a different direction to keep the power charging. At the end of the 30 second countdown you will automatically chop the plates.

Using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk.
Swing the Wii Remote and Nunchuk repetitively in alternating directions to charge your chop. When the avatar changes his action, change your swings to a different direction to keep the power charging. At the end of the 30 second countdown you will automatically chop the plates.

Balancing:
Using the Wii Remote.
Tilt the Wii Remote forward, backward, left and right to keep your avatar balanced on top of a pedestrian's hat.

When your avatar is well balanced, swing the Wii Remote upwards to make him jump onto the next pedestrian.

Using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk.
Tilt the Wii Remote and Nunchuk forward, backward, left and right together to keep your avatar balanced on top of a pedestrian's hat.

When your avatar is well balanced, swing both the Wii Remote and Nunchuk upwards simultaneously to make him jump onto the next pedestrian.

Singing:
Using the Wii Remote.
Swing the Wii Remote left, right or diagonally left and diagonally right towards the signaled ox.

Using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk.
Swing the Wii Remote right or down or swing the Nunchuk left or down, separately or together towards the signaled oxen.

Jumping:
Using the Wii Remote.
Swing the Wii Remote up, left or right to jump forward or sideways.

Using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk.
Swing the Wii Remote and Nunchuk together up, left or right to jump forward or sideways.

Sneaking:
Using the Wii Remote.
Swing the Wii Remote up and down to move, stop swinging to stop moving.

Using the Wii Remote and Nunchuk.
Swing the Wii Remote and Nunchuk alternately up and down to move, stop swinging to stop moving.
