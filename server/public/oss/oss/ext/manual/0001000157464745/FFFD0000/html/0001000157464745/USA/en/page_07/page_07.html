Multiplayer Modes

There are four multiplayer mini games in Frogger Returns. Each one offers a quick and fun two player experience.

 

Classic Race:

The classic race is a simple best of 5 race set against another opponent. There are 5 lily pad goals on one end of the screen which the players must try to reach before their opponent. As soon as a player successfully reaches a goal, that goal is filled with a frog of that player's color and that goal cannot be used again. The first player to fill three goals wins the match.

 

Territories:

Territories is a simple game of occupying the most goals in a set time limit. Goals are occupied by the player who reached them last and occupied goals can still be taken over by the other player until time runs out. The player with the most occupied goals when time runs out wins the match.

 

Collector:

In Collector, flies of varying values are placed around the level. As the player picks up flies the value of each fly is added to their inventory. When a player reaches a goal they take over that goal and the value of their inventory is added to their score. Players lose their inventory if they die before reaching a goal. The game ends when all of the goals have been filled. The winner is the player who has the most points when the game is over.

 

Fly Feast:

This mini game is a timed match set against an opponent on a single screen map. Flies appear on random points on the map. Players must collect as many flies as they can before time runs out. When time runs out, the player with the most flies wins the match.
