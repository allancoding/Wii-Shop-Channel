Add-On Content
You can spend even more hours enjoying the game by purchasing add-on content! Like with 1P PUZZLE mode, each package contains 135 stages spread out over 27 zones, with every stage given a unique design.
1P EXTRA PACK 1
This package contains re-imagined stages from BUST-A-MOVE 2, a game with one of the best difficulty curves and puzzle designs in the series.



Name: 1P EXTRA PACK 1
Wii Points: 200
Blocks: 1

<<WARNING>>
1P EXTRA PACK 1 simply offers add-on content for BUST-A-MOVE Plus! and cannot be played on its own.
1P EXTRA PACK 2
These 135 hand-picked stages from BUST-A-MOVE 3 offer an exciting variety of puzzle designs and difficulties. Recommended for anyone wanting to expand their BUST-A-MOVE experience.


Name: 1P EXTRA PACK 2
Wii Points: 200
Blocks: 1

<<WARNING>>
1P EXTRA PACK 2 simply offers add-on content for BUST-A-MOVE Plus! and cannot be played on its own.
