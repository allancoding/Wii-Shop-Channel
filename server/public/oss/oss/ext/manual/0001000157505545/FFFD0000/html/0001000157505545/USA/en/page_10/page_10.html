Bubbles and Blocks
You'll encounter various bubbles and blocks throughout the game.
Bubbles
<Normal Bubbles>
These bubbles come in 8 different colors and lack any kind of special qualities.
Bursting the bubbles will release the game characters contained inside.
<Star Bubble>
Firing a Star Bubble at a Normal Bubble causes all bubbles of the same color to burst. Firing a Normal Bubble at a Star Bubble also has this effect. However, firing a Star Bubble at another Star Bubble has no effect.
<Rainbow Bubble>
If a Normal Bubble touching a Rainbow Bubble bursts, the Rainbow Bubble turns into a Normal Bubble of the same color. However, firing a Rainbow Bubble at another Rainbow Bubble has no effect.
<Metal Bubble>
A powerful bubble that destroys any bubble in its path, but bursts upon contact with blocks and ceilings.
<Fire Bubble>
A Fire Bubble explodes upon contact with a Normal Bubble, destroying all bubbles around it. This includes special bubbles as well, so watch out!
Blocks
<Support Blocks>
These blocks hold up bubbles within the Bubble Zone and can only be destroyed by bursting the bubbles around it.
<Hinder Blocks>
These annoying blocks get stuck between bubbles, and the only way to get rid of them is by making them fall. However, you can still clear a stage without getting rid of all Hinder Blocks. If a Hinder Block drops below the Zone Line due to bubbles dropping in from above, it will simply fall off without the stage ending.
