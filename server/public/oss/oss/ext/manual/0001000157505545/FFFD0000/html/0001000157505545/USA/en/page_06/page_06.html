The Basics
BUST-A-MOVE Plus! is a puzzle game where the goal is to remove all the bubbles in every stage by firing colored bubbles at them. Any time three bubbles of the same color touch, they will burst.



You can play the game alone in 1P PLAY or compete against a computer or human opponent in BATTLE MODE. Your records are saved, so you can always come back to challenge your best score!
Game Rules
Fire bubbles from your Bubble Launcher at bubbles of the same color until none remain on screen.
Game Over and Continue

If the bubbles cross over the Zone Line, you lose the stage.

You can continue playing infinitely by selecting YES at the Continue Screen.

If you select NO, the game will end.
Saving Your Progress
Everything from Settings to Hi-Scores are automatically saved as you play the game. However, the following actions may cause your save data to be deleted. Deleted data cannot be restored, so please be careful.

  • Turning off the Wii console or Wii Remote while saving.
  • Resetting the Wii console or Wii Remote while saving.
  • Accessing the Wii Menu from the HOME Menu while saving.
  • Resetting from the HOME Menu while saving.
  • Unplugging the Wii console while saving.
