1P PLAY
Tackle the puzzles on your own in these modes.

1P PUZZLE
Stages created specifically for the WiiWare version.

Re-creations of stages from BUST-A-MOVE 2.
1P EXTRA PACK 2 *

BATTLE
Compete against opponents in these modes. The first to win two rounds wins the battle.

CPU BATTLE
Play against 8 computer opponents.
2P BATTLE
Play against a friend.

SCORE RANKING
You can view Hi-Scores for the following game modes.

• 1P PUZZLE
• 1P EXTRA PACK 1 *
• 1P EXTRA PACK 2 *
• CPU BATTLE

* 1P EXTRA PACK 1 and 2 are add-on content. You will need to purchase them before being able to view their Hi-Scores.



OPTIONS
You can change the following game settings.

• Difficulty in 1P PUZZLE mode
• Guide Line (ON/OFF)
• Music volume
• Sound effects volume
