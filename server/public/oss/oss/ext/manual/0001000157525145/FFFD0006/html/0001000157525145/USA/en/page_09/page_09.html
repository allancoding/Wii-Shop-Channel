World Tour

Here you can go to the main game, see unlocked trophies, or view the statistics and status of your skills.

The Map

Highlight and confirm the next location by pressing the B button. You can play/re-play all the unlocked levels, improve your skills and serarch for the legendary animals.

 

Types of challenge

Numbered

Kill a certain number of animals.

 

Sniper

High skilled distance shots.

 

Tagging

You must use special ammo to “tag” a particular animal.

 

Vitals

Kill a certain number of animals by hitting their vitals only.

 

Avoiding

Avoid shooting a particular kind of animal.

 

Skills

Increase your skills by using skill points earned:

 

Precision: increase the shooting precision of weapons.

 

Reloading time: decrease the reloading time of weapons.

 

Heart rate: influenced by a dangerous situation. If it is too high, the player will lose accuracy.

 

Health: increase your health points.
