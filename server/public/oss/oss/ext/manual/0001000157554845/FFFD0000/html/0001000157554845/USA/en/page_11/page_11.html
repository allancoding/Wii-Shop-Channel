Game-Play Techniques

Here are a few useful techniques to help you achieve the highest score possible and spice up this grilling competition!

● Turn Up the Heat!
Crank up the heat on your grill and cook the meat even faster by pressing and holding either or . Try this trick once you get the hang of picking up and transporting the meat. Points will be added to your score automatically!

● Just Right!
When a piece of meat has been cooked to perfection, it will plump up on the grill. Grab the meat right after it plumps up and quickly transport it to your plate. These Just Right! pieces of meat will earn you bonus points!

● Combo Scores
Get more than one Just Right! in a row to start a combo that will earn you even more points for as long as it continues!
