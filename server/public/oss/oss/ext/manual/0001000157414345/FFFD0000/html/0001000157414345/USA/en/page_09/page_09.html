9
Color Cells

Your goal is to clear all the cells before the platform reaches the bottom of the screen. Shoot cells upwards and try to form groups of 3 or more cells of the same color. Once the group of 3 cells is formed – it disappears. Sometimes, you might need to shoot the cells against the wall, so that they bounce off and hit your target. The game will end if you cannot clear all the cells before they reach the bottom of the screen. From time to time, bonuses appear within the cells – try to get them by arranging a group of 3 cells, where the cell with the bonus inside will be a part of the group of 3 cells.

Controls:

  • Hold Wii Remote Horizontally.
  • Aim the cannon using and shoot using .


Bonuses:

  • Life - You get an additional life.
  • UP - Your cannon gets loaded with a special shell that shifts the whole group of cells upwards by 2 rows.
  • Row - Your cannon gets loaded with a special shell that clears one row of cells.
  • Bomb - Your cannon gets loaded with a special shell that destroys all nearby cells.
