Global Rankings
Global Rankings allow you to view the top 30 players in your region and around the world. You can also view your rank by registering your clear record via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. The three types of rankings are high score (Total Score Ranking), speed (Clear Time Ranking), and bubbles used (Bubble Ranking).
Note: Your own results will not be displayed until you have cleared all stages.
Regional Rankings
View the top 30 scores in your region as well as your result for each map.
World Rankings
View the top 30 scores in the world as well as your result for each map.
Register Hi-Score
Register your results for Total Score Ranking, Clear Time Ranking, and Bubble Ranking.
