Play Mode
Standard Mode lets you relive classic Bubble Bobble gameplay. Arrange Mode has stages with floors set at 45-degree angles and allows up to 4 players to play simultaneously. You can also purchase add-on content at the Shop.
Standard Mode
This mode brings back classic Bubble Bobble gameplay, with a total of 100 normal rounds for 2 players.
Arrange Mode
Gameplay is the same as Standard Mode with simultaneous play expanded to 4 players and 45-degree angle floors added. It also consists of 100 normal rounds.
Super Standard Mode
These are 100 rounds with a higher difficulty level than Standard Mode.
Super Arrange Mode
These are 100 rounds with a higher difficulty level than Arrange Mode.
Expert Mode 1
Expert Mode 2
Available for purchase in the Shop. These levels are more difficult than those in the regular game. We recommend these for players who want to further master the game and have a fun, nerve-wracking experience. Features a total of 50 rounds, as well as a challenging, unusual final boss.
