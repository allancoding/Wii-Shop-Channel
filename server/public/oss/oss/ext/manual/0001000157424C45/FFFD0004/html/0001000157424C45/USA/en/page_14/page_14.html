Items & Food
Here is a list of the effects of the main items and the point values of food.
Main Items
[Pink Candy]
Extends the distance bubbles travel.
[Blue Candy]
Increases the speed of bubbles.
[Orange Candy]
Allows for rapid-fire release of bubbles.
[Shoes]
Increases your movement speed.
[Orange Umbrella]
Warps 3 stages.
[Dynamite]
Kills all enemies with a large explosion.
[Chakkun's Heart]
Player becomes invincible temporarily. Any enemies touched during this time die.
[Cross of Thunder]
Creates massive lightning, eliminating all enemies.
[Cross of Water]
Floods the current stage, eliminating all enemies.
[Cross of Fire]
Lets you breathe fire 16 times.
Main Food
[Banana]
Gives you 500 pts.
[Field Peas]
Gives you either 70 or 700 pts.
[Peach]
Gives you either 450 or 2000 pts.
[Tangerine]
Gives you either 400 or 1000 pts.
[Watermelon]
Gives you either 600 or 3000 pts.
[Big Eclair]
Enormous piece of food that appears upon clearing a round if you have obtained a certain item. Worth 30000 pts.
[Big Shortcake]
Enormous piece of food that appears upon clearing a round if you have obtained a certain item. Worth 30000 pts.
[Big Soda Pop Ice]
Enormous piece of food that appears upon clearing a round if you have obtained a certain item. Worth 10000 pts.
[Big Watermelon]
Enormous piece of food that appears upon clearing a round if you have obtained a certain item. Worth 20000 pts.
[Big Tangerine]
Enormous piece of food that appears upon clearing a round if you have obtained a certain item. Worth 20000 pts.
