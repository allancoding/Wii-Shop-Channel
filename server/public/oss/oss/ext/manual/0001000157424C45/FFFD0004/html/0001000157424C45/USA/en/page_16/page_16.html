Extend Bubbles
EXTEND Bubbles are bubbles that each contain a letter of the alphabet. They appear from air vents during rounds when you fulfill certain conditions. Collect them to spell out words and increase your lives.
In Standard Mode

Spelling out E-X-T-E-N-D in Standard Mode will increase your lives by 1. Collecting duplicate bubbles will not eliminate other bubbles you have collected.

Note: This also applies to Super Standard Mode

In Arrange Mode
EXTEND Bubbles in Arrange Mode are a little different. There are 4 patterns that can be collected. The patterns and the corresponding number of lives are listed below.
Note: This also applies to Super Arrange Mode, Expert Mode 1, and Expert Mode 2.

 

 


[H][E][R][O] . . . . . . . . . increases lives by 1.
[E][X][T][E][N][D] . . . . increases lives by 2.
[N][E][V][E][R]. . . . . . . increases lives by 3.
[W][I][N][N][E][R] . . . . increases lives by 5.

[Tip]
When playing Arrange Mode, think about which word you want to spell before you start collecting letters. If you end up getting a letter not in the word you are aiming for, you will begin spelling a new word and lose all letters not in the new word. Spelling out words may seem difficult at first, but the reward is worth the effort. Hang in there!
