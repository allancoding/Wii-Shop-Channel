How to play

Commands of the game

 

PLAYER : Point at your player with your Wii Remote to start. It will follow the movements of your Pointer.

 

ENEMIES : If you touch an enemy of a different color you lose a life.

You automatically absorb enemies of the same color as yourself when you touch them (unless they are unabsorbable enemies).

 

COLOR SWAP : Change the color of your spaceship by moving over a color change item. You will then take on its color.

 

FUSE YOUR ENTITIES : Bring your entities together and hold down Z Button (or B Button). Separate them by releasing the button.

TAKING A BREAK : Press the + Button.
