Items
These items will appear during battle.

 

Fire Up
Your blast range will increase by 1 block, to a maximum of 8.
Bomb Up
The number of bombs you can set increases by 1, to a maximum of 8.
Speed Up
Your movement speed increases by 1 level, up to a maximum level of 8.
Bomb Kick
Enables you to kick set bombs. Press while a kicked bomb is moving to stop it.
Line Bomb
After setting a bomb, press again to set a line of bombs all at once in the direction you're facing.
Penetrating Bomb
A blast from this bomb will penetrate Soft Blocks.
Bouncing Bomb
Kick this bomb into the wall and it will bounce back the other way.
Fire Down
Your blast range will decrease by 1 block, to a minimum of 1.
Bomb Down
The number of bombs you can set decreases by 1, to a minimum of 1.
Speed Down
Your movement speed decreases by 1 level, to a minimum level of 0.
Full Fire
Blast range is maxed out for all bombs.
Punch
Punch a set bomb to send it flying.
Power Glove
This allows you to throw bombs. You can also pick up a bomb and carry it around.
Power Bomb
The first bomb you set will have a maximum blast range.
Dangerous Bomb
The first bomb you set will explode over a wide area.
Mine Bomb
The first bomb you set will be buried in the ground, and no one can see it. It will explode once a player steps on it.
Skull
One of the following 9 symptoms will occur at random.

● Diarrhea: Bombs will set themselves.

● Constipation: You won't be able to set bombs.

● Slow: You'll start to move very slowly.

● Quick: You'll start to move very quickly.

● Dizzy: You'll suddenly be stunned while playing.

● Reckless: You won't be able to stop moving.

● Confusion: You will move in the opposite direction.

● Quick Boom: Bombs will take a shorter amount of time to explode.

● Slow Boom: Bombs will take a longer amount of time to explode.

 

The following "Shaker Items" have effects that are triggered by shaking the Wii Remote up and down. When using a Nintendo GameCube Controller, Shaker Item effects must be triggered by alternately pressing the L Button and R Button.
Bomb Change
Shake the Wii Remote for a certain amount of time to transform into a bomb. Press to return to normal.
Rocket
Shake the Wii Remote for a certain amount of time to fly up in the sky, avoiding bomb blasts, etc.
Shield
Shake the Wii Remote to raise a shield in the direction you're facing. Bomb blasts etc. can be blocked by the shield.
