Wi-Fi Battle
Select "Wi-Fi Battle" to battle with friends from far away. You can also register and confirm your friend code here, which is needed to battle with friends.

 

Menus
Wi-Fi Battle Connect to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection to start "Wi-Fi Battle." Up to 4 people can participate per Wii console.
Friend Setup Check your own friend code here, or add/delete codes from your friends to/from the Friend Roster.

* You'll need to acquire a friend code when selecting "Wi-Fi Battle" and connecting to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection for the first time.

 

Wi-Fi Menus
You can select these menus after connecting to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.
Friend Battle Battle with friends who you've exchanged friend codes with.
Ranked Battle Compete for the highest rank by acquiring points during battle. Since the "Score" battle type is used for Ranked Battle, you will earn points during gameplay. Points earned in Ranked Battle are called "Bomber Grades," which players compete for.
Bomber Grade Examples
WON ROUND +50
BOMBED OTHERS +20
BOMBED BY OTHERS -20
SUICIDE -20
Three Points to Note Before Playing

● When everyone selects "Multi Entry" for a Ranked Battle in Wi-Fi Battle mode, 1 to 4 players can participate on a single console, but only the person using a Player 1 Wii Remote will acquire Bomber Grades.

● If you turn off the power during battle, or for some reason are disconnected 3 times in a row, you will lose 1000 Bomber Grades as a penalty, so be careful.

● Please note that ranking may be reset without notice due to server-related problems.
Update Leaderboard Send Ranked Battle results to update the leaderboard.

* You will not be able to update in some situations.

* Any rank lower than 9999 will not be displayed on the leaderboard.
