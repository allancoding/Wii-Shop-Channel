Stages
There are 10 stage types in total. Select "Random" for a stage to be automatically selected.

 

● Classic
The most basic of the stages.

● Open Field
There are no Hard Blocks used in this stage.

● Power Freaks
Each player starts out with quick movement and powerful bomb blasts in this stage. Bomb Kick and Punch actions can also be used.

● UFO Mania
Get abducted by a UFO and throw bombs from above.

● Potholes
This stage is full of potholes, but bomb blasts can travel through them.

● Hyper Feet
This stage starts you off with maxed out movement speed.

● Bomb Bowling
Kicked bombs will travel in the direction of arrows in this stage.

● Item Grab
Items are available for you to grab from the start.

● Volcanoes
Setting a bomb where smoke billows out will change it into a Dangerous Bomb with a super-high blast range.

● Zombies
Zombie hands come up from the ground in this stage. If caught, you will lose your items and be paralyzed for a certain amount of time.

Map Sizes

Press up or down to select a map size. There are 2 sizes to choose from--Normal Map or Large Map.
