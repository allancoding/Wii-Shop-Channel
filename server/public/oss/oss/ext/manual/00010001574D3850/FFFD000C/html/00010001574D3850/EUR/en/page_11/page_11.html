About Wi-Fi Battle
Wi-Fi Battle lets you battle with friends from far away via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.

What is Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection?

Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection is an easy, safe and free way to play games with people worldwide via the Internet brought to you by Nintendo. It's the first system of its kind. Use your family's wireless or wired LAN connection to battle, go on an adventure, or learn about other cultures with users from all over the world.

 

Respect Others When Playing Wi-Fi Battle

● During battle, we ask that you do not shut off the Wii console, intentionally disconnect, or cause trouble for other players when connected to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.

● Nicknames containing vulgarities or other words offensive to the general public may result in denial of Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection use.
Nicknames can be changed at User Information.

 

Things to Note for Wi-Fi Battle

● If multiple consoles are connected to a single wireless access point, it may result in a slow or unstable connection.

● When playing Wi-Fi Battle via Internet connection, problems such as connection lag, walking over bombs, or other unusual effects may occur on occasion. These are only display problems, and there is nothing wrong with the game itself when they occur.

● Up to 8 people can play Wi-Fi Battle at once when connected to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. No more than 4 players can participate per Wii console, and no COM players are used.

 * This software can also be used with "Bomberman" for Wi-Fi Battle, which has not yet been released.

 * "Bomberman" for Wii is compatible with WiiWare Bomberman Blast in terms of Wi-Fi Battles. Please note that currently (Autumn 2008), "Bomberman" for Wii is only available in certain countries.
The "Bomberman" for Wii has more Action Poses available, so you may find some which cannot be used with the WiiWare version.
