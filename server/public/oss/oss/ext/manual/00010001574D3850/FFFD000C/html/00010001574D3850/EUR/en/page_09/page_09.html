Screens
This screen shows a battle in progress.
 Minutes
  Time remaining in the battle.

 Wins
  Total wins are displayed for "Trophies" mode, and total rank is displayed for "Score" mode.

 Bomberman
  This is the player character.

 Revenge
  When Revenge is set to ON, you can still throw bombs into the battlefield after getting blown up. If you hit someone, you will be brought back to life. Use to move and to throw bombs. Hold down while using to move faster. Releasing after holding it down for awhile will make you throw a bomb farther than usual.

 Bomb
  This bomb has been set and will explode once a certain amount of time has passed.

 Bomb Blast
  The flames of an exploded bomb. Anyone who gets hit by a bomb blast will die.

 Hard Block
  This type of block cannot be destroyed by a bomb blast.

 Soft Block
  This type of block can be destroyed by a bomb blast. Once destroyed, an item will sometimes appear.

 Items
  Pick up an item for one of various effects.

Normal Battle Pause Menu

The following options are displayed when pressing during gameplay.

RESUME GAME
Return to the game.
BACK TO RULES SCREEN
Return to the Rules screen.

Quick Finish

Press when only COM characters are left in battle for the Quick Finish option. This option allows you to skip to the end of the round and immediately determines the winner for that round.
