Tips

Don't miss a checkpoint!
Checkpoints are highlighted in green on your map. Try to hit them all! You will lose a life if you miss a checkpoint.


Boost!
You can use your Booster to reach tricky checkpoints.
Press to boost. The number of boosts is limited, so remember to collect additional Boost Power-Ups as you drop.

Shoot or be shot
Take out enemy defenses before they have a chance to take you out! Press to fire. Priority targets appear in red; shoot them quickly to destroy any force fields in your way.


Force Field
Force Fields are highlighted in red, and if you don't turn them off, they will kill you. You have to destroy the Heavy Fighter to turn the field off!


Speed control
Use the airbrake to slow down and control your falling speed. Press to activate the airbrake. Press again to deactivate it.

Avoid a rough landing!
Use your airbrake to scrub off speed on your final approach to your drop zone. You'll be killed on landing if you don't use your airbrake.
