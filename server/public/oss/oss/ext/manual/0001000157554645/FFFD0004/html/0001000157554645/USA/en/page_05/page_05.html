Goals

The year is 2070. After depleting Earth’s natural resources, humanity has dispersed to the stars, leaving Earth powerless against invasion. Now aliens have deployed robotic armaments on Earth, and driven the few remaining inhabitants underground.

The humans have begun a rebellion, established the Return to Earth Defense Force, and created secret landing zones which cannot be detected by the robots. The alien defenses are too tight for spacecraft to penetrate, so the elite Drop Zone Team must skydive through, carrying a cargo of special weapons in backpacks.

As part of the Drop Zone Team, your job is to evade enemy attacks and obstacles, and shoot down their weapons while you navigate to your target landing zone. If you survive, a new mission awaits you on the next level!


Your goal is to use the perfect combination of maneuvering in the sky and around buildings and shooting to stay alive until you reach the jump target.

While you float down and maneuver to your jump target, you will be attacked by enemies, starting from the time you jump out into the sky.



Shoot at Power-Ups for Extra Shields, and Weapons Upgrades. You will be maneuvering around futuristic buildings, with fantastic twists and turns. Some are even suspended in the air.

Experience the thrill of jumping from the sky and flying around buildings, and the adrenaline rush of shooting at enemies to stay alive as you rush toward your jump target.
