Bonus Multipliers
Survival is only the first goal of Nonstop mode! The real game begins when you begin to focus your strategies on getting the high score.

On the left side of the screen are your Bonus Indicators. You can earn a temporary 3x score multiplier for achieving any of the following bonus conditions:

Pure Light: All three of your lights are in use at once, and no targets are incorrectly lit.

Big Mix: Four or more targets are solving at once.

White Plus: One white target and at least one other target are solving at once.

In addition to the multipliers you get from these bonuses, you can get additional Bonus Blend multipliers by activating two adjacent bonuses! You also get more points per beat when you are solving more than one target at a time, an important key to getting the most points from the targets you erase.
