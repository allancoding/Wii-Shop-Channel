Main Menu: To open the main menu, point at the gear icon in the upper right corner of the screen and press .
Strong Bad's Inventory

Strong Bad's inventory shows what Strong Bad is carrying. To use an item that's in the inventory, select it by pointing at it and pressing . Your Pointer will turn into that item. Then point at the area of the screen where you want to use it, and press . Typically, items can be used on people or on other items in the game world. When you move the item over an area where it can be used, it will get a little bigger, which is how you'll know you can use it. Don’t forget to try using items on Strong Bad himself!

The Map

The map shows the locations you can visit. New locations appear on the map throughout the game, so make sure to check it often! To go to a new place, point at the location you want to go and press .


Point at the arrows to the right and left of the map to shuffle through the other pages. Here you’ll see a chart that ranks Strong Bad’s awesomeness level (based on your achievements throughout the game) and a list of the collectables you’ve found scattered around the world. These items are not necessary to complete the game, but they can make it more fun, so keep an eye on these pages to make sure you’ve done everything there is to do!


To return to the game, point at Close underneath the map and press .

Photo Mode

Photo mode allows you to take pictures at almost any time during the game. In photo mode you can perform the following functions:

  • To take a picture, point at Snap Picture and press .
  • To view or delete your photos, point at View Photo and press .
  • To view help, point at Help and press .
  • To exit photo mode, point at Quit and press .

You can share these pictures with people on your Wii Friends Roster using Strong Bad's laptop.
