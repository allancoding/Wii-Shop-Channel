Introduction

Welcome to "Homestar Ruiner," the first game in the five-episode series, Strong Bad's Cool Game for Attractive People. As a cool and attractive person yourself, you are cordially invited to play. Congrats!



When the Free Country USA Race to the End of the Race comes to town, our hero Strong Bad (the self-proclaimed coolest person ever) sets out to dominate! His actions, however, land him an unwanted house guest: his dimwitted adversary, Homestar Runner. Only you can figure out how to return life in the House of Strong back to normal. Control Strong Bad as he goes about his routine of playing practical jokes on his friends, pummeling his enemies, and beating his highest high score in the latest and greatest Videlectrix arcade classic.
