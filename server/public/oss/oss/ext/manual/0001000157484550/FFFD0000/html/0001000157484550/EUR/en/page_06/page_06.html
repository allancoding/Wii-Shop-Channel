Story

The famous Heracles has to face a challenge like he has never faced before. The gods have decided to organise a chariot racing tournament. Our hero will have to beat his enemies in a race across mythological kingdoms and be the first to reach the top of Mount Olympus. You can play as Heracles and help him return the sacred trophy, or play as one of villains and claim the prize for yourself! Get your friends together and you can play at up to 4 players in championship, single race or battle mode!


Characters

Heracles

Our hero will have to defeat all his enemies to reach Mount Olympus. He has a well-balanced chariot.

Koboldo

He is a Kobold soldier in the Cerber army. In spite of its size, his chariot is pretty light and has excellent handling.

Snake

Snake is a vicious, double dealing lizard. He also works for Satyre. His chariot has a good top-speed. 

Beesy

Beesy is brutal and strict. She is one of the most loyal soldiers the Minotaur has. Her chariot is sturdy and accelerates very well.

Leares

Leares is a Dryad. She is at the service of Poseidon. Her chariot has a good top speed.

Satyre

Dionysus’ “slave”, Satyre is half-human half-goat. Satyre likes to trap his enemies and his chariot is powerful and well balanced.

Medusa

Medusa is an eccentric and wild woman. Her chariot has a very good acceleration.

Minotaur

Minotaur is a violent character who is impulsive and very expressive. His chariot is easy to handle but very slow. A dangerous combination!

Poseidon

The master of the sea, he is the big boss. He is a despot and Heracles will have to be wary.
