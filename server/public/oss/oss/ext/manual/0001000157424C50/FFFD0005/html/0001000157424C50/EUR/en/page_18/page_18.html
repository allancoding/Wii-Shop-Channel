Add-on Content
Here is the add-on content that can be purchased at the Shop. Visit the Wii Shop Channel or the Nintendo website for instructions regarding the purchase of Wii Points.
Expert Mode 1
Available: 10/04/2009
200 Wii Points
Blocks Required: 1

These levels are more difficult than those in the regular game. We recommend these for players who want to further master the game and have a fun, nerve-wracking experience. Enemies move faster, and the key is not to be overwhelmed by their pace. This demands techniques like striking with rapid counterattacks and dodging enemies. You will also find a new, more powerful boss waiting for you at the end! This pack includes a total of 50 rounds and supports simultaneous play for up to 4 players.
Expert Mode 2
Available: 10/04/2009
200 Wii Points
Blocks Required: 1

The levels you will find here are even harder than those in Expert Mode 1! Strictly for those who want heart-pounding levels to train their Bubble Bobble skills to the ultimate level. In these stages air currents are fast, and there are many enemies with projectiles. The key is to judge their movement carefully. You will need to become even better at avoiding enemies and counterattacking quickly. You will also find a powerful (in certain sense of the word, ha ha), unconventional boss waiting for you at the end. Consider yourself warned! This pack includes a total of 50 rounds and supports simultaneous play for up to 4 players.
