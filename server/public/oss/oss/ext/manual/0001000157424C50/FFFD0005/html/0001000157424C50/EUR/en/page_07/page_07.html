Game Overview
BUBBLE BOBBLE Plus! is an action game where players take control of Bub and Bob, two kids turned into bubble-blowing dinosaurs. Their objective is to trap enemies in bubbles and then pop them.

In addition to the game's Standard Mode (100 Normal Rounds/100 Super Rounds), a port of the classic arcade game, players will also find many other modes. Arrange Mode (100 Normal Rounds/100 Super Rounds), new for this version. Ranking Mode, where players compete for the quickest clear time, highest score, and least bubbles used. And Ranking Mode, where players can register their own results and view the results of players from around the world via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.

In Arrange Mode, up to 4 players can play simultaneously with the addition of Pab and Peb, Bub and Bob's girlfriends, who have also been turned into bubble-blowing dinosaurs.


Players can also purchase Expert Mode 1 (50 rounds) and Expert Mode 2 (50 rounds) from the game's shop, featuring higher difficulty and a new boss character, Grumple Grommit.

Arrange Mode also features a new boss character awaiting all challengers!
Defeating Enemies & Clearing Rounds
You defeat enemies by blowing bubbles at them. If the bubble hits them they will become trapped in it, and then you can defeat them by breaking the bubble. Defeated enemies drop food.
Defeat all enemies in a floor to clear the round and move on to the next stage.
Game Over & Continues
You lose a life if you touch objects such as enemies, spikes, balls of fire, bottles, or energy beams.

If you lose all your lives, the game is over and you will be taken to the continue screen.

If you want to continue playing, select "Yes". Your progress throughout the game is saved automatically.
