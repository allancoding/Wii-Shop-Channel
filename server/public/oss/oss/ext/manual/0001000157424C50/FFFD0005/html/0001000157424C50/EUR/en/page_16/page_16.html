Techniques
This game includes several techniques that are essential for clearing some of the game's more difficult rounds. Be sure to make full use of them during the game.
Bubble Jump
Bubbles will normally break if you land on them. However, they won't break if you jump up just before landing on them. This will allow you to reach higher floors, using your own bubbles as stepping stones.
Reverse Break
First stop as many bubbles as possible moving in your direction by touching them lightly with your head, then turn around to pop all of them at once with the spikes on your back.
Chain Break
Bubbles that touch one another will break in a chain. You can use this to break multiple enemies at once.
