Modes
The following modes can be selected from the Game Menu.
GAME START
Begin the game. Selecting this will bring up the following menu.
NEW GAME
Start a new game.
LOAD
Continue a previously saved game.
TIME ATTACK
Play each stage in Time Attack mode. Times will be recorded on the Ranking List.
ENDLESS ATTACK
Displayed after purchase from Downloadable Content. A stage that doesn't have an end. The challenge is to try and get as far as possible. Distances will be recorded on the Ranking List.
SPECIAL GAMES
Displayed after purchase from Downloadable Content. A variety of unique and fun-filled games. See the Downloadable Content page for more details.
CHALLENGES LIST
Rules for completing Challenges.
RANKINGS
View player rankings.
OPTIONS
Change the game's settings.
DOWNLOADABLE CONTENT
Purchase additional content for MEGA MAN 9.
