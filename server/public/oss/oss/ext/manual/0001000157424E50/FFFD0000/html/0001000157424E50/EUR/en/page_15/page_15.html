Playing with Friends

The village barber shop is a friendly place – the customers think nothing of people dropping in and helping with the styling. In fact, the more the merrier. Usually.

 

Up to four barbers can co-operate to cut a customer's hair, each with their own Wii Remote. Of course, “co-operation” can be quite tricky when there are four sets of tools all working at once, but the customers are quite understanding, providing the end results are up to scratch.

 

Barbers can come and go as they please, and each can select and use their own tools freely in the normal way. You can even co-operate when practising.

 

In co-operative mode, a slight aura appears around the tools when held in front of the mirror – white for the first Wii Remote, red for the second, blue for the third and yellow for the fourth. It might help you keep track of your tools when things get a bit frantic! 

 

To avoid confusing the customers, not to mention the barbers, only the first Wii Remote can operate the game menus, such as profile selection and when in the waiting room.
