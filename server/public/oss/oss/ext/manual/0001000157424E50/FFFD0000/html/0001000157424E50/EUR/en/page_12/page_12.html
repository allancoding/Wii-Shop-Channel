Collectables

From time to time you may be given souvenirs, such as professional awards, postcards, and even gifts. They will be automatically stored in your Bumper Book once you've picked them up from the waiting-room table, so you can feel smug about them later.

 

Postcards will be automatically posted to your Wii Message Board too, but the village postal service is somewhat understaffed, so don't expect same-day delivery!

 

If you give a haircut you particularly like, you can take a photograph of it using the camera and keep it in the Bumper Book. If something happens you haven't seen before, why not capture it on camera?

 

Photographs kept in the Bumper Book can be viewed and sent to the Wii Message Board by selecting the option POST A COPY TO Wii MESSAGE BOARD on their back. From there, you can send them on to your Wii Friends on other Wii consoles.
