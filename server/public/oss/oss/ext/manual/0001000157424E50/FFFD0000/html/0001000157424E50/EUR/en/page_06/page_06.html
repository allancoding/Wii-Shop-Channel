Setting Up Shop

The village has a new barber: you! Are you ready to try out your hair cutting skills?

 

To start the game, select a player profile and press . Up to three profiles can be active at a time.

 

The first time you select a profile, the only option is to START, but after you have played and saved data, you will have the option to PLAY or ERASE that profile.

 

Everytime you make a new profile, the game will guide you around your new barber's shop: your waiting room, your barber's chair, and your tools.
