How to Play

    Your goal is to advance as far into the game as possible and get the highest score. The more levels you complete and the more enemies you defeat – the greater your score will be. If you are accurate and defeat several enemies in a row, you will get bonus points for accuracy.


    Aim with the cursor and press to shoot. Some enemies need to be hit twice or more to defeat them.


    Watch out for the quantity of arrows you have in your quiver - you only have a limited number of them. Once you use them all, you have to reload by pressing .
Your playing field is not limited by what you see on the screen.
In order to progress to the next level, you need to survive until the time runs out. There are several types of enemies that can hurt you, and your life will decrease if you are hit. Your life bar is displayed in the top-left corner. If the bar is empty – it is a game over.


Hints:

  • Apart from the enemies, there are also friendly people in certain levels. Do not hit them.
  • You can get additional points by finding and shooting the hidden objects.
