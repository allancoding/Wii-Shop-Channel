Story

You have entered Stonekeep, the home of your ancestors. An evil force has entered the keep and driven out most of the former residents. Only a true descendent of the clans can solve the mystery of Stonekeep and give the restless souls of the dead peace at last.

 

 

You enter Stonekeep in the dungeons. To survive your journey you will need an ally to guide your path. You may choose Duhg (a dwarf), Pierre (an elf) or Skuzz (a Sharga).

 

You select your ally by navigating your character through one of the three available doors as you enter the game. Once you enter a door, there is no going back. Your ally has clues to the levels and storyline. Allies can also help you in battle, but be careful not to accidentally or intentionally attack an ally or the ally may turn on you!

Upon selecting your ally, he will teach you how to properly use your dirk. After you have proven your ability to use the dirk, you will enter the dungeon level. 

As you travel through the game you will have the opportunity to save your progress to the Wii Menu Data Management screen.

When there is not enough memory left in the Wii console you can choose to continue playing the game without saving. The game will still save your status temporarily but does not save the status to the Wii Menu Data Management screen. If you return to the Wii Menu or power off the Wii Console you will loose the temporary save data.
