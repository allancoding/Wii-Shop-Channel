Blackjack
Play Blackjack against a beautiful girl, and try to reach the money objective. When all she has left is her underwear, you win. After that you can continue playing with the girl to reach a high score if you want.

Blackjack game mode basic rules:
  • Get as close as possible to 21 without going over.

  • Face cards (J, Q, or K) have a value of 10.

  • Aces can have a value of 1 or 11.

  • The house will stand on any value between 17 and 21.

  • The player can choose to double a bet.

  • The bet values are $10, $20, or $40.



Hit:


Take another card.


Stand:


Stay with the cards you have.


Double:


After your first two cards, you can double your bet, but this means you agree to take only one last card.
