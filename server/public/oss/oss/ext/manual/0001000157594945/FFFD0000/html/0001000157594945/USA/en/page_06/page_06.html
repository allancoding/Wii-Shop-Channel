Objective
Help Vektor Escape!
To do this you must take control of the circuits and cells in the level from the CPU. Once you do so the exit will appear.

When the exit appears try to escape quickly as the enemies will be alerted to your presence!
 
Circuits and Cells
As you pass over each white circuit line it will change to your color.

When you completely border a cell you can claim it.
Note: Each time you detonate, any fully bordered cells will become claimed (note any cells which are already in your possession will not be claimed again).

If you cannot, or choose not to, detonate you will automatically claim cells once you have bordered all the visible cells.
Note 2: Some levels have invisible cells. Finding them will increase your score and could lead to new areas!
Note 3: The more cells you claim at the same time the higher your score!
 
Exits

Once you have claimed all (or sometimes most) of the visible cells in a level the exit will appear. To complete the level you must enter the exit.
Note: Some levels have more than one exit. Different exits will lead to new levels or worlds!
Note 2: When the exit appears all the enemies in the level will become permanently alerted to your presence. Can you make it to the exit before they catch you!?
 
Badges and Medals

Each level has four medals to collect: Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum. You will need to work hard to obtain all the Platinum medals!
 
Badges are unlocked by achieving impressive feats. For example unlocking the entrance to the next world, defeating a certain number of enemies at once or filling multiple cells at once. Can you unlock them all?
