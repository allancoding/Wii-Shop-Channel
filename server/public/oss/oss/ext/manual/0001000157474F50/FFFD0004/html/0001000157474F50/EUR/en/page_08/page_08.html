How to Play
The goal of each level is to get as many Goo Balls as possible into the Pipe.

Grab and Drop the Goo Balls to build towers, bridges, zeppelins, giant tongues, and other lovingly assembled structures.

Be careful! Manipulating Goo can become messy if you have not built a solid foundation of goo and learning.

To Grab a Goo Ball, point the cursor at your Goo Ball of choice, and Grab with . Move the Goo Ball wherever you like, and let go.

To connect the Goo Ball to the structure, Grab and move a Goo Ball near the structure, and let go. The Goo Ball will automatically latch onto the others. In this way, you can build anything.

Remember, this is an entire world. Experimentation and discovery are a part of the game. You can't hurt the game or the world, so try anything you like.

Have fun, valued friend!
