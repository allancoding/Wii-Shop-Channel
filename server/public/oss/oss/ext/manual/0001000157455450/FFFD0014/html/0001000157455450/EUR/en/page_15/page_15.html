Types of Spaces

Normal spaces

Here’s a selection of spaces that will appear on the map.

 

Receive one card chosen at random.

Takes away one card from your hand.

Receive one coin.
Takes away one coin from your total.

All players receive a number of coins equal to their rank.

Allows you to spend coins to buy cards.

Note: The Buy Cards space allows you to trade in a number of coins for that number of randomly-selected cards.

 

Press left and right on  to select the number of coins to spend. (You can hold a maximum of five cards in your hand.)

 

Special spaces

Some maps also contain certain special spaces.

 

Balloon Ride

If you land on one of these spaces while the balloon is stopped there, you will be transported to another Balloon Ride space on the map.

 

Gate and Gate Key

There’s a circuit in front of the gate that you must travel along until you or someone else opens the gate. To open the gate, land on the Gate Key space.

 

In the circuit, you can only open a space by stopping on it.

Landing on the Gate Key space opens the gate, allowing you to advance.

The Magic Tome

Each map includes Magic Tome spaces. Stopping on these spaces will open up the Magic Tome, and the page it opens to will trigger a variety of special effects.

 

Note: Very occasionally, the effect triggered by a page might change.
