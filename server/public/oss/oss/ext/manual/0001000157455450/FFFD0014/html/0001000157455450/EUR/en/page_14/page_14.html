Types of Cards

Step Cards

Step Cards are blue cards that allow you to advance through spaces on the board. Using a Step Card will end your turn.

 

Examples of Step Cards


Move 5

Advance the number of spaces written on the card.


Quick Step

Advance a number of spaces equal to your current rank.


Move 2+★

Move the number of steps written on the card, plus the number of coins you use. Press left and right on to choose the number of coins you’d like to use, then press to confirm.

 

Action Cards

Action cards come in colours other than blue. You can use as many Action Cards as you like during a turn.

 

Examples of Action Cards


Filthy Lucre

Receive the specified number of coins.


Push ★

Advance another player a number of spaces equal to the amount of coins you use.


Card Trick

Destroy any player’s card.


Pull -4

Move the selected player back the number of spaces written on the card.

 

The difference between cards marked “any other player” and cards marked “any player”
Some cards can only be used on other players (“any other player”) and some can be used on all players including yourself (“any player”). Depending on the situation, it might be to your advantage to use certain “any player” Action Cards on yourself!
