Deciding the Result

Clearing the map and deciding the result

The map is cleared when someone lands on the goal space (Free-for-all Mode) or both members of a team land on the goal (Team Mode). Once the map has been cleared, the Results Screen will display and the final ranking will be decided.

 

The Results Screen

Points are added up on the Results Screen. Each player earns points based on their rank when the map was cleared and by trading in coins, and their point total decides the final ranking.

 

Earning rank-based points

You earn points based on your rank when the map is cleared. The player who reached the goal is considered first, with the ranks descending in order of distance from the goal.

Coin Bonus

Next, you’ll receive points for your coin total after receiving points for your rank. Once the coin trade-in rate is decided, your current coin total will be exchanged for points – as long as the trade-in rate isn’t 0!

 

The player in first place chooses one card to decide the trade-in rate for coins to points.

Each player’s coins are multiplied by the number on the chosen card and turned into points.

To the next map!
