About PictureBook Games: A Pop-Up Adventure

PictureBook Games: A Pop-Up Adventure is a board game set in a world of pop-up books. Unlike many board games, you use cards instead of dice to advance across the board. Master the use of the different effects of the cards and head for the goal!

 

Use coins to make tactical moves

If you’ve got coins, you can increase the number of spaces you or other players move when you use certain cards. You can also use coins to buy cards when you land on certain spaces!

 

What mysteries lie in the Magic Tome?

There’s also a type of space called the Magic Tome. Land on this space and the Magic Tome will open! Each page of the Magic Tome has a different effect – on which page will it open?

 

Turn the tables by collecting coins!

Each player will receive points depending on their rank after the map is cleared. Next, your current coin total is exchanged for additional points on the Coin Trade-In Screen, and the total amount of points you get decides your final rank.

Even if you didn’t reach the goal first, you can still turn things around by collecting lots of coins!
