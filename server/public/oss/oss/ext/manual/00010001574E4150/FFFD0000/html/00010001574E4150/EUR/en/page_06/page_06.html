Main screen


Story mode
Your task here is to battle your way through the six minigames. Complete the tasks at a particular level in order to access a new level.

Training mode
In training mode you can play all mini games, so as to improve your score there. Your results achieved in training mode do not contribute to your high score.

Settings
You can adjust the settings for the game here. For example you can turn off sounds or decide which language you would like to use.

High score
At this screen you can review your best results from story mode for each minigame played.
