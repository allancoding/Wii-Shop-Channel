Stunts

If your Hot Rod is in the air, you can perform a stunt. Doing so will add boost to your meter. Try to combo stunts to increase the amount of boost you gain:

A word of warning: don’t get greedy! If you try too many stunts and hit the ground while stunting, your Hot Rod will crash and you’ll lose all of your boost, Speed Stars, and precious time.

 

Ramps

Ramps are the bread and butter of the race. Hit one to get air so you can perform stunts and build boost! To get maximum air, boost into the ramp and bunny hop just as you hit the ramp. Do it right and you will get a SUPER JUMP! This takes some skill and good timing, but it's the best way to hit Boost Rings and Speed Stars.

 

Bunny Hops

Pressing the Bunny Hop button will jump your Hot Rod in the air. You can do this to avoid mud hazards on the track or get extra air off of ramps!

 

Powerslide

Holding the Powerslide button will break your Hot Rod into a sideways slide. Doing so will give you less control of your Hot Rod, but allow you to maintain your speed through a turn as well as making any Boost in your Boost Meter last longer. A key to winning races is figuring out when (and when not) to use your Powerslide. Learn this and before long you’ll be waving goodbye to the competition.
