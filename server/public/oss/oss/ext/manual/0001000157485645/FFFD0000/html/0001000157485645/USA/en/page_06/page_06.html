Controller Choices

From the Press the A Button screen, you can press the A Button on any of the supported game controllers. The first player to press the A button at this screen is designated as "Player 1."


High Voltage Hot Rod Show supports multiple controllers and controller styles, so choose the one you like best:

  • Wii Remote (held horizontally)
  • Wii Wheel Style
  • Nunchuk Style
  • Classic Style
  • Nintendo GameCube Controller
  • WaveBird

You can change your control scheme at any time before the race starts. Once a race starts, however, you are not allowed to change your controller.
