Pause Menu

Press or while playing to display the pause menu. Select a tab on the pause-menu screen to switch between the three sections of the pause menu: Snowpack Park Map, Headwear, and Help. Press or again, or select in the top-right corner of the pause-menu screen to close the pause menu.

*If you display the pause menu by pressing , the headwear page will display. If you display the pause menu by pressing , the Snowpack Park map will display.

Snowpack Park Map

This page displays a map of the park. Point to an icon to display the names of minigames and penguin-gathering locations, as well as other points of interest throughout the park.

*If is displayed when you point to a balloon icon (), it indicates that there are penguins to be found at the destination for those balloons.

Headwear

Throughout the game, you will be able to collect various hats and masks for the penguins to wear. On the headwear menu, the headwear pieces you have collected up to that point will be displayed.

You can change pages by pointing to or and pressing or by pressing to the left or right.

 ・Putting Headwear on a Penguin

If you select a piece of headwear from the headwear menu, you will be holding it when you exit the menu. Stand next to a penguin while holding the headwear and press to put the headwear on the penguin’s head. You can remove the headwear by standing next to the penguin and pressing .
