Snowpack Park Activities

There are lots of activities available in the park. As you progress through the game and meet certain conditions, the number of things you can do and places you can visit will increase.

● Fishing for Chowfish

Find good spots to fish for Chowfish using your fish sticks (→P.14).

● Researching Penguin Information

If you hoist up a penguin and stand on top of the four machines next to the tent or set the penguin down on the machines, you will be able to learn the penguin's weight, height, gender, and name. Once you gather all four information types, a crown will appear on the penguin's mood icon. To view a penguin's information at any time, stand next to it, then press and hold .

Changing the Penguin’s Name

When a penguin is on top of the machine that tells you its name, point at the penguin and press . A screen will appear that allows you to change the penguin's name.

● Searching for Penguins

When you spend friendship points to use balloons like the ones shown in the picture, you will travel to different islands and icebergs. When you arrive at each location, you will be able to collect penguins (→P.13) and bring them back to Snowpack Park. There are various places to visit, and the amount of friendship points required will vary depending on the location.

*A penguin icon on a set of balloons indicates that there are penguins to be found at the destination for those balloons.

Penguin Departure

If a penguin's mood becomes too bad, or if it falls off of the edge and into the ocean, it may leave Snowpack Park and go to one of the penguin-gathering locations. If you can find it and catch it again, you can bring it back to the park.

● Playing Minigames
There are lots of minigames you can play with the penguins, some out in the open and some inside igloos throughout the park. Playing games is a good way to interact with the penguins and earn friendship points. Instructions for games in the park are available on bulletin boards next to each game. Instructions for games inside the igloos will appear when you enter the igloo to begin playing.

 ・Minigames in the Park

There are various games you can play at any time around the park. When you come close to a game location, select "START" to begin the game. However, you may need specific items and a certain number of penguins with you in order to play. The bulletin board next to the game will provide more details.

 ・Minigames in the Igloos

There are several minigames you can play by entering igloos located throughout the park. However, you will need to find keys in order to unlock the igloos.

*One of the igloos contains Flip-a-Penguin, which can be played with two players (→P.17).

Quitting a Minigame Midgame

If you leave a gaming area or press to exit a game before finishing, or are no longer able to fulfill the conditions necessary to play, you will not obtain any friendship points for playing that game.

● Playing Whirl to Win

You can use friendship points to play Whirl to Win at the table next to the tent. A winning whirl will get you various items.

● Taking Photos

You can take pictures of yourself and the penguins in photo spots around the park. Stand behind the board and press to take a photo. You can add comments to the photo, then save it on the picture board in the tent (→P.16).

*You can save only one picture in each spot on the picture board.

● Fishing

If you walk around to the side of the log facing the ocean, you can sit down and start fishing. Swing the Wii Remote controller down to cast your line. When something is caught, the float will disappear under the water. Depending on its settings, the Wii Remote may vibrate or a sound may come from the speaker at this time. Swing the Wii Remote up to pull your line out of the water.
