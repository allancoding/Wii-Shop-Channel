Penguin Moods

There are five different penguin moods. The behavior of a penguin will change according to its mood.

● Checking a Penguin's Mood

When you point at a penguin, its mood will be displayed.

Loyal

This is the best penguin mood. When you approach the penguin, it will follow you willingly.
Cheerful

This is a good penguin mood. When you approach the penguin, it will follow you curiously for a while.
Hesitant

This is an average penguin mood. When you approach the penguin, it will become nervous.
Cranky

This is a bad penguin mood. When you approach the penguin, it will be combative.
Skittish

This is a terrible penguin mood. When you approach the penguin, it will run away.

● Mood Changes

Interactions such as feeding, petting, and playing games with the penguins will improve their moods. However, if you do not interact with them for long periods of time, the moods of the penguins will worsen. If a penguin's mood becomes too bad, it may leave Snowpack Park (→P.12).

Friendship Points

When you interact with the penguins, you earn friendship points (fp). You collect earned friendship points when the in-game clock completes a daily cycle. You can end the day immediately by going to sleep on the cot inside the tent. Friendship points can be used to visit other areas to search for more penguins or to play Whirl to Win.
