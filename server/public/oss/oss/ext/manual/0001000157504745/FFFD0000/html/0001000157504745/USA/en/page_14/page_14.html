Using Items

To use an item, point at the item icon in the lower-left corner of the screen and press . The items you will be able to use are listed below.

*Put away items by pressing .

Fish Sticks

The fish sticks will tell you where Chowfish are hiding under the ice and snow. If you move while holding the fish sticks, they will open when you find a good spot to fish.

 ・Fishing for Chowfish

When the fish sticks open, press to drill a hole and begin fishing. When a Chowfish is caught, the fishing pole will move. Depending on its settings, the Wii Remote may vibrate or a sound may come from the speaker at this time. If you swing the Wii Remote up at the right time, you will pull back your pole, along with all of the Chowfish you have caught. Walk over the Chowfish to collect them.

Blowfish

This is a little round fish that becomes a snowball when you toss it by pressing .

 ・Rolling a Snowball

When you toss a blowfish, it will roll up into a snowball. If you get next to the snowball and hold , you can grab onto it and move it around by pressing . The farther you roll the snowball, the bigger it will become.

*Snowballs will be destroyed when they hit an obstacle.

*You can have up to three snowballs rolled up at the same time.

Golden Penguin

This is a golden penguin statue. After selecting it with the pointer, press to set it down. Penguins in the area will gather around the golden penguin and won't move until the statue is removed.

*To remove a golden penguin, stand next to it and press to pick it up, then press to put it away.

Tuna

Tuna is a treat for the penguins. When they see one, they will rush toward it and eat it up!
