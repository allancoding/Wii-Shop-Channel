Interacting with Penguins

You can interact with the penguins of Snowpack Park in a variety of ways. By feeding, petting, and greeting them, the moods of the penguins will improve, and you will earn friendship points.

● Holding the Penguins

Get next to a penguin, then press and hold . Once you're holding the penguin, slide it around by pushing .

 ・Hoist up the Penguin

Swing the Wii Remote up while holding the penguin to hoist up the penguin. You will not need to keep holding at this time. If you push while the penguin is hoisted up, you will move while holding the penguin over your head. Press to set down the penguin. Swing the Wii Remote down to toss the penguin gently.

● Feeding the Penguins

Press to throw a Chowfish in the direction of the pointer. A penguin's mood may improve when it eats a Chowfish.

● Petting the Penguins
Get next to a penguin, then press and hold to hold it in place. While holding the penguin, use the Wii Remote to pet it with the cursor. If you are doing a good job, the blue line around the penguin will turn pink, and the mood of the penguin may improve.

*Pressing and holding while standing next to a penguin will also allow you to view any information you have gathered for that penguin (→P.12).

● Greeting the Penguins

Sometimes, the penguins will stretch out their necks and greet you. If you press at that same moment, you can greet the penguins back. When you greet them successfully, the moods of the penguins may improve.
