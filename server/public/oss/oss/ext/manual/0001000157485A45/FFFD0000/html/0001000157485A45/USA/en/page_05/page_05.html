Introduction

The bridge’s serenity is broken by the Comms System alerting that a message has been received. The DST Horizon has been on standby for the better part of the last month and my team, the highly-trained Kraig’s Riders, are restless and ready to take on a new assignment.

 

The mixed feeling I get from both the source of the communiqué and it’s high-level encryption forces me to retire to my private quarters to give the message it’s due privacy.

 

--------------------------------------------

Defence Bureau

Galactic Confederation of Planets

Deep Space Special Operations Division
Comtrack # -Unavailable-

 

Kraig,

Old friend, the desk life is not easy for grunts like us. I come to you for help in getting something done in this endless sea of GalCon bureaucracy.

 

The Galactic Confederation Colony Program has developed Artificial Intelligence cores to automate colonial efforts in the Omega Nebula. The project was working better than expected as more than 20 colonies where pre-settled by automated terraforming systems. Obviously, you would not be reading this if everything was running smoothly.

 

Planet Zethos, located in NGC 6618, had an A.I. core, Thau-Phi 10-01, installed 12 solar cycles ago with a directive to focus all available resources on terraforming and settling of the surface. Apparently, the plastic mind has gone haywire, severing all communication and transforming workbots into a dangerous robotic army.

This new synthetic warlord has not made any demands, but the whole Orion Nebula sector is in jeopardy considering the amount of weaponry Thau-Phi could produce in just a couple of weeks. A GalCon Marine Force will be too late to stop it without suffering extremely heavy casualties. We need you and your team to sneak past the planetary barrage and strike the A.I at its core to take it out without blasting the entire colony.

 

Attached to this message, you can find a quote that I know you will find acceptable. The SOD will pay for all your operational expenses and supplies, including ammunition, so don’t hold back. My faith in you is absolute, do this not in the name of GalCon, but in the name of the lives of all the Marines you will be saving. If not then as a favor to me and all the blood we have shed together.

 

Forever your brother in arms,

Marshall Alistor Kain

--------------------------------------------

I close the message, print a copy and permit myself a thin smile. The team will be happy.

 

We ride once again.
