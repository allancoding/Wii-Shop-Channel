Introduction

● What Is ThruSpace?

This is an exciting puzzle game in which you'll move and rotate an object called a keydron through gaps in walls. As the game progresses, you'll face increasingly complex challenges for you to overcome.

 ・Slip the Keydron through Gaps to Get Points
You get points when you put your keydron through a gap. Also, when you pick up pairs of crystals or use special maneuvers to get through the gap—known as tricks (→P.13)—you'll get a higher score.

 ・Register Your Score and Challenge Yourself on the Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection (Nintendo WFC) Leaderboard
When you update your high score, you can connect to the Internet and register your score in the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Leaderboard (→P.14) to compare your score with other ThruSpace players.
