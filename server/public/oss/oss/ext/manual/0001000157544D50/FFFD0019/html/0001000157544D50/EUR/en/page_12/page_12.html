Records
When selecting "Records", there are three options to choose from: "Main Game", "Mini Games", and "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking".

● Main Game
Check your total score for Adventure Island.

● Mini Games
Check your scores for each of the mini games.

● NintendoWi-FiConnectionRanking
See how you stack up in Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking.

*Save data must be created to register with Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking.

Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking Registration
Records achieved will first be recorded to "Local Ranking" in the Wii system memory. You can then select whether or not those records are registered to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking.
In order to register to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking, select "Entry" while viewing your "Main Game" or "Mini Games" records.
Viewing Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking
Records registered to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking can be viewed by selecting "Nintendo
Wi-Fi Connection Ranking".
When selecting the type of ranking you want to view from "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Ranking", the top 30 will be displayed for each category, along with your own standing. Select "Refresh" here in order to update the ranking category currently being viewed.

*Ranking updates may take time, or cannot be performed in some situations.

Ranking Information

● Hudson WiiWare Portal Website
Ranking/leaderboard information including your game registration name and territory for ADVENTURE ISLAND The Beginning, may be publicly displayed on the Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.

● Operation Policy
Operations may be altered depending on frequency of use. If any changes occur, details will be posted on the Hudson WiiWare Portal Website.
