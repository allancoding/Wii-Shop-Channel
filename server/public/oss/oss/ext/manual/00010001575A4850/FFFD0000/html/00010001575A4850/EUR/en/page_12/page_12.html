Animal Growth


After raising a young animal for a certain amount of days, it will temporarily be relocated to another Animal Center.
Eventually the animal will be returned to your area as an adult.
Personality

After a certain amount of days have passed, your animal's personality will be affected by various actions taken during that time, such as how often you fed, pet, or called it over.
Personality effects how rapidly its Stomach and Energy parameters decrease, and also influences its actions.
●Wild
The animal acts as it pleases.
Stomach and Energy parameters decrease at a
normal rate.


●Naughty
The animal becomes a lot more active.
Since it will run around quite a bit, its Energy will decrease at a faster rate.


●Obedient
The animal moves around on occasion but also relaxes. Energy increases at a higher rate when playing with it.


●Glutton
Doesn't move much, but gets hungry a lot faster, causing Stomach to decrease at a faster rate.


●Bratty
The animal becomes a lot more active.
Both Stomach and Energy parameters increase/decrease at a slightly faster rate.


●Easygoing
Spends a lot of its time laying around.
Stomach and Energy parameters decrease at a slower rate.


●Gourmet
The animal becomes a lot less active.
Its Stomach parameter decreases at an even faster rate than with Glutton.
Both Stomach and Energy parameters increase/decrease at a slightly faster rate.


●Strong-willed
The animal becomes a lot more active.
Its Stomach and Energy parameters increase/decrease at a normal rate.


●Spoiled
The animal acts as it pleases.
Since it always wants to play in this case, its Energy parameter will increase/decrease at a faster rate.
