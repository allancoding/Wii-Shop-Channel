Email

A big part of being awesome Strong Bad means checking his email on the Lappy 486. Sometimes emails are related to the game and set up the story. Some emails are just for fun.

Checking Email

To check email, point at the Lappy 486 and press . Looking at Lappy, point at email inbox and press . Select an email from the list by pointing at it and pressing . After you read an email, you can Delete it, or just go Back to Inbox.

Sending Email

Point at Compose Email and press . A keyboard will appear on screen. Use this to compose your email. When you're ready to send your email, point at Send and press . (You can also Clear your email to start over, or go Back to Main Menu without sending an email.)

You can now Send the email, Add Photo, Edit the email, or go Back to Main Menu without sending your email. Point at the option you want and press .

When you select Send, your Wii Friends list will display. Select the friends you want to send the email to from the list.
