Characters

It won't take very long for you to discover that Mimiga Island is host to many unique inhabitants. Some characters are instantly friendly and others, well, not so nice.


The majority of the island's denizens will offer advice, cryptic hints or even ask for a favor, usually for something in return. A few new faces, two in particular, have no intention of letting you stop the events that have already been put in motion.


Protecting your new friends won't be easy and you can't do it alone. Along the way you're going to need some help. Be sure to speak with everyone you meet. They might have something useful to say or even give you an item or weapon. Here are some of the more notable characters found in the journey.

Mister Traveler
The main protagonist doesn't remember his name or where he came from. In his journey he'll learn about his past and his original purpose. Do everything right and, maybe, he'll save his Mimiga friends!

King
Call him number one in Mimiga Village, a responsibility passed onto him by the previous leader, Arthur. King's duty is to the Mimiga who live in the village. He's not the friendliest Mimiga you'll meet, but he does have good intentions.

Toroko
What Toroko lacks in size, she makes up for in heart and charm. Easy to make friends, Toroko warmly welcomes you to the island when King and Jack do nothing but question your motives.

Jack
He's happy to be number-two to King's number-one, that's Jack. Loyal to the end, he has King covered under any circumstance.

Misery
Using her nearly limitless magic, Misery is greatly responsible for the ongoing turmoil. For whatever reason it may be, she is in search of someone named Sue.
