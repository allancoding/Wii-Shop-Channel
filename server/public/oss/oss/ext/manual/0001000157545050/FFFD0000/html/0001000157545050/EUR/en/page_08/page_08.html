The Game
Tetris Party is a puzzle game in which you try to eliminate blocks called Tetriminos (7 types) by forming uninterrupted lines on the playing field.

There are several rule variations for each mode that offer players a number of ways to earn tons of bonus points; such as erasing multiple lines at once, or erasing a consecutive number of lines. The object of the game is to effectively use these techniques to achieve high scores and beat your opponents.

What's more, in Tetris Party players can connect to the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection and battle players around the world, as well as compete for World Rankings.

Rankings are broken down into three areas:
World, Europe/Oceania, and Country.
