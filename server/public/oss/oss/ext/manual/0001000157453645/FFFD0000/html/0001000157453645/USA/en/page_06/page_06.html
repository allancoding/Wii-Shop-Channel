Introduction
My Dolphin is a therapeutic action game in which you can enjoy dolphin shows. You can also play with your dolphin and enter it in shows and championships.
●Show time
In Show time, you become your dolphin's trainer and perform in shows.
Try to earn a high score with technique points and artistic points!
By recording your show scores in the ranking system, you can compete with players from around the country.
●Championship
In the My Dolphin championships, you compete in dolphin shows.
Clear all four championships to become the best dolphin trainer in the world!
●Play time
In Play time, you can watch your dolphin as it swims around in the pool, or play with your dolphin and practice tricks together as its trainer.
