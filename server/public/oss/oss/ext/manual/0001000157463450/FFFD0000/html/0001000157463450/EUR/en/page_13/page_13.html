The Field Map

● Viewing the Field Map

Use to move your character around the field map. When there are two or more characters in your party, you can press to change the leader.

● Talk / Search

If you want to talk to someone or examine an object, go up to it and press .

● Save Points

You will find save points inside towns and dungeons. You can save your game (→ P. 11) and use tents or cottages to rest your party when standing on top of a save point.

 

● Inns

You can pay gil (money) to rest at inns, which will restore your HP and MP and cure any status ailments (→ P. 23) your party is suffering from. Staying at an inn also causes time to pass, resulting in a new moon phase (→ P. 21).

 

● Sundries / Weaponsmiths / Armourers

There are three types of shops in this game, each selling a different type of item. Select "Buy" or "Sell" to begin a transaction.

 

How to Shop

Select the item you wish to buy (or sell) and press to confirm your choice. Use to choose the quantity of items to buy (or sell)—press left or right to change the single digits and up or down to change the tens digit—then press again to confirm. ● Transportation

As you progress, you will acquire assorted modes of transportation to help you get around the field map:

 

Airship

Press to board or disembark.
Chocobo

Talk to a chocobo to ride it; press to dismount. The chocobo will run off the moment you dismount.
