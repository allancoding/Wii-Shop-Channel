The Menu Screen / Status

This lets you view your characters' statuses, equipment, and abilities. Select a character, then press left or right to switch between info screens. Hold down and press left or right to switch between characters.


Basic Stats

Lv

The character's current level.

 

HP

 

The character's current and maximum HP. This number goes down as damage is taken. The character will be KO'd when HP reaches 0.

MP

 

The character's current and maximum MP. Casting magic spells reduces this number.

EXP

 

The experience points the character has earned up to this point.

Next

 

When the character’s EXP reaches this number, the character will level up.

● Status

This lets you view each character's statistics.

 

Strength

Affects physical attacks.

Speed

Affects the speed of actions in battle.

Stamina

Affects total HP.

Intellect

Affects the strength of black and summon magic.

Spirit

Affects the strength of white magic.

Attack

 

The maximum number of times the character can hit with a physical attack, and the force of each attack.

Accuracy

 

The chance of the character hitting the target with an attack.

Defense

 

The number of times the character can dodge physical attacks (the higher this is, the fewer the times the enemy will land a hit with a physical attack) and the character's ability to withstand damage.

Evasion

 

The chances of the character evading physical attacks.

Magic Defense

 

The number of times the character can dodge magic attacks (the higher this is, the fewer the times the enemy will land a hit with a magic attack) and the character's ability to withstand magical damage.

Magic Evasion

 

The chances of the character evading magical attacks.

● Equipment

View the items the current character has equipped.

● Abilities

Abilities are battle commands unique to certain characters (→ P. 23). This screen shows the character's abilities and their effects.
