The Menu Screen / Navigation

Presson the field map to display the menu screen. Press to switch the information displayed on this screen.


Items
(→P.15)

Display and use items in your inventory.

Magic
(→P.16)

Check or use the magic you have learned.

Bands
(→P.17)

Check the Bands you have learned.

Equip
(→P.18)

Equip weapons and armour.

Status
(→P.19)

Display the status of your party's characters.

Party

Change the order of your party.

Formation

Move your characters to the front or back row.

Settings
(→P.20)

Change the game's options.

Quicksave
(→P.11)

Quicksave and return to the title screen.

Save
(→P.11)

Save your current progress.

● Party

Select "Party" to rearrange the characters in your party. First select a character, then choose the space or character to switch places with. Press to confirm your choice.

● Formation

This menu lets you define where each character stands in the party's formation. Characters in the back row lose attack power and accuracy on physical attacks (with some exceptions) but take less damage from enemies than party members in the front row.
