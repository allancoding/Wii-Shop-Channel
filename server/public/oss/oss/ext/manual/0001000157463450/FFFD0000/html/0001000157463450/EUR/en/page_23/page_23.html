The Battle Screen / Status Ailments

Your characters may be stricken with the status ailments outlined below after sustaining certain types of enemy attack. You'll want to cure them quickly through the use of items or magic. Some ailments, though not all, automatically go away once the battle ends.

 

Ailments that go away after battle

   Sleep

Fall asleep, becoming unable to act.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Alarm Clock (item)
   Paralyze

Become paralyzed and unable to act.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Unicorn Horn (item)
   Confuse

Attack both friend and foe at random; unable to choose your own battle commands.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), getting

attacked
   Curse

Cuts Attack and Defense in half.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Cross (item)

 

Ailments that remain in effect after battle

   Poison

Gradually reduces your HP.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Antidote (item)
   Darkness

Makes it more difficult to land physical attacks.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Eye Drops (item)
   Silence

Makes you unable to use magic.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Echo Herbs (item)
   Pig

The only magic a character with this status ailment can use is Pig.

Cure via: Pig (black magic) or Esuna (white magic), Diet Ration (item)
   Toad

The only magic a character with this status ailment can use is Toad.

Cure via: Toad (black magic) or Esuna (white magic), Maiden's Kiss (item)
   Mini

Reduces Attack and Defense.

Cure via: Mini or Esuna (white magic), Mallet (item)
   Petrify

You are turned into stone, making you unable to act.

Cure via: Esuna (white magic), Gold Needle (item)
   KO

Occurs when HP drops to 0.

Cure via: Raise (white magic), Phoenix Down (item)
