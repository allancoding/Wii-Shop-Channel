How To Start the Game

Welcome to SPOGS Racing™! In this game you can race souped-up SPOGS on straight race courses, complete jumps and loops on over-the-top stunt tracks, or simply crash into opponents to steal parts and upgrade your ride.


To get started, select the SPOGS Racing™ Channel Icon on the Wii Menu. This will bring you to the game's Main Menu.


Press Up or Down on the +Control Pad (holding the Wii Remote horizontally) to navigate through the menu. Press the 2 Button to select an option and the 1 Button to go back.


Use the menu to choose the type of race you want: Crash 'N Grab™, Season, 2 Player or a simple Quick Race. You can also change your character's image and color by selecting SPOG Maker or set game options such as SFX/music volume or control type by selecting Options.
