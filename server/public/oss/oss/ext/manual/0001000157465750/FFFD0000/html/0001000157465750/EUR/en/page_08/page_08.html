Navigating the World
When playing in Adventure Mode, Follie explores Elilia - the mysterious planet where she crash landed.

Move Follie to the highlighted location, by pointing the Wii Remote at the screen and pressing the A Button. If Follie cannot reach the location, the target cursor will turn red.

As she explores, Follie will come across different objects, characters and locations:
  • Flowerwork Seeds:
    These seeds were scattered during the crash landing. Collect these to plant Flowerwork flowers at planting sites, and gain access to a new level.
    When Follie completes a new level, she is awarded with additional seeds.

  • Planting Sites:
    Each of these glowing sites is the location of a new level.
    Before a level can be played for the first time, the player must possess the right seeds. Move over a planting site to find out which seeds are needed.
    Once a level has been completed, it can be replayed any number of times (no extra seeds are needed). A completed level awards the player with new seeds and a 1-5 star rating.

  • Pesky Gnomes:
    These pesky and unhelpful gnomes block Follie's progress when exploring.
    Follie uses her Star Power to pacify them, allowing Follie to pass. The value displayed on each gnome is the minimum Star Power Follie needs to pacify it.
    Move next to a gnome to start pacifying.

  • Hints:
    As Follie explores she will come across signs and hints. They provide strategies and tips for the game, and also reveal the history and secrets of Elilia.
    Experienced players can turn hints off from the options menu.

  • Locals:
    Follie occasionally comes across one of the local inhabitants of Elilia. They will offer Follie invaluable advice, and may sell her a new ability (in exchange for coins).
    Stand in front of a local to talk to them.

  • Coins:
    Collect the coins that are scattered around the world by walking over them.
    If you meet a local, they may offer to sell you an ability in exchange for coins.
