Growing Flowers
Each level begins with Follie planting Flowerwork seeds, and is completed when all the seeds have blossomed into flowers.

Move Follie by pointing the Wii Remote at the screen, and collect the floating pollen. Follie can initially hold up to 3 pollen, but only ever one color at a time.

Help a flower grow by feeding it matching colored pollen. Follie will automatically target the closest flower.

Launch fireworks by making a released pollen collect other pollen before it reaches the flower. The more pollen collected, the bigger the fireworks launched. Flowers will attract fireworks, which gives them a growth bonus.

Once a flower blooms, you have a limited time to get the remaining flowers blooming. Run out of time and the level will be lost.

If a flower is left unfed for too long, it will start to wilt. If a flower wilts completely or a seed shrinks too small, the level will be lost.
