Controls
Only the Wii Remote is required to play Flowerworks. The Nunchuk™ and Classic Controller™ are not supported.

Growing Flowers Controls
Move Follie around by pointing the Wii Remote at the screen. Follie will move towards where you are currently pointing. Collect a pollen by touching it. Press the A Button to release a pollen.
Hold the A Button to target the closest flower. While holding the A Button, Follie can move around the screen and will continue to lock onto this flower. Quickly release and press the A Button to launch a pollen, while keeping the flower lock active.
Press the B Button to switch target to the closest flower (this is only useful when still holding the A Button).
After you have gained the Follie Firework ability, press down on the +Control Pad to release a firework.
Press the - Button or the + Button to open the menu.

Exploring World Controls
Move Follie around by pointing the Wii Remote at the screen and pressing the A Button. Walk over coins or seeds to collect them.
Stand next to a gnome to pacify it. Stand in front of a local to talk.
The +Control Pad can also be used to move around.
Press - Button or the + Button to open the menu and cycle through the pages.
Shake the Wii Remote when indicated to pacify a gnome.
