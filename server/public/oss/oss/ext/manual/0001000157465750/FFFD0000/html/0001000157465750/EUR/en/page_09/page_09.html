Star Power
Follie's Star Power is a measure of her self-confidence. Follie is so upset with the style of her surroundings that it has made her upset and damaged her confidence.

As a level is completed, the bloomed Flowerworks have a permanent effect on their surroundings, giving them a magical makeover. Each time this happens, Follie's confidence increases and she feels more certain she can complete her adventure.

Each completed level awards Follie with a star ranking. Score more points to get a higher star ranking. Each level has a maximum star ranking of 5. Every new star earned goes towards Follie's Star Power ranking. This is visible at the top of the screen when exploring the world.

As Follie gains new abilities, you will be able to get higher scores and better star rankings. Replay a level as often as you like, and keep trying for those 5-star rankings!
