Hints and Strategies
Having difficulty getting that last star on a level? Unable to work out how to complete a level?

Here are some tips to get you started:
  • If you are having trouble with the basics, make sure you can complete the tutorial.
  • Be sure to read all the signs and hints you come across, they contain valuable tips!
  • Try to feed flowers evenly. Focus on a single flower for too long, and you might run out of time.
  • Make sure you feed the right colors to the right flowers. Pay attention to what each flower is saying.
  • Learn your color mixing rules. Did you know you can color mix in the sky?
  • Are you lost? Can't find a level or the right seeds? Take some time out for exploration, try following the paths.
  • Learn to use the map. Not only can you teleport to any level previously played, it will show you gnomes or levels not yet cleared.
  • Can't get a 5-star ranking on a level? Don't stress - leave them for later. As you collect more abilities, try replaying older levels. The results may surprise you!
  • Experts can focus on fireworks alone - get enough of them going, and you'll never worry about feeding flowers again.
  • Try releasing your pollen as far from the flowers as possible. The further away, the more chance of fireworks.
  • Just about everything earns you points, but Firework combos earn massive bonuses. Big combos can earn you a massive 500,000pts!
