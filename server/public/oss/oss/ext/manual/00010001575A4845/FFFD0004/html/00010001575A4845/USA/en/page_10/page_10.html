Time Elapse

Approximately 1 real-time hour is equal to 24 game hours.

Some animals are active during the day, and others at night.

Animal parameters decrease on a daily basis, even when the game is not on.

And even when you're not playing the game, messes will gradually build up in each area.


Screen Saver Function

Pressing will hide the icons, and when leaving the Wii Remote idle for a certain amount of time, game hours will stop elapsing, and you will be taken into Screen Saver mode.

Since game time stops in this case, you can enjoy watching your animals move about without worrying about status changes.
