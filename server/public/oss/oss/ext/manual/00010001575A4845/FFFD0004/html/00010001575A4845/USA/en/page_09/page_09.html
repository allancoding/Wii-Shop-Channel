Animal Center
　              


There are 5 menus to choose from at the Animal Center.

● Take Animal
  Take on a new animal to raise.
  Select an animal you'd like to raise and press  
  .
  After giving your new animal a name, you'll
  have to select an area to raise it in.

　　　　　　　

 
● Enter Area
　Move to a screen where breeding areas can be
   selected.
   Select the area of an animal you'd like to
   check up on, then press .

　　　　　　　


● Trophy Room
　Check the titles (trophies) you've earned for 
   raising up various animals.
   Earn all titles to become the ultimate "Animal
   Master."

　　　　　　　


● Release Animal
　Release an animal you are currently raising.
   Select an area holding the animal you want to
   release, then press .
   Once you've released an animal, you can
   never get it back.
  
　　　　　　　


● Animal Records
　View information for animals you have
   released.
