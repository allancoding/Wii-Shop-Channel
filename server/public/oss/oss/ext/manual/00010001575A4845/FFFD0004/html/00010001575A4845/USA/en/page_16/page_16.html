Add-on Content
　            



New animal types can be added to "My Zoo" by purchasing add-on content with Wii Points (sold separately).

To purchase add-on content, press at the title screen, then point at the "Add-on Content" display and press again.
Once you've been taken to the Add-on Content screen, select the new type of animal you'd like to purchase.

●Giraffe
   Release Date: 12/07/2009
   Wii Points: 100　Blocks Used: 9
   This herbivore can be found in the savannah,
   and is known for its incredibly long neck.
   It is the tallest land animal on Earth, reaching
   heights of more than 16 feet.

 

●White Rhinoceros
　 Release Date: 01/11/2010
   Wii Points: 100　Blocks Used: 9
   This herbivore is known for the large horn atop
   its head, and usually travels in herds.


●Koala
　Release Date: 02/08/2010
   Wii Points: 100　Blocks Used: 16
   This cuddly herbivore spends most of its life
   sleeping up in trees.

●Cheetah
　Release Date: 03/08/2010
   Wii Points: 100　Blocks Used: 11
   This sleek carnivore can be found in the
   savannah. It is the fastest land animal on
   Earth, able to run at speeds of more than 60
   miles per hour.


Add-on content cannot be used independently of "My Zoo".
