Joining a Conversation

To join a conversation already in progress, please follow the steps below.

Note: You will not be able to join a conversation if there are already four users participating.

1.
Making a Request to Join

Go to the Lobby and select the host of the conversation group you wish to join. The host will be shown with a yellow outline.

2.
Confirming a Request to Join

After selecting the host, a confirmation screen will be displayed. Select YES.

3.
Receiving a Request to Join

A confirmation screen will then be displayed on the hostâ€™s screen. The host can accept or decline your request by selecting YES or NO.

If the host of the conversation selects YES, all members will appear in the Conversation Room and the conversation will continue with its new participant.
