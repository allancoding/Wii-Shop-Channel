Friend Battle
Played with standard Tetris battle rules. You can compete with friends and other players around the world via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.
The standard rules are the same as those for "VS Battle". When there are several destinations for lines to be sent, the target will be randomly selected. In "World Battle", participating members are chosen at random from players worldwide.
In "Friend Battle", participants are players who have mutually registered Friend Codes.
When "Item" setting is ON, a maximum of 6 players can compete. If the setting is OFF, the game will be a two-player head-to-head competition.
While standing by for a game to start, pressing will activate "Start Now" status. When all participating players have activated "Start Now", the game will start immediately regardless of whether there is a full number of participants or not. (The computer will not fill in for the missing number of players).

In addition, you can send preset chat messages to all participating players by pressing .

Tournament
Allows you to participate in tournaments held for a limited period of time.

Chat Messages
Allows you to edit chat messages to be sent to battle opponents. You can select from a set of fixed messages. You can register up to a maximum of 4 messages.

Note about Registering Friends

When you register someone on your Friend Roster, profile information such as online status and ranking will remain accessible to that person until both respective Friend Codes are removed.
