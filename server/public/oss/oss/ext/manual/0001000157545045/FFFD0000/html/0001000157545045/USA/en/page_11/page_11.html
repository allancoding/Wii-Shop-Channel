Items
How To Use Items
Item Blocks (pictured on the right) will appear during a game when the Item setting is ON in "VS Battle", "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection" and other "Multi-Player" modes. Clear the line containing the Item Block to obtain the item.

Press to use an item you have obtained. You can stock up to a maximum of three items, using them in the order you obtained them. When you already have three items and pick up a fourth, the new item will replace the longest held item.

Item Types
There are 10 types of items. For items with the mark, holding the Wii Remote vertically and using the Pointer will enhance their effect. (These same items can be also used without using the Pointer).
Smoke [Effect: One Opponent]

Aim your Pointer at an opponent's Matrix and press to cover that Matrix in smoke. Shake the Wii Remote to get rid of the smoke.
Speed up [Effect: All Opponents]

Use this item to increase the drop speed of your opponents' Tetriminos for a short time.

Block Shot [Effect: Anyone]

Aim your Pointer at an empty area in any Matrix and press to send blocks to that area. The greater number of times you rapidly press , the more blocks you can send into that area.
Line Kick [Effect: Anyone]

Use this item to clear lines on your Matrix. To choose a line just point at it and activate the Line Kick.
Cascade [Effect: Self]

When this item is activated the Tetriminos you lock down will break apart and fill in the spaces beneath them for a short time.

Lock [Effect: All Opponents]

Use this item to stop all opponents' Tetriminos from rotating for a short time.

Field Climber [Effect: All Opponents]

Use this item to cause a climber to appear in your opponents' Matrices. If the climber is crushed, lines will appear from the bottom.

Shift [Effect: Self]

Aim your Pointer towards one side of your Matrix, then press . The blocks will align together in the area where you aimed the Pointer.
Time Stop [Effect: All Opponents]

Activating this item will temporarily freeze your opponents' game play for a short amount of time. Your own game play will not be affected during this time.

Hide [Effect: One Opponent]

Aim your Pointer at an opponent's Matrix and press to temporarily hide their NEXT queue.
You can change the items that appear for each game's Rules by selecting "Item" in the menu of that game.
When playing "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection" mode, you can select ON (for all Items)/OFF (no Items) as a Matchmaking condition.
