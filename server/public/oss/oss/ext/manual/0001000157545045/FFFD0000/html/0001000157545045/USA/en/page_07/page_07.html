The Game
Tetris Party is a puzzle game in which you try to eliminate blocks called Tetriminos (7 types) by clearing lines in the Matrix.

There are several rule variations for each mode that offer players a number of ways to earn tons of bonus points, such as erasing multiple lines at once, or erasing a consecutive number of lines. The object of the game is to effectively use these techniques to achieve high scores and beat your opponents.

What's more, in Tetris Party players can connect to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection to battle players around the world and compete for World Rankings.

Ranking is broken down into three areas: World, Americas, and Country.
