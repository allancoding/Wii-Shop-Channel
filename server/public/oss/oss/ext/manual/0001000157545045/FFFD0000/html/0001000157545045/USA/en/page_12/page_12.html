Techniques
In Tetris Party, make use of the following techniques to gain bonus points as well as send several penalty blocks into an opponent's Matrix.
TETRIS
Bonus points are awarded when you clear 4 lines with the I-Tetrimino. This is one of the classic Tetris techniques.
COMBO
Bonus points are awarded for consecutive lines cleared. The bonus award ends when you fail to clear consecutive lines.
ALL CLEAR
When you clear all blocks on the Matrix, bonus points are awarded. This bonus will not be awarded if you use items such as "Line Kick".
T-SPIN
Rotating a T-Tetrimino under specific conditions is called a T-Spin and results in bonus points. Clearing a line(s) while performing this maneuver will yield additional bonus points resultingina"T-SPINSINGLE" or "T-SPIN DOUBLE".

BACK-TO-BACK
A successive chain of
"TETRIS", "T-SPIN SINGLE"
 and "T-SPIN DOUBLE" will award bonus points. If the chain is interrupted by some other elimination technique (example: a normal
"DOUBLE"), this bonus is not awarded.
