WHAT’S UBONGO?

Get ready for a brand new experience... playing your favourite board game!


Ubongo is a game that will enjoy the whole family. Its mechanics is very simple; since it consists of being solving puzzles with the aim to obtain gems of colours. At the end of the established time, the player who has more gems will be the winner. Under this seemingly simple premise an extremely addictive game hides... You always want to play one more time!


This board game version is a blockbuster in countries as Germany, where its popularity has taken Ubongo to the highest position in the lists of sales of board games. The game has sold 1,000,000 units in Germany (including all the different versions) and has won many awards, including Best Family Game award in Sweden (Ubongo is the only game that has won this award two times, in 2003 and in 2009) and Game of the Year award in Norway and Finland.
