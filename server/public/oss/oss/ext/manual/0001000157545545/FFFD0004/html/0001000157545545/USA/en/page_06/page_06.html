Game Modes

Adventure Mode:
Clicking on Start will begin the Adventure mode. In this mode, you will be unlocking levels as you progress. There are a total of 12 levels with each level having either five, six, or eight rounds.

Endless Mode:
The endless mode consists of the same levels as Adventure Mode. Here, the only difference is that the balls won't stop coming unless the player fails. Some of the levels here will be locked first, and will be unlocked on playing some of the levels from Adventure Mode. And here, we can select the levels we like from the thumbnail view and play. There are 27 different levels and 11 bonus levels.

Rescue Mode:
The butterfly is in trouble, we have to free her by winning the levels within 1 hour. That's the statistics of this mode. The player has to win 25 levels within 60 minutes.

Bonus Rounds:
You get the bonus rounds as the final two rounds of each level in Adventure Mode. There are two types of Bonus Rounds.

Score Bonus:
In this round, you will have to clear all balls on the screen within 10 shots to get the bonus.

Ring Bonus:
In this round, you will have to collect the maximum number of rings you can in 45 seconds.
