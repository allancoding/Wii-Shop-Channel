Practice Mode

 

You have to be at the top of your game to take on Carmen Sandiego and her V.I.L.E. network.

 

That’s why the ACME Detective Agency lets you practice solving puzzles like the ones you come across in Story Mode.

 

To jump straight into these practice puzzles, move the cursor to Practice Mode on the game menu, and press. You can choose any puzzle from the puzzle menu.

 

For all of your hard work, you win Reputation Points when you complete these practice puzzles correctly. But you don’t get as many points as you get for solving the puzzles in Story Mode.
