Pause Menu
Press during a race in World Tour Mode or a custom track race to pause the game and display the Pause Menu. The following options are available.
RESUME

Close the Pause Menu and continue the race.
RESTART

Restart the race from the beginning.
OPTIONS

Adjust the volume of music and sound effects, and change the lane indicator. You can also change the control style, but only before the race starts.
QUIT

Quit the current race and return to the Track Selection Screen.
