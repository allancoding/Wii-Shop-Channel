Options Menu
Select OPTIONS from the Main Menu to display the Options Menu. The following options are available.
MUSIC VOLUME

Adjust music volume.
FX VOLUME

Adjust the volume of the sound effects.
LANE INDICATOR

Adjust how the lane indicator is displayed.

OFF: The indicator is not displayed.

TERRAIN: The indicator moves up and over hills, following the terrain of the track.

FLAT: The indicator stays at ground level and moves through hills.

CONTROL STYLE

Choose a control style
(see “8. Controls”).
