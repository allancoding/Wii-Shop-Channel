Sending Tracks
Using WiiConnect24, you can exchange tracks with your Wii Friends.
Note: Tracks can only be sent to Wii Friends, which are different from Friends registered in the game. Wii Friends are registered through the Wii Address Book. Please refer to the Wii Operations Manual – Channels & Settings for details.
Select SEND TRACK from the Track Options Menu (see “19. Creating Your Own Tracks”), then choose a Wii Friend to send the track to (you can also send it to yourself by pressing ).
Enter a message to be sent with your track. When you are finished, select DONE. Select OK to send the track.
