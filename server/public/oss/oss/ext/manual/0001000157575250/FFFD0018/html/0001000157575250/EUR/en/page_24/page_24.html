WiiConnect24

WiiConnect24 is a service that keeps your Wii console connected to the internet 24 hours a day, even if the console is in Standby Mode. This allows you to receive data and messages from friends and family at any time.

In order to use this feature, you will need to have already set up and configured your internet connection. You will also need to turn on WiiConnect24.

Attention!

If you connect to WiiConnect24, there is a possibility that your profile name will be seen by many people.
