Special Weapons
When you defeat a boss, you will be able to use their weapon. Using a Special Weapon decreases your Weapon Energy Level for that weapon.
Magma Bazooka
Fires magma bullets in three directions. Charging the weapon will increase its attack power.
Laser Trident
Fires a laser that can pierce objects.
Black Hole Bomb

Press the Fire button once to launch the bomb, press the Fire button a second time to detonate it and create a black hole. The black hole created will swallow up projectile attacks and enemies. Press up or down on the +Control Pad to change the bomb's path before detonating it.

Hornet Chaser
Fire mechanized hornets that hone in on a target.
Concrete Shot
Fires a chunk of concrete. You can make use of the concrete as a foothold.
Jewel Satellite

A diamond will circle the player's body and protect them. Press the Fire button again to send the diamond flying forward.

Plug Ball
Fires a plasma ball that moves along any surface.
Tornado Blow
Fires a tornado that blows back enemies.
Rush Coil
Jump on Rush's back to reach high places. Available from the beginning of the game.
Rush Jet

Jump on Rush's back and fly through the air. Obtained during the course of the game.
