Activities

At each stage of your baby's development they can do many different things. Your baby only needs your encouragement. Follow these simple tips and soon your baby will learn lots of fun activities.

 

Getting Attention

While zoomed out, keep pressing the A Button on your baby, and they will give you their attention.

 

Clap Hands!

Do you want your baby to clap hands? While zoomed out, keep pressing the A Button on your baby and your baby will their clap hands! This can only happen once the baby reaches a certain age so keep trying!

 

Woo hoo!

Do you want to say 'Hi' to your baby? While zoomed out, press the A Button and move the cursor quickly from left to right on the screen and you will wave 'Hi' to your baby! This can only happen once the baby reaches a certain age so keep trying!

 

Tickling

Tickling your baby will make it laugh and make it very happy! To do this, zoom in and keep pressing the A Button on your baby.

 

Caressing

Your baby needs lots of affection! To caress it, zoom in and carefully move the cursor back and forth over the baby's body while pressing the A Button.
