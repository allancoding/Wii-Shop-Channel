The town map

Time for a shopping trip! If your are low on supplies, want to stock up or just want to buy your baby a new toy or outfit, visit the shops in town to do your shopping!

 

 

There are four different shops in town:


Toy Shop

For time spent in the playroom and garden, visit the Toy Shop to buy items to keep your baby happy and entertained so they do not get bored!

 

Clothes & Cradle

In this store you will find furniture, clothing and accessories for your baby.

 

Health & Bath

This store stocks items such as soaps, creams and other items for you to keep your baby clean and healthy.

 

Food Shop

If you are out of supplies to make food for your baby, visit the Food Shop to buy milk and food for delicious meals for your baby.

 

 

Once in a shop, view the available items by pressing the A Button on the left or right arrow icons. When you have chosen an item, press the A Button on either the up or down arrow to adjust the quantity level. To purchase the item, press the A Button on the "Thumbs Up" icon. Make sure you have enough money to afford the purchase of your items! After you are done in the store, press the B Button to take you back to the town map.
