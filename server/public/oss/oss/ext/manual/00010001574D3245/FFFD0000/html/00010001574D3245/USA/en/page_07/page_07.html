The Main Menu

The Main Menu is where you can access the main game, view your baby's photo album, change the game's language or view the game credits.

 

Play
This is where the game begins. Are you ready for your new assignment?

 

Photos
How sweet! Here you can look at your baby's photos! Select this icon to see all your photo albums. Next, select the album with the photos you want to view of your baby!

 

Language
Here you can select the game's language.
