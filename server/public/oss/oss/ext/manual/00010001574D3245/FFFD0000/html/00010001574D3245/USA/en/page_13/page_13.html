Mini games

You can play several different mini games that will positively affect your baby's levels and their development! Remember each mini game is only accessible within certain rooms. Best follow the Nanny's tips in order to play the mini games successfully!

 

Clean this Room

This mini game can be played in every room except in the garden. Every room needs to stay clean because the cleanliness level of a room will also affect your baby's cleanliness level. If you use the right tools, the room you are cleaning will quickly become spick and span!

 

Bathroom

Change Nappy

Your baby's cleanliness is very important. Changing your baby's nappy will need to be done regularly. Do not forget to put cream on your baby's tender skin when you are changing their nappy so their skin doesn't get sore! The Nanny has prepared everything for you in the bathroom!

 

Prepare a Bath

As a caring parent, you know that regular baths are very important for your baby's health and development. Always try to use the best soap, a very soft sponge and gently dry your baby off after every bath. At the Health & Bath Shop you will find everything you need to stock up your bathroom!

 

Bedroom

Lull your Baby to Sleep

Your baby needs its strength and energy to grow up healthy. By lulling your baby to sleep for either a nap during the day or to sleep for the night will allow your baby to regain energy. Keep your eye on the energy levels to know when your baby needs a little break.

 

Playroom & Garden

Learning to Crawl

When your baby is old enough, you can start teaching them to crawl, to balance and then to walk. The Nanny will tell you when your baby is ready to learn. The baby will need a lot of room to practice. The best locations are in the playroom or out in the garden.

 

Learning to Balance

After your baby learns to crawls they next need to know how to balance. In order for your baby to learn how to walk they will need to know how to balance. Take your baby to the playroom or the garden to practice balancing with your baby.

 

Learning to Walk

Ready? Get set... Go! After your baby is able to balance it is then time to teach your baby to walk! Be careful when teaching your baby to walk. Make sure they do not lose their balance and fall over! The playroom and the garden are the best places for your baby to practice!

 

Kitchen

Prepare the Bottle

Preparing the right bottle for your baby might be harder than you thought but the Nanny is there to help you. When you want to prepare a bottle head to the kitchen but make sure you have enough milk or you might have to make a trip into town for more supplies!

 

Prepare Food

Once your baby reaches the appropriate age it will be time for your baby to stop using the bottle and switch to food. When this happens you will need to prepare food instead of a bottle when your baby is hungry. There are many different ingredients you can purchase from the Food Shop in town.

 

Feed your baby

Feed the Baby

After preparing the food it is time to feed your baby. Be careful when feeding your baby solid food, it might be too hot! When you take a spoonful from the plate, wave the spoon back and forth in the air to cool the food so you do not burn your baby's mouth.

 

Giving the Bottle

After preparing a bottle for your baby you can feed them the bottle when they are hungry. When feeding your baby the bottle you need to hold the bottle at the right angle so the baby does not sputter and spit the bottle out.

 

Photo Album
Beauty Contest
Every month there is a beauty contest. There are prizes to be won and you can participate each month if you have enough money for the participation fee. To enter the beauty contest, press the A Button to view your baby's photos. Once you have found the best photo of your baby from your album press the A Button on the mail icon to submit it into the contest. Your Nanny will let you know when the contest is over. Be sure your baby is wearing their best outfit for the contest! Good luck!
