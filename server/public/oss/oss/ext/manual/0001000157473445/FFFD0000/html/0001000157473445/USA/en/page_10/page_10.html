Game Controls

How to play

This game is played using the Wii Remote. Wii MotionPlus is recommended.


Menu Screen Operation

To select option tabs

  • Point at any option tab and press the A Button to confirm selection.
  • Alternatively, use +Control Pad to change selection and press the A Button to confirm.
  • Press the B Button to go back to previous menu (if applicable).


    • In-Game operation

    You can choose to play the game with or without the Wii MotionPlus. Please take note that the controls will vary when using different modes of playing.

    Controls
    (Operation Mode with Wii MotionPlus)

  • Attacking : Attack the enemies by swinging the Wii Remote in the direction you want to attack. The sword will move according to the way you swing the Wii Remote.
  • Defending : Press and hold the B Button to block attacks, rotate the Wii Remote to change blocking angle.
  • Executing special technique : Press and hold the A Button, swing the Wii Remote to execute special technique. There are 2 types of special techniques depending on whether the direction of the slash is vertical or horizontal.


    • Recalibrate

  • If the Wii Remote / Wii MotionPlus is not accurately following your motions, during the game press the + button to activate the pause menu. Select the Recalibrate tab to recalibrate the Wii MotionPlus.

    • Controls
    (Operation Mode without Wii MotionPlus)

  • Attacking : Attack the enemy by shaking the Wii Remote. Change the angle of attack by holding the B button and rotating the Wii Remote. Release the B button before shaking to attack. Release the B button again before shaking to attack.
  • Defending: Press and hold the B Button to block attacks. While holding the B button, rotate the Wii Remote to change sword direction.
  • Executing special technique : Press and hold the A Button, shake the Wii Remote to execute special technique. There are 2 types of special techniques depending on whether the direction of the slash is vertical or horizontal.


    • Tutorial

    The tutorial game is played at the start of stage 1. Tutorial is available for both modes of controls (with and without Wii MotionPlus).
