Gameplay

Gameplay Tips :

Accumulate combo hits by successfully hitting the enemies consecutively, higher combo hits increases the Power Bar faster.

During blocking, align your sword in the direction perpendicular to the enemies’ sword or direction of attack to block sucessfully.

When executing special technique, slashing vertically or horizontally will execute a different type of technique.

Clearing a Stage

A stage will be cleared when all enemies in the stage have been eliminated. You can progress to the next stage when Cleared Percentage is 100%.

Skill Level

As you clear more stages, your skill level will increase.

Unlocks

Rank, sword, Health Point (HP) and Power Bar bonus can be unlocked and acquired when stages are cleared.
