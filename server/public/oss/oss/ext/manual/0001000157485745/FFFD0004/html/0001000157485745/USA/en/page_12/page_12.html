Game Modes

 

QUICK MATCH


Select a difficulty, team and court and play against a friend or the CPU.


 

TOURNAMENT


Choose a difficulty level and team: play a World Championship through all the courts. Win and unlock teams and courts in Quick Match!

 

 

 

SURVIVAL


This is an endless mode where you keep playing matches until you lose, increasing your score with each victory. See how long you can survive as the difficulty increases with each match, and compare your scores to those of the best players around the world with HoopWorld Global Ranking.
