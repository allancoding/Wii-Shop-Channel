Power-Ups

Power-ups appear randomly on the court in a mystery box. Run over them to pick them up, press the A Button to use them.

 

Some items are for instant use, others have a temporary effect. An item is queued for later use if another effect is currently active.

 

Turtle's Shell (lasts 15 seconds)

 

Makes your team immune to damage

 

Energy Surge (lasts 10 seconds)


Fills your Energy Meter, allowing you to endlessly use Energy-consuming moves for its duration.

 

The Bomb (Instant)


A massive explosion knocks your opponents down.

 

Bull Rage (lasts 15 seconds)


Gives your team a strength boost. Knock out opponents in one hit!

 

Straight Shooter (lasts 15 seconds)


Greatly increases the chance of a successful basket.

 

Basket Shield (lasts 15 seconds)


Magic protects your basket, causing enemy shots to be repelled.

 

Golden Ball


Get a basket with this to get 5 points. Watch out - opponents can steal it from you. This effect stays active until someone scores.

 

Shadow Ball


Get a basket with this to remove 5 points from the opponent's score. This ball can also be stolen. This effect stays active until someone scores.
