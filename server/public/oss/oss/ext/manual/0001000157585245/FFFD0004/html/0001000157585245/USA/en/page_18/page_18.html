Tips

●Can't find the right fish?
Are you casting in the same location, even you don't see any fish?
If you want to find the target fish, try casting frequently in various locations. Also, fish will sometimes approach your lure if you wait a bit after casting. If you still can't find your target fish, try using different tackle.

Press the - Button when you're underwater, and the camera angle will switch. This may help you find fish more easily.

 

●Tackle Choice

Do you often hook fish that you don't want to land?
As you may already know, some tackle is more appealing to certain fish. If you keep hooking fish you don't want, check the tackle you are using and find tackle that is more appealing to your target fish.

 

●Tension Gauge
Once you hook a fish, you'll see the tension gauge on the left of the screen. This gauge guide shows the line tension. The higher the gauge goes up, the more tense the line gets. The lower the gauge goes down, the looser the line gets and more chance the fish will get away.

Keep a close eye on the tension gauge while fighting, and it'll help you land fish successfully.

 

If you'd like to experience a more thrilling fishing fight, turn off the tension gauge in Options and challenge landing one on your own!
