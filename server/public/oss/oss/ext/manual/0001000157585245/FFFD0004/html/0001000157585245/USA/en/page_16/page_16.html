Change Tackle

Press the - Button when you're on land, and you'll be able to change your tackle. Choose "Select Tackle" from the menu.

 

To change your tackle, press left or right on the +Control Pad or Control Stick on the Nunchuk.

 

You'll see how the tackle behaves in the water in the picture on the right.

 

The number of stars shows how suitable the tackle is to hook the target fish. The more stars there are, the bigger chance you have to land it.

 

The target fish can be changed by pressing the - and + Buttons.

 

Press the A Button to select a tackle.
