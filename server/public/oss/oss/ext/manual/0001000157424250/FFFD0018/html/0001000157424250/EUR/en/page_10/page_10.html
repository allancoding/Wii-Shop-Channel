● Curve Turn
It's a more difficult turn to make than a Drift Turn, but you'll lose less speed with this one.

● Forward Jump
When starting a race or losing speed after falling, performing a Forward Jump will cause you to gain speed more quickly than sliding down the slope normally.

● Grab Jump
Your character flips his or her board up and grabs onto the edge to perform a "Grab Jump". Time your Grabs just right to defend against attacks, or deflect an opponent's attack while using the Gauge.

● Tricks
Your SP Gauge will increase each time a trick is performed successfully. Multiple tricks can be performed in midair when timed right, but landing back on the ground while in the middle of a trick will cause you to wipe out.

● SP Gauge
You can increase the SP Gauge by simply snowboarding down the course or doing tricks. Each effect will last a certain amount of time when the Gauge power is consumed.

・ Speed Up
Snowboarding speed will increase.

・ Tackle Attack
Knock down an opponent by making contact.
