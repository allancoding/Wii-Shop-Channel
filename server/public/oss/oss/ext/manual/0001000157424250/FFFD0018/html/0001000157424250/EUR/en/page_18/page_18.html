STAND ALONE Mode
STAND ALONE mode has three different sets of rules to choose from. Select the type of race you want to participate in, then press .

● BATTLE
Use items placed throughout the course, and be the first to complete 3 laps to win. Check "BATTLE" in Nintendo WFC Mode for details.

● STOIC
Make good use of techniques in a race for first place. Check "STOIC" in Nintnendo WFC Mode for details.

● TIME ATTACK
Make use of 3 Turbo Items in the race against the clock. It is very important to use techniques and items selectively to shave time off of your final score.

Once a type of race has been chosen, you must select a character and equipment to race with. Finally, select a course to board on, and the race will begin.
The level of difficulty can be changed for BATTLE and STOIC via OPTIONS mode.
