How to Play

 

Santa needs your help to decorate all the trees.  Press the A Button to start the level.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

You can help Santa decorate the tree by removing the presents and ornaments that he stacks.

 

You can remove the presents in several ways.
 

Horizontally, Vertically, or Diagonally: Select one present, then select another, and the presents that are matching in a line will be added to the tree.

 

Square: Select a present at one corner, then select the same type present on the opposite corner, and the presents making a square (outlined or full) will be added to the tree.

 

Extra Bonuses:

When you consecutively connect packages without missing a beat, you get extra points. Oh boy, what a treat!

 

Ornaments:
Ornaments can be removed simply by selecting two of the same color.

 

  Candy Canes:
When you remove presents or ornaments from the game play area and add them to the Christmas tree, candy canes will fall to the bottom of the screen! Collect the candy canes by moving your pointer over them.

Collect as many candy canes as you can because when you collect enough, Santa will add a special star to the game that can greatly help you get through a level or just bring some additional fun!

 

The star meter will let you know approximately how many candy canes you still need.
 

Gold Coin Ice Blocks:
Once you collect enough candy canes Santa will also start adding Gold Coin Ice Blocks to the game screen!

Chip away the ice by selecting the ice blocks with your pointer. When you chip away the ice magical gold coins will be released!


 Magical Gold Coins:

These are stuck in the ice blocks; when released they fall to the bottom of the screen. Collect these gold coins by moving your pointer over them. Once you collect enough gold coins Santa will throw out a magical Toy Soldier!

 

The Toy Soldier meter will let you know approximately how many gold coins you still need.

 

 Magical Toy Soldier:
If you collect enough gold coins Santa will add a Magical Toy Soldier to the game. Quickly select the Toy Soldier before he disappears!

The Toy Soldier will unlock one of ten Mini Games.

Once the Mini Game has ended, you'll continue your level at the exact place you left off.
 

Happy Star:

When Santa is done stacking presents, he sends you a Happy Star to put on the top of the tree.  Select the star to complete the tree and move to the next level and be as happy as can be.
