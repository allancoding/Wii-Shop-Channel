Starting the Game
The title screen will appear after the opening sequence.


Press the confirm button on the title screen.
The menu will be displayed.


Move the cursor with the +Control Pad
and press the confirm button to select a mode.
To play by yourself, select 1 PLAYER.
To play with a friend, select 2 PLAYERS.
Once you have selected your character, the game will start.

●MENU

1 PLAYER　Start a 1-player game.
2 PLAYERS　Start a 2-players game.
OPTIONS　Change various settings in the game.
