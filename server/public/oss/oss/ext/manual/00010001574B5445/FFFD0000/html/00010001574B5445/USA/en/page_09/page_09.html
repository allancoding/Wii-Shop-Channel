Controls

●Wii Remote

+Control Pad　Player movement, weapon aiming
1 Button　Attack/Fixed Attack (*)
2 Button　Jump
Swing　Weapon change
＋ Button　Pause menu

(*) Hold down the 1 Button

●Classic Controller

+Control Pad　Player movement, weapon aiming
ｘ Button　Weapon change
ｙ Button　Attack
ａ Button　－－
ｂ Button　Jump
＋ Button　Pause menu
Ｒ Button　Fixed Attack

●Nintendo GameCube Controller

+Control Pad　Player movement, weapon aiming
Ｘ Button　－－
Ｙ Button　Weapon change
Ａ Button　Jump
Ｂ Button　Attack
START/PAUSE　Pause menu
Ｒ Button　Fixed Attack
  • Press left/right on +Control Pad to move sideways.
    Gun will be aimed in direction faced.
  • Press down on +Control Pad to lie down on the floor.
    You cannot move while lying on the floor.
    Gun direction is the same as before lying down.
  • Press up on +Control Pad to stop and aim upwards.
  • Press diagonally up/down to aim diagonally up/down while moving.
  • Press the jump button to jump straight up.
    Jump while walking to take a big jump forward.
  • Press left/right on +Control Pad while jumping to move slightly.
    The +Control Pad will also aim your gun.
  • On some platforms, you can jump while lying flat to drop down a level.
  • You can hang from certain platforms by touching them while jumping.
    You can move left and right while hanging.
    Jump again to let go of the platform.
  • You can climb up certain walls by touching them.
    Use the +Control Pad to move up and down.
    Press away from the wall on the +Control Pad and jump to let go of the wall.
  • Press the attack button to shoot in the direction your gun is aimed in.
  • Holding the attack button down will put you in Fixed Attack mode.
  • In Fixed Attack mode, pressing the +Control Pad allows you to aim in any direction instead of moving or lying down.
  • In your play settings, you can change the button used for Fixed Attack mode.
    If you have done so, holding down the attack button will not switch to Fixed Attack mode.
  • When you have 2 or more weapons, you can change the weapon you are using.

●Changing Button Assignment
You can change the button assignment by going to OPTIONS and then CONTROLLER SETTINGS.

●Nintendo GameCube Controller Rumble Settings
You can change the Nintendo GameCube Controller Rumble settings by going to OPTIONS and then CONTROLLER SETTINGS.
