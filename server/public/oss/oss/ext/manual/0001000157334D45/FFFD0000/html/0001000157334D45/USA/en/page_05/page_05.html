Introduction

In the tranquility of a lazy Parisian day, France’s three most renowned heroes, The Three Musketeers, Porthos, Aramis and Athos, are enjoying themselves at their favorite bar the Red Dove Inn. Suddenly the door flies open! Standing in the doorway is their comrade, the young, aspiring musketeer D’Artagnan. His friends welcome him and the four heroes settle down for an evening of revelry.

 

But our heroes’ fun is short-lived when the inn is unexpectedly stormed by the sinister Count Xavier, his evil lieutenant, the hook-handed count Orsini and a gang of their henchmen. A fierce battle ensues, but after a valiant struggle the four friends are overpowered.

 

Having been knocked unconscious in the scuffle, Porthos wakes to see Aramis, Athos and D’Artagnan being hauled away in a prison cart by their dastardly foes. Now it’s up to you to take on the role of Porthos and save your friends from the count’s evil clutches!
