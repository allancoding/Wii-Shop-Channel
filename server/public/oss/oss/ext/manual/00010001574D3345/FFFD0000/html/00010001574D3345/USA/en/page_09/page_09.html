Controls

Throwing: To aim your throw, use the pointer of the Wii Remote. Hold swing the Wii Remote to set the throw power and release .

After Touch: Holding will cause your banana to ‘pull’ in that direction.

Catching: To catch a banana, press briefly. Timing is critical.

Dodging: Press and your monkey will lean in that direction to dodge incoming bananas.

Jumping: Aim at an uninhabited podium and press to jump to it.

Shooting: To fire projectile weapons, briefly press .

Ammunition: To collect extra banana ammunition, shake the Wii Remote vigorously to magically summon a banana tree.
