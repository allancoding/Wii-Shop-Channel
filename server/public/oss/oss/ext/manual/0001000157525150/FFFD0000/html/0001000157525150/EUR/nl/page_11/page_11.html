Weapons and Bonuses

Pistol: has a rapid fire rate and effective on smaller prey.

Shotgun: is mid-range and has high firepower, but a frequent and slow reloading time.

 

Bow: is a silent weapon and can be fired several times without alerting an animal.

 

DB shotgun: this is like the shotgun but can fire two shots almost simultaneously.

 

Rifle: is fast, accurate and high-range. It has mid firepower.

 

Sniper Rifle: same as rifle, but has a scope for better aim. It is good for distance shots.

 

 

Ammo Clip: increases the number of ammunition.

 

Bandage: restores some health points.

 

Full health pack: fully restores your health.

 

Slow motion: a temporary full scene slowed down.

 

Camouflage: decreases the animals' ability to detect you.

 

Cover Scent: decreases the animals' ability to detect you.

 

Ammo Belt: doubles the amount of ammo for a certain time.

 

Ocarina: its sound will lure target animal to you.

 

Special view: helps you in targeting the animals.
