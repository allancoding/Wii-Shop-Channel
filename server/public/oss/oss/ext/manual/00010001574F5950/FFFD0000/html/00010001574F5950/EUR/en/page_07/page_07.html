Controls
Bit Boy!! Can be played in
+Control Pad style or in joystick style.
The switch between the two control types will be detected automatically, depending on how you are holding the Wii Remote.

 

+Control Pad style

Hold the Wii Remote in both hands
in a horizontal position.
 

 

   Move  

  Pause / Confirm your choice  

  Special attack
 

Starting with 8-bit

  

  Change camera view
 

Starting w. 32-Bit

  

  Jump
 

Only in 128-Bit

  

  Change zoom
 

Only in 128-Bit

  
 

Joystick style

Hold the Wii Remote in a upright position and place it on a flat, even surface, e.g. a table.
 

 

   Tilt the Wii Remote left, right, forwards or
  backwards to move in the game.

  Pause / Confirm your choice  

  Special attack
 

Starting with 8-bit

  

  Change camera view
 

Starting w. 32-bit

  

  Jump
 

Only in 128-Bit

  

 

Multiplayer

 

You can play with a friend cooperatively.
Just connect a second Wii Remote to do so.
