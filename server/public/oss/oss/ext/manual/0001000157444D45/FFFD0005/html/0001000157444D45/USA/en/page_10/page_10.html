Basic Rules and Techniques

Dr. Mario and Virus Buster share the same basic rules and objectives. As the capsules enter the bottle and fall, line them up with like-colored viruses in order to exterminate them. Once all viruses have been removed, a menu will appear, and you may choose to proceed to the next stage (1-Player Classic mode or Virus Buster only), retry the same stage, or quit and return to the options screen.

● Eliminating Viruses
Rotate the capsules and drop them so they are next to, on top of, or below viruses of the same color. When you have vertically or horizontally aligned four or more capsule pieces or viruses of the same color, they will all disappear.

● Chains and Combos
When viruses and capsules disappear, any remaining capsule pieces will drop, potentially causing more like-colored capsules and viruses to line up. These are called chains. When two or more sets disappear at the same time, they are called combos. Getting chains and combos in Battle mode will send garbage to your opponent.

● Game Over
If capsules stack up to the top of the bottle, your game will end. You can then choose Try Again or Quit.
