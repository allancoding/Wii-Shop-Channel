How to Play (Virus Buster)

Virus Buster can be played cooperatively with up to four players. The rules of the game are the same as in Dr. Marioâ€™s Classic mode, but the game is controlled with the Wii Remote Pointer. To play, point at a capsule and use or to rotate it. To move a capsule, press and hold either button and drag the capsule to the location you want.

Use the settings screen to choose the virus level, difficulty, and music, then press Start to begin the game.

Note: Each player must use a Wii Remote controller in Virus Buster.
