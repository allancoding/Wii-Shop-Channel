Items and Special Units

To keep those gemstones safe, you will also need to make use of items and special units in addition to your standard complement of bears.

 

Obtaining Items and Special Units
To obtain an item or special unit from inside the book, simply throw a bear close to the desired object and the unit will collect it automatically.

 

Using Items and Special Units
The items and units you have collected will appear to the right of the bear icon at the bottom of the screen. Select the item or unit you wish to use by pressing left or right on the +Control Pad. Throw items or special units into the book in the same way as the bear units. Choose a direction, then hold down the A Button or B Button while you swing the Wii Remote upwards.

 

Types of Items and Special Units


Magic spells: There are several types of elemental magic spells such as fire, water, and ice, as well as spells that power up your units. Fire spells can be used to burn everything in an area. Water spells can be used to slow down badbears, or even extinguish fire-based enemies. Ice spells freeze everything in an area. Elemental magic is very useful, but be careful: any nearby bear units will also suffer the spell's effects. Lastly, Powerballs can be used to enhance the strength of all friendly bear units within the spell's area of effect.

 

Bookmarks: The three types of bookmarks are Blazing, Torrential, and Glacial. Each bookmark respectively burns, floods, or freezes the entire page.

 

Frogs: Frogs spit water at distant enemies.

 

 

 

Bittybears: Not as tough as standard bears, but what they lack in strength they make up for in numbers.

 

 

Jack o' Lanterns: These pumpkin-headed units can set fire to nearby enemies. They can also light bomb fuses.

 

Penguins: Penguins can freeze nearby enemies.
