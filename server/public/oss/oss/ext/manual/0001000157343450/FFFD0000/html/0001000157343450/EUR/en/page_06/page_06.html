Introduction

A city like any other... a dawn like any other.
The day begins, but your dream doesn’t end.

And you are dreaming of Hawaii, the beach, a hammock with the sea in the background. Everything is perfect. Everything is peaceful.

And that’s how you spend the night, dreaming about being surrounded by beautiful native girls, drinking a Mai Tai while you listen to the relaxing sound of the waves beating against the sand.

A city like any other... a dawn like any other.
The sound of the sea.
Of peace.
Of relaxation.
The sound of silence.
The sound of... a door slamming?

The neighbour’s shower? Children crying. Road construction. Car horns honking.

The alarm clock that reminds you that it is all a dream.

A city like any other... a dawn like any other. Your dream ends and your nightmare begins. The stress has arrived and you have to put an end to it!

Climb into the skin of Jack, the most stressed-out man in the world.
Grab your Wii Remote as if it were a baseball bat and destroy everything you find in your path!
