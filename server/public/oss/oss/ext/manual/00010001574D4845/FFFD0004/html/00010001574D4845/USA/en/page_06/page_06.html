Gameplay
Your goal is to collect all of the Cheese on each floor of the Mouse House, then make your way to the exit. You will encounter many things as you make your way through all 100 floors!

Hot Peppers are a spicy treat for the Mouse, but because they are so hot he can only eat one at a time! After eating a Hot Pepper, he will have a flaming glow and can shoot one fireball, which can damage some enemies.


Hint Coins can be found on early levels. Get one and Grandpa Mouse will help you on your way.
