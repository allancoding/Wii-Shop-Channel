Eaters

Each eater has one of four strengths. Use these to pick a character who matches your eating style!

  Fast Hands: These characters can grab food and stuff it into their mouths quickly.
  Jaw Power: These characters can chew more rapidly than others.
  Iron Stomach: These characters' Barf-O-Meters increase very slowly.
  Super Jiggle: These characters can decrease their Barf-O-Meters in a hurry.
Each eater also has a specialty food that he or she can eat faster than anyone, and some excel at certain kinds of attacks.

Crazy Legs Conti
An all-round eater whose strong stomach makes him a good choice for beginners.
Records: 34.75 ears of sweet corn in 12 minutes

Erik “The Red” Denmark
A berserker in battle like his Viking ancestors, he accelerates quickly but his stomach is a little unstable.
Records: 4 lbs 15 oz spot shrimp in 12 minutes

Juliet Lee
Don’t be deceived by her lady-like appearance. Juliet is an aggressive eater who overwhelms her opponents with powerful burps.
Records: 13.23 lbs cranberry sauce in 8 minutes

El Toro Jimenez
Some say his bark is worse than his bite, but his gas is the most dangerous of all.
Records: 34 hard-boiled eggs in 8 minutes (personal best)

Tim “Eater X” Janus
This mysterious eater wears a mask to hide his inner torment. When he eats burritos, he unleashes that torment in an explosive mix of gas and spicy burps.
Records: 11.81 lbs burritos in 10 minutes

Sonya Thomas
Known as “The Black Widow” for her strength across the board, her strong defense makes her difficult to defeat with attacks alone.
Records: 10 lbs 3 oz meatballs in 12 minutes

Richard “The Locust” LeFevre
An experienced eater with a durable stomach that allows him to dominate in the dangerous jalapeno contests.
Records: 247 jalapenos in 8 minutes

Patrick “Deep Dish” Bertoletti
A hot-headed young eater with fire in his belly, he eats at a furious pace and excels in all kinds of attacks. Defense is his weakness.
Records: 21 lbs grits in 10 minutes

Joey “Jaws” Chestnut
As of 2007, the world champion of hot dogs. Joey is a very fast eater with great all-round strength.
Records: 66 hot dogs with buns in 12 minutes

Takeru “The Tsunami” Kobayashi
A living legend and a force to be reckoned with. You will have to master all of your gustatory skills to defeat him.
Records: 58 bratwurst in 10 minutes
