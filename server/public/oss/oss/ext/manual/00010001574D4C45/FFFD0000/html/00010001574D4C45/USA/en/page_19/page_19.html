Power-Up Effects
Mega Burp: This shock wave will knock your opponent back and raise his Barf-O-Meter while decreasing your own.

Gas Attack: A foul odor that can ruin any eater’s appetite. Use it to stun your opponent and increase his Barf-O-Meter.

Red Hot Chili Pepper: This gives you a fiery burp that will slow down your opponent. It might also ignite flammable gases.

Invincibility: An all-purpose defense that can save you from any gastro-intestinal attack.

Antacid: This little pill can settle your stomach. It’s also known to counter the effects of some spicy foods.

Lazy Susan: This sneaky technique lets you trade plates with your opponent. A great way to steal Power-Ups and get rid of Bad Stuff.

Bad Stuff: How did this get on the table? If you eat this nasty item, you’ll spit it out and lose everything in your mouth. Make your opponent eat it instead.

Ketch-Up: This magical red condiment makes food taste better. It will boost your appetite, letting you “ketch-up” to your opponent.
