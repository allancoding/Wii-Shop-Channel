Other Features
Ranch Level

The Ranch's level is determined by the size of the Ranch. The larger the Ranch gets, the higher the Ranch's level.

Level Up the Ranch

When you bring a certain number of Pokémon to the Ranch, Hayley will level up your Ranch and expand it, construction will be completed on the given date.

Features available after
the Ranch's level gets higher

● The number of Pokémon or Miis you can bring to your Ranch rises.

● Free Mode becomes available.

● You can add Pokémon to Favorites and bring only those Pokémon to the Ranch.

TOY BOX

● You will receive more Toy Boxes (see "22. Toy Box"). When you press + to grab one and then release it, a Toy will come out.

● You can often trade a Pokémon with Hayley.

Events available without
a high-level Ranch

● When you start the game, you may get invited to a Ranch by an owner of another Pokémon Ranch.

● Fun things happen if you deposit a certain number of Pokémon or a certain kind of Pokémon.

What happens if I shake
the Wii Remote?

● When you are playing the game and you shake the Wii Remote controller, different things can happen depending on how you shake it!
