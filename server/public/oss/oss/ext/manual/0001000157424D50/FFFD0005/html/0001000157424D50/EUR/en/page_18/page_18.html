Deposit Details

Here you can check Pokémon on the Ranch, see Mii info and view the Pokémon Parade.

You can also change the selection of Pokémon you want to bring to the Ranch by pointing at the POKéMON ON THE RANCH button and pressing . You can change the selection to choose one of the trainers, ALL or FAVORITES.

● What is a Pokémon Caretaker?

A Pokémon Caretaker is a Mii who takes care of Pokémon brought to the Ranch by a Trainer. The Pokémon on the ranch will be extra friendly with the Mii who has been assigned as their Caretaker.

● Change the Caretaker of Pokémon

On the Deposit Details Screen, point at the Mii you want to change and press .

Select a Mii Already on the Ranch as a Caretaker

When the Caretaker Setup Screen is displayed, point at the Mii that you want to change and press . The background of the Mii changes to blue.

Note: A Mii that has a below its face is a Mii who is already assigned as a Caretaker on the Ranch.

Select a Mii as a Caretaker Who Is Not on the Ranch

If you want to select a different Mii that you have on the Caretaker Setup Screen, point at SELECT A DIFFERENT Mii and press . If you have any other Miis in the Mii Channel, those Miis will be displayed. Point at the Mii you want to select and press . The background of the Mii changes to blue.

Note: You cannot choose SELECT A DIFFERENT Mii if you have the maximum number of Miis on the Ranch.

Once setup is complete, point at OKAY AND RETURN and press to return to the Deposit Details Screen.

Note: You cannot change the Caretaker status for Hayley.

If You Want to Erase a Mii

When you erase a Mii on the Mii Channel, that Mii will disappear from the Ranch, and a new Mii will be automatically assigned as a Caretaker.

● Add a Pokémon to Favorites

This feature becomes available when you bring a certain number of Pokémon to the Ranch. Once you add Pokémon to Favorites, you have the option of bringing only those Pokémon to the Ranch. To add to Favorites, point at next to the Pokémon Name and press . You'll see a crown next to the Pokémon Name. When you want to remove it from Favorites, point at the crown and press .

● Parade

Point at the Parade button and press to start the Pokémon Parade. In the Pokémon Parade, the Pokémon Name, Level, Capture Date, and Location info will be displayed. Point at the door or press when you want to exit the Parade.
