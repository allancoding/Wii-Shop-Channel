Taking Pictures

You can take a picture of your Ranch. Point at TAKE PICTURE on the Main Menu and press , or just press or . You can save up to 20 pictures in the Album. When you have 20 pictures in the Album and you take a picture, the oldest picture will automatically be deleted.

Note: The capture date, location info etc. displayed during a Parade will be shown in the picture but on-screen text and menus will not.
