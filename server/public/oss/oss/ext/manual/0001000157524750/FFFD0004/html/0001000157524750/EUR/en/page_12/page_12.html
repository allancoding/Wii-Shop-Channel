Avoiding Attacks


Rage of the Gladiator is a game of avoiding attacks, and then executing a well-timed series of hits. Some attacks require precise timing, and others require that you avoid the attack in the right way.



Most attacks are easiest to dodge left or right.


When an attack is coming at your feet, your only option may be to jump over it, such as the Minotaur's shockwave.


Some attacks must be blocked, such as The Sea Witch's Gorgon Gaze.

Getting Stunned


Certain attacks have the chance of stunning you.

To get out of the stun, you need to swing the Wii Remote and/or repeatedly press the A Button, 1 Button or 2 Button.
