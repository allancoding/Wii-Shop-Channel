Using Wii MotionPlus
Connecting Wii MotionPlus
  1. Ensure that the LOCK Switch on the back of the accessory is set as shown in the diagram, with the white arrow visible.
  2. With the front of the Wii MotionPlus accessory facing up, slide the Wii Remote into the jacket component as far as it will go.
  3. Feed the Wii Remote Wrist Strap from the inside of the jacket through the strap hole and pull through until all of the wrist strap and cord are visible.
  4. With the Wii MotionPlus connector facing the Wii Remote, gently pull the sensor unit up at an angle, moving it from side to side so that the Wii Remote is fully inside the jacket.
  5. Holding down the Lock / Release Levers, connect the sensor unit to the Wii Remoteâ€™s External Extension Connector.
  6. Check that the jacket and wrist strap are properly fitted.
  7. Slide the LOCK Switch on the back of Wii MotionPlus in the direction of the arrow until it locks.
  8. Put your hand through the wrist strap and tighten the strap lock. If the strap lock has a lock lever, close the lever to hold the strap lock in place.

For information on how to disconnect Wii MotionPlus, or how to connect the Nunchuk controller, please refer to the Wii MotionPlus Operations Guide.
