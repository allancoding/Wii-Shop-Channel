How to Play

Welcome to Trenches Generals! Fight your way through the Trenches of World War One and defeat your opponent!

Trenches Generals is an action-oriented strategy game that tasks you with deploying combat units to the battlefield, advance the battlefront, reach and destroy the enemy base.

Moving the in-game view

 

There are several ways to move your view of the battlefield.

 

- By moving the Wii Remote controller cursor on the edges of the screen in the appropriate direction.

 

- By pressing on the Wii Remote controller.

 

- By using the "view lens" on the mini map both in single player and 2-player game modes (see below).

Earning Gold and Producing Units

 

In Trenches the main goal is to train units and get them to the opposite side of the battlefield.

 

 

 

As the game goes on, a constant gold income is earned. Gold is used to train units and unleash artillery and gas strikes.

 

 

To train units, press on the unit button corresponding to the desired unit to train it. It will instantly appear on the far side of the battlefield, exiting from the player base.

 

The units bar

 

In 2-players game modes, the units bar is not constantly displayed. Press to display and hide the bar.

 

The units bar in a 2-player game

 

Commanding Units

Commanding units in Trenches is very straightforward.

Simply select a unit by placing the Wii Remote controller cursor over it and pressing .

With still pressed, drag a line to where the unit is required to move. Release to confirm the order.

Drag a movement line to order your units to move

 

Moving all your on-screen units with Global Orders:

 

Global Orders are a simple way to move all your on-screen units to the next trench with one gesture. No need to select the units individually and issue separate orders.

 

To issue a global order, press + and shake the Wii Remote controller:

  • left shake: move units to the next trench on the left
  • right shake: move units to the next trench on the right
  • down: halt all units
Weapon strikes

 

Artillery and Toxic Gas strikes are available from the units bar.

Press on any of these two to see the Wii Remote controller cursor change to a cross hair.

Press on the field to unleash the most deadly attacks in the game.

Press to cancel the strike.

Accelerate the game

To accelerate the game at any time, press on the acceleration switch.

Press it again to return to normal speed.

In single player modes, you can also press to accelerate the game.
