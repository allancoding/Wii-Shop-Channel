‘Missile’ spells are fast spells that seek towards your opponent. ‘Aura’ spells need to be flown into and add to your dragons abilities. These differ for each dragon. ‘Area Effect’ spells are slow but cover a large area and drain your opponent’s life while they are inside them. ‘Freeze’ spells will not damage your opponent. They are used to slow your opponent down and prevent them from casting. They can also be used to create even more powerful rank 3 spells.
