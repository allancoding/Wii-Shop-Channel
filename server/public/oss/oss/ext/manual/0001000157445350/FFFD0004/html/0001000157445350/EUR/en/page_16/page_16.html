Melee

Melee can only be performed by holding down the B Button on the Wii Remote. Then you must swing the Wii Remote to perform a melee attack. Swinging the Wii Remote up or down will result in one off strong attacks. Swinging left and right will result in fast, weaker attacks. The easiest way to get close is to dash towards your opponent while holding the C Button on the Nunchuk.

 

Combos

If you continue to swing the remote left and right rapidly, you will perform a combo.

 

Repelling Roar

Holding back on the control stick will make your dragon perform 'Repelling Roar'. This will repel all spells that are in close range and push the opposing dragon away from you.
