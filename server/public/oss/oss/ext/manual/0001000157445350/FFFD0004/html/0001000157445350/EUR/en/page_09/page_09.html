Dragons

 

Susinuo - Dragon Lord of Water
Physical Description:

The Dragon Lord of Water is the master of the oceans. Turquoise in colour, Susinuo has evolved a large blade like tail and webbed talons. Blessed with four wings and a long flexible body Susinuo is fast and agile.

Susinuo and Jen
Jen used the book of Scale to find Susinuo and told the Dragon Lord of Water of her quest to combine the books release the dragons. Clever Susinuo agreed to help Jen defeat her opponents and unite the Tomes so that dragons can rule Nakiha.

 

Unga - Dragon Lord of Earth
Physical Description:

Formed from the boulders and roots of the world, the Dragon Lord of Earth is the biggest and strongest of all. However, all that weight means Unga is the slowest in the air, making this giant

very stable and very tough. Slow to anger and hard to hurt, Unga is the easiest to control of all the Dragon Lords.

Unga and Elziath

Elziath found the ancient Earth Dragon and wisely advised him to help return the Dragon Lords and their kin back to the elements from which they were created. The gentle giant Unga agreed that the time of the dragons was over and they should return to a place of peace, far away from the wars of man.

 

Pyrozian - Dragon Lord of Fire

Physical Description:

The fiercest of all the Dragon Lords, Pyrozian has the temper of fire; quick to flare into a rage, and the hardest to control. Born from burning lava this huge creature of the inferno has the deadliest flame and most unpredictable nature.

Pyrozian and Vourak

Fire left unattended will run rampant and destroy all it touches and Pyrozian delights in chaos. Vourak traveled to Pyrozianâ€™s volcano and challenged the Dragon Lord to a duel. Pyrozian accepted, delighting in the battle that followed. After much fighting Vourak used the Tome of Flame to enslave the ferocious Dragon Lord and now he holds the leash of this mighty creature of destruction.

 

Kullwraith - Dragon Lord of Storm

Physical Description:

With wings of raw lightning, Kullwraith is fast and temperamental, switching from extreme calm to fury and rage in seconds. The power of the storm provides Kullwraith with the fastest attacks of all the Dragon Lords and the speed of lightning makes the Storm Lord a difficult enemy to fight.

Kullwraith and Muru Moko
Muru Moko found the Kullwraith atop a great mountain during a huge lightning storm. He used the book of Talon and his Storm power to dominate the dragon and command it to defeat all who would oppose him in his quest to become the Dragon Master.
