Getting Started
Hold the Wii Remote sideways as shown on the screen, and press to display the Title screen. Press again to display the Battle Menus screen.

Select one of three menus to move onto a game.

Normal Battle Up to 8 people can use 4 Wii Remotes and 4 Nintendo GameCube controllers to battle at once.
Nintendo Wi-Fi
Connection Battle		 Enjoy Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection battle via "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection." Up to 8 players can battle at once, with up to 4 players per Wii console.

* User registration via "User Information" is required for Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection battles.
User Information Once registered as a user, you can play in Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection battles, register Miis, check battle records, etc.
Credits View names of those involved in the development of Bomberman Blast.

About Miis

You can use Miis from the Mii Channel in Bomberman Blast as follows.

You can register Miis from your Wii console as users, and use them in normal battle. However, Miis cannot be registered until after you've played all battle stages.
