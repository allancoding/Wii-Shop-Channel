Rules
The following 6 types of rules are available for battle.

 

● NORMAL
Defeat your rivals with bombs, and the last man standing wins.

● COUNTDOWN
Instead of being defeated when hit by a bomb blast, a countdown will appear above your head. Once the count reaches zero, you will be out, so try and pass it onto your opponents before that happens.

● TEAMS
Make a team to battle against rival teams. Press right or left to select 2 to 4 teams at the Teams screen.

 * These rules cannot be selected for  Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Battle:  Ranked Battle.

● KING
It's a scramble for the crown. Whoever ends up with it last is the winner.

● SURPRISE
The rules and stage are decided at random, and surprise battles will be played until the designated number of rounds is up.

 * These rules cannot be selected for  Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection Battle:  Ranked Battle.

● AIR RAID
Dodge bombs as they fall from the sky. You can't set bombs when playing with these rules, but you can use Shaker Item Shields for protection.
