Basic Rules & Techniques
Rules

● Flipper
Use the flippers to keep the ball in play and bump it against targets.

● MULTI BALL
When a specific condition is met, multiple balls appear on the playing field. This situation is called "MULTI BALL" and presents the player with an opportunity to rack up the score.

● EXTRA BALL
By achieving a specific number of points, you can add a ball to the number of remaining balls.
This is referred to as an "EXTRA BALL"

Nudge the Machine
By quickly swinging the Wii Remote, you can nudge the machine to slightly change a ball's course. You can nudge in three directions: forward, right, or left.
However, if too much motion is applied in this manner, the game will TILT and the player loses all control until the ball in play exits the playing field.
ACTION BALL
Ａn ACTION BALL allows the player to trigger a special action with the ball by pressing . At the outset, only Boost Ball can be used. But after achieving certain conditions, other ACTION BALLs become available.
Boost Ball
Makes your ball speed up, and allows you to inflict more damage against enemies.
Reverse Ball
Reverses the direction of your ball.
Split Ball
Splits your ball in 2. The new ball will
disappear after a certain amount of time.
* ACTION BALLs cannot be triggered on top of each other. What's more, an ACTION BALL can only be triggered when the gauge displayed at the bottom of the screen is full.
