STORY MODE
In STORY MODE, the player must battle and defeat enemy aliens. This screen will appear whenever there's saved
data for a stage in progress.
The player can choose either "START OVER" to start from the beginning, or
"CONTINUE" to continue a game in progress.
Defeat all the enemy aliens to clear each stage.
Use all your skill to smash the ball against the enemy aliens. Defeat the boss to clear the boss stage.
When you run out of balls,
it's Game Over.
