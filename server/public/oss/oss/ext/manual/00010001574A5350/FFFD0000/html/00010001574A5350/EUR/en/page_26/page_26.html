Winning the Game

New Winning Conditions

As you can expect these new cards can also change how you can win the game.

 

If the last card revealed by you is an INNER HANDS arrow card and you do not win the hand stacking, you take all of the face-up cards on the table and resume playing.

 

If the last card revealed by you is the PASS IT FORWARD card, you immediately get all of the face-up cards on the table and keep playing.

 

Now you know everything you need to play with the Jungle Speed expansion.

 

Play a couple of rounds to get used to the game, or go through the tutorials so you can master the basics.

 

Have fun!
