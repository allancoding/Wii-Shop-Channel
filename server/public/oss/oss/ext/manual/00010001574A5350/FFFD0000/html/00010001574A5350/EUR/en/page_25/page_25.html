Pass It Forward

When the PASS IT FORWARD card is revealed, forget all about the card you have in front of you.

 



This card, when drawn, will be shown to all players and then placed in The Pot. The Pass It Forward ring will appear around the Totem to indicate that the card is in effect.

 

Once a PASS IT FORWARD is revealed, you now use the face-up card of the player to YOUR LEFT as your card.

 

This card is in effect until a duel is won, somebody grabs or knocks over the Totem or another Arrow or Special Card appears.

 

You only participate in a Duel if the card to your left has a matching symbol. However, your own cards are at stake.

 

Special Situations Involving the Arrow Cards

During an ALL FLIP, if a COLOR MATCH and a PASS IT FORWARD card are revealed at the same time, they are both in effect at the same time. You must match using the card to your left AND use color to match.

 

During an ALL FLIP, if a FAST GRAB and an INNER HANDS card are revealed at the same time, the first player to grab or stack hands determines which card is in effect.
