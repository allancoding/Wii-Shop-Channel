Duels & Mistakes

Duels

The key to winning at Jungle Speed is your ability to recognize card patterns. You need to wait for Duels to happen, that’s your best chance to get rid of your cards.

 

Duels happen when your face-up card symbol card matches one or more symbol cards that are already on the table.

 



 

The first player in the Duel to grab the Totem wins!

 

The winner of a Duel gives their face-up cards to the loser, who then takes all of their cards and puts them back into their draw stack.

 

Additionally, if there are any cards under the Totem, the loser takes those cards as well.

 

Mistakes

If you grab the Totem when you should not or knock over the totem, you get all of the face-up cards on the table. These cards get placed into your draw stack.

 

This means that you take all other players’ face-up cards and all cards in The Pot.
