Party Mode

There are several boards to choose from in Party Mode.

There are 6 normal challenges:

Strength

Power up to break through the wooden planks and hit all the pins. You have 2 chances.

Spin

Counterbalance the effects of the tilted lane with your spin and knock down all the pins. You have 2 chances.

Accuracy

Hit a special set of pins. You have 2 chances.

High Score

Hit at least a certain amount of pins in 3 tries.

Rail

Conquer the railway and hit all the pins. You have 2 chances.

Springboard

Use your skill to hit all the pins hiding behind the springboard. You have 2 chances.

There are 3 special tiles:

Special events can happen during random challenges.

Step Forward

Finish a challenge to go forward a certain number of steps.

Step Backward

Finish a challenge or else you'll move back a certain number of steps.

VS

Choose one opponent for a head-to-head game! If you win, you will switch places with the loser if he or she is further ahead on the board!
