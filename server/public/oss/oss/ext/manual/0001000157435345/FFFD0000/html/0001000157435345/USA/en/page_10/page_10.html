Contest Type

● 9 Ball
From low to high, sink the balls numbered 1-9 in order into a pocket. The player who sinks the 9 ball at the end of the game is the winner.

● 8 Ball
The balls are divided into two groups: 1-7 and 9-15. Players first need to sink their entire set of balls into a pocket, and then sink the 8 ball to win the game. A player who sinks the 8 ball before that point will lose the game.

● Rotation
Sink the 1-15 balls in order. Players receive points equal to each ball's number. The first player to reach the number set beforehand is the winner.

● Snooker
Alternate pocketing the red balls and the other colored balls to receive points. Fouls award points to your opponent. Whoever has the highest score when the table is cleared is the winner.
