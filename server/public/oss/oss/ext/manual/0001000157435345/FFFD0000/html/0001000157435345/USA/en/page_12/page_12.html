Matchup
Depending on the game, up to 4 players may participate. You can also set handicaps for fouls other than scratches, according to player skill levels.

● Assigning Wii Remotes
You can use a single Wii Remote for each player to use in turn. When your shot is complete, you can pass the Wii Remote to the next player.

● Select a Mii
You can display a Mii of your choice during the game. You can also play without selecting a Mii.

● Matchup Play
Depending on the contest you select, you can play against 1-4 players in Individual Play or form two teams and square off in Team Play.

● Rules
Some games allow you to choose from a number of optional rules. Some also have rules that are unique to "CueSports - Pool Revolution".

● Bank Shots
When playing a contest, the order of play is determined by a bank shot. Shoot the cue ball from behind the head line towards the foot rail. The player whose shot returns closest to the head rail starts the contest.

● Winning Conditions
Game wins are determined by the number of racks won or by points. You can set the winning conditions before starting a game.

● Replay
You can save replays of particularly good shots you made during a match. You can review a replay as many times as you wish in Replay Mode.
You can save up to 32 replays.
