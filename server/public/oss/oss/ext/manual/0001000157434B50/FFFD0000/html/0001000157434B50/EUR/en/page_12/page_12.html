Offense


Each team has an attack time in which to carry out one of the three basic attacks: Bomb, Weight and Plant. When this elapses, it's your opponents turn!



You carry out an offensive move by first choosing an attack using . A template consisting of dots will appear. You have to draw over the dots using the cursor. If you press the Cancel button whilst doing this you will abandon the attack.



When the template has been drawn, the attack is ready. Now wait for a suitable moment and then launch the offense by clicking on the attack button with . You should always keep your eye on the timer. If your time runs out, then your offensive move will be abandoned.

There are two different kinds of attack:

  • Basic Attacks



    These attacks are subject to the attack time and can only be carried out by the team whose turn it currently is. Basic attacks are the Bomb, Weight and Plant.

  • Special Attacks



    Special attacks are independent of attack times and can be deployed by both teams as soon as they are activated. Whichever team is first will carry out the special attack and then it will no longer be available. Special attacks are Lightning, UFO, Sea monster and Ghost.

Note: You can find out more about different attacks and how to defend them in the Chicken coop.
