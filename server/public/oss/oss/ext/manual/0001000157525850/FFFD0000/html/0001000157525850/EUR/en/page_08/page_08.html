Characters
Mega Man

A robot created by Dr. Light to be his lab assistant. He is a young boy who always strives to do what's right. Once again he has asked to be modified for battle to protect the world.

Dr. Light

A brilliant scientist whose countless robotic creations have made him the de facto expert on robotics. He cares for Mega Man and Roll as if they were his own children, and his fervent desire is to see a world free of pain and strife.

Dr. Wily

A highly intelligent scientist responsible for numerous past evil deeds. He has been studying the recent roboenza outbreak, but what does he hope to gain?

Roll

A robot assistant created by Dr. Light. She's like Mega Man's kid sister. She's particularly good at cooking and housekeeping.

Auto

A robot assistant created by Dr. Light. He spends his days researching ways to help Mega Man in his fights.

Rush

Mega Man's trusted canine robot friend and sidekick.

Proto Man

An early prototype robot designed by Dr. Light. He has come to Mega Man's aid a number of times.

Bass

A robot design by Dr. Wily. He is Mega Man's greatest rival.

Treble

A robotic dog that assists Bass.
