Special Weapons

When you defeat a boss, you will be able to use that boss' weapon. Using a Special Weapon decreases your Weapon Energy Level for that weapon.

TRIPLE BLADE

Fires 3 blades in different directions. Jump and fire to change the angle.

WATER SHIELD

Creates a spinning shield of water droplets. Press the Fire button again to shoot the droplets.

COMMANDO BOMB

Fires a projectile that can be aimed with the +Control Pad. The flight path can only be changed twice.

CHILL SPIKE

Fires a cooling gel that solidifies into ice spikes when it impacts a surface. A direct hit can momentarily freeze an enemy, but not all enemies will be affected.

THUNDER WOOL
Releases a wooly cloud that fires lightning.

REBOUND STRIKER

Fires a projectile that can bounce off of surfaces. Its destructive power increases with every bounce. Use the +Control Pad to aim it when fired.

WHEEL CUTTER

Pressing Fire will make the spinning cutter appear. Release the button to fire the cutter.

SOLAR BLAZE
Fires a flame projectile that splits with one half going left and the other going right.

RUSH COIL

Jump on Rush's back to reach high places. Available from the beginning of the game.
Can only be used by Mega Man.

RUSH JET

Jump on Rush's back and fly through the air. Obtained during the course of the game.
Can only be used by Mega Man.

PROTO COIL

Jump on this coil to reach high places. Available from the beginning of the game.
Can only be used by Proto Man.
PROTO JET

Jump on this jet to fly through the air. Available from the beginning of the game.
Can only be used by Proto Man.
