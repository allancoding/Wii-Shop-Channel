Objectives

Pop all the bugz on screen. To pop the bugz you need to match the color of your balls to the color of the bugz. When you match 3 or more of the same color, all the adjacent bugz of the same color will pop.

Some bugz will have a circular icon on top of them. The icons are special power-ups. When popping a bug with a power-up, the power-up will float down in your direction. Touch the power-up and you will get a special ability. By popping a chain of more than 5 bugz you create a combo which emits falling stars. Collecting falling stars will increase the amount of power-ups on the bugz.
