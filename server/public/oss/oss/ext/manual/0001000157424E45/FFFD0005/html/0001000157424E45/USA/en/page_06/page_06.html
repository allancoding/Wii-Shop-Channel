Playing and Saving

Up to five customers will come into your shop each day for a haircut. They will sit in the waiting room, hoping to be picked next.


On the table in front of the customers are a number of items to help you in your daily barbering life.


You may be lucky enough to be given a practice plant on which you can hone your barbering skills or challenge other barbers to tests of speedy perfection.


Pressing on Otto the Goldfish's glass bowl allows you to “Save and Quit” (back to the profile selection screen), “Save and Continue,” or “Continue without Saving.” Keep an eye on Otto: he'll perform acrobatics if he thinks it would be a good idea for you to save your game.


The brown Bumper Book of Barbering Business contains a wealth of useful information about your tools, customers, and hair styles. You can also use it to make customer appointments, or just wait to see who turns up.
