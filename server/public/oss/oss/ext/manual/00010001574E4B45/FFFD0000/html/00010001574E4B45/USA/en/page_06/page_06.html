Controls
Niki - Rock 'n' Ball can be controlled both in Wii style as well as in Retro style.
To toggle between these two types of control, press or set this in the Pause Menu.

 

Wii style
 
Hold the Wii Remote upright and tilt it left or right to accelerate in the corresponding direction.


Swing the Wii Remote up quickly
or press to jump.

Hold down to transform Niki Ball into Niki Rock.

Release to change back again into Niki Ball.

Press to display the Pause Menu.

 

Retro style

 
Hold the Wii Remote in a horizontal position.


Press or to accelerate in the corresponding direction.


Press to jump.


Hold down to transform Niki Ball into Niki Rock.
 

Release to change back into Niki Ball.

Press to display the Pause Menu.
