Game Selection Menu


In the Game Selection menu, there are three kinds of icons:

those representing locked games

Note: If icon background is red, you do not have enough points to unlock the game.

those representing unlocked games,

and a third kind, representing games you have completed with the minimum score. They have a star attached.

You can scroll the games, just by moving the cursor to the desired edge of screen.


To select a game just point at it with the Wii Remote. You will see the high score for unlocked games and the unlock price for the games that still are locked. Press to play a game or unlock it.


There is also a special icon that randomly chooses a game for you. Select it and it will choose an unlocked game for you.
