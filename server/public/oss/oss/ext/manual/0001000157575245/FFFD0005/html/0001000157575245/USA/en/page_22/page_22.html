Sending Tracks

*In order for you to send tracks to someone, the recipient must be registered as a Wii Friend. Wii Friends are different from friends registered in the game. Instead, Wii Friends are registered through the Wii address book. Please refer to the Wii Operations Manual for details.

Using WiiConnect24, you can exchange tracks with your Wii Friends.

After selecting Send Track from the Edit Options menu (see page 21), choose a Wii Friend to send the track to (you can also send it to yourself by pressing ).

Enter a message to be sent with your track. When you are finished, select Done and press . Select OK to send.
