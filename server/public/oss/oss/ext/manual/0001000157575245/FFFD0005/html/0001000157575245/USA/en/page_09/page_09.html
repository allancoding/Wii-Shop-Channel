Starting a Game

Create a new profile by choosing an empty slot and entering your profile name. If a profile exists, select the profile you would like to use.

*Once you create a profile, you can change its name or delete it in Options by pressing on the profile screen. Once erased, player data cannot be recovered!

After you select a profile, you will be taken to the main menu.

Main Menu
World Tour Compete in a variety of exciting races through a series of cups around the world.
Nintendo WFC Compete with up to three other players from around the world via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.
Track Editor Create and edit your very own tracks and share them with your Wii friends.
Options Adjust game settings such as sound, display, and controller settings.
