Race Friends

*You will not be able to race friends unless you and at least one other friend register each other's friend code. You can either press while highlighting Race Friends on the Nintendo WFC screen or press on the Friends screen to register friend codes. You can register up to 64 friends.

  1. On this screen you can check the status of your friends.

Not connected to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection or not playing in Race Friends Mode.
Connected to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection and waiting on the Friends Screen in Race Friends Mode.
In a lobby or race. You can join the lobby unless it is already full. Up to four players can join a lobby. Once you join, you can view the race info.
In a lobby or race created by another player who is not on your Friend Roster. You cannot join the lobby.
  1. To join a race, choose a friend with an open lobby and press . To create a lobby, press and then wait for friends.

  1. In the lobby, you can change the color of your bike by pressing . Press to decide. If you want to leave the lobby, press and select Leave Lobby.

*Players from different regions are distinguished by the color of their name. A white name indicates a player from the Americas, a blue name indicates a player from Europe/Australia and a red name indicates a player from Japan. Your name will always be displayed in yellow.

  1. Select an unlocked track or one that you have created. After every player has selected a track, one of the tracks is chosen randomly.

*A track you've created won't be chosen when you select Random.

  1. Once the first player finishes the race, the countdown timer will start and the other players will need to finish the race within 30 seconds or they will be timed-out.

  1. When the race ends, you will earn Friends Points. Better finishes earn more points. Friends Points are used to unlock special designs.

*If you see a transparent player with an unknown race position, it means they are experiencing Internet difficulties. They will remain transparent until their connection has improved.

Attention!

Each profile will receive a 12-digit friend code the first time it is used to access Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. Friend codes cannot be obtained without connecting to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. If your friend code is not displayed, try connecting to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection to see if this resolves the problem.
