Purchase, Place, and
Sell Items

To Purchase and item:

 

In the Catalog, point the Wii Remote at the item you want to buy and hold to select it.


While holding , drag the item around the room.

If you see a green highlight under the furniture, the furniture can be placed in the current spot.

If the highlight is red, the furniture cannot be placed there.

To Place an item:

After you have found the place you want an item, simply release .

Placing an item will complete the sale.

To Rotate an item:

Point the Wii Remote at the furniture you wish to rotate and press .

Continue to press until the item is facing the desired direction.

To Sell an item:

Drag an item you no longer want, to the Recycling Bin located to the right of the screen.
