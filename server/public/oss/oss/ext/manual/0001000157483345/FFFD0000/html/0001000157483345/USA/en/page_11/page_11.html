Catalog
At the top of the screen you will find a Catalog displaying a series of Furniture Tabs.

Here you can access the inventory and purchase items for the room.

To open a Catalog window:

Point the Wii Remote at the desired tab and press .

This will open a pop-up window that displays the furniture in that category.

You can scroll through tabs and windows by pressing or on the Wii Remote.

New Items in the Catalog will become available throughout the game.

You will know an item is new because it will be marked with a green "!" icon.
