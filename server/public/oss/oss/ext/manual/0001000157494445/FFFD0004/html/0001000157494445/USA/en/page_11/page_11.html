Perks, Pick-ups & Omen

Perks

As if you didn’t already have enough bone-jarring weaponry and explosive ammo, you also get to upgrade and improve your abilities throughout the game. The better you play, the bigger the prizes!

Uranium Bullets:

Deal greater damage to enemies than normal bullets.

Reloader:

You will reload your weapons with greater urgency.

Sniper:

Your weapons will fire over a longer distance.

Panic:

The more you are wounded, the faster you will reload!

Bloodlust:

The closer you are to death, the more damage you will deal.

Extra Health:

Build your endurance with 30% extra health.

Bandage:

Instant heal!

Score Master:

Earn more points for every kill with this ability.

Fieldrunner:

Speed is the key to staying alive, and this perk will make you move faster.

Bonus Finder:

Increase your luck and find better bonuses and ammo from the corpses of defeated enemies.

Power Striker:

Push your enemies back with greater force with this perk.

Immortality:

This makes you invulnerable from damage for 30 seconds... but then you die!

Crossbow Master:

Your bow shoots three arrows instead of the normal one.

Large Pockets:

Increase the amount of ammunition you can hold with this handy ability.

Ninja:

Lightning-fast skills ensure you block some enemy attacks like a ninja.

New Weapon:

Upgrade your current weapon and receive a more powerful firearm.

Telekinesis:

Become a bonus magnet, dragging items to you when they are nearby.

Blood Sacrifice:

Lose half of your health but release a smart bomb that kills every monster around you.

Armor:

50% damage reduction is invaluable – but it’s going to stop you moving too much!

Death Dice:

Take a chance and roll the die – a “1” means death for you, but any other number gives you 100,000 points!

Regeneration:

Constantly gain health and avoid death as long as you stay out of the firing line.

Shotgun Master:

You’re able to reload your shotgun in an instant to keep taking down your enemies fast.
Pick-ups

As you venture through increasingly monstrous environments and battle more ferocious foes, you may need some help to stay alive.
Fear not – items such as Health Potions, bombs and speed increase potions will randomly appear from the bodies of your defeated enemies. Use them wisely and prepare for the onslaught...

Health bottle
Gain an instant percentage health.
Gold

Let your wealth grow by picking up gold and buy the best weapons needed.
Item explosion

An instant explosion where everyone nearby vaporizes.
Reloader

Reload time of all weapons gets faster for a short period.
Slomo

The world suddenly slows down temporarily, making it easy to avoid and target enemies.
Speed

Gives extra moving speed for a short amount of time, creating advantage to avoid being hit.
Omen

 

Perks and Pick-ups are quite easy to get, however Omen are not because only Dracula drops these when he dies. Although it's hard to get them, it's your ticket to reach new ranks!
