Getting started

Introduction
Are you a vampire Hunter? Are you ready to battle legions of undead foes including zombies, ghouls, werewolves and more?

You will need to wield an arsenal of jaw-dropping powerful weaponry with skill and intelligence. Uncover devastating firepower such as Machine Guns, Buzz-

Saws and Flamethrowers, choosing upgrades for them as well as unique new perks for your character as your enemies increase in number and ferocity.


You are a tough customer but beware - Lord Dracula is always on the hunt and you are his prey!

How to play


In this game the Wii Remote is used in vertical position.

Pointer:
Aim at enemies that are approaching you.

Perk:
If the perk ready icon is highlighted press the A Button to activate one (or the A Button on the Nintendo GameCube Controller).

Shoot:
Press the B Button to shoot (move the C stick on the Nintendo GameCube Controller or the R Stick on the Classic Controller). The type of gun you are currently holding will then be fired.

Next weapon:
Use the Z Button or the R Button on the Classic Controller and the Nintendo GameCube Controller to select the next weapon.

Previous weapon:
Use the C Button or the L Button on the Classic Controller and the Nintendo GameCube Controller to select the previous weapon.

Pause:
Use the + Button or START/PAUSE on the Nintendo GameCube Controller, to pause the game.

Movement:
Use the Control Stick on the Nunchuk, the L Stick on the Classic Controller or the Control Stick on the Nintendo GameCube controller to move your character.

*Note: This game supports the rumble feature. To turn the rumble feature on or off for the Wii Remote, enter the HOME Menu or for the Nintendo GameCube Controller, please go to the options menu in the game.
