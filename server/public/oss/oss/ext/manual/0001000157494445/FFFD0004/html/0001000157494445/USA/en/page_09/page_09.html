Game modes & Locations

Game Modes

Do you like an endless supply of ammo with a non-stop stream of attackers coming?
How about a scarier mode where the monsters creep
up without warning to surprise and attack you from all angles? Or maybe you like to role-play and have more control over your powerful new skills and abilities. "Dracula - Undead Awakening" offers all this and much more...

 

Rush

One weapon, unlimited ammo, and loads of monsters. No power ups and perks. Get ready to show those bloodthirsty beasts what you’ve got in this desperate battle for survival.

 

Survival

As your enemies gradually grow in strength and numbers, you can choose powerful new perks and ability upgrades, at the same time as accessing awesome new weaponry.

 

Super Survival
Just like Survival Mode, but bigger and badder! Use powerful new bonuses like Explosive Blast, Haste, and Rage to defeat enemies that are greater in number, and each one is stronger.

 

Wave Attack
Prepare for an onslaught! Monsters will attack in waves, dropping valuable gold which can be used to buy unique new perks, upgrades and supplies after each wave.

 

Locations

 

"Dracula - Undead Awakening" takes place across a variety of rich brooding environments. At first silence ominously

reigns and everything stays eerily still... Until ravenous monsters begin jumping out and attacking you in droves with their fangs, claws and magic!

 

Frozen Earth
A harsh environment populated only by Arctic winds and frozen earth. Once you arrive though, things come to life and you’ll face an army of dangerously deformed wildlife.

 

Grave Park
In this isolated, eerie graveyard you’ll see plenty of gruesome décor and strangely disturbed tombs, until recently inhabited by the numerous angry undead enemies craving your blood!

 

Castle Hall
Within such stately grandeur hides a heart of darkness. Diabolical demons and unearthly beasts await you, followed by specters and vampires aplenty.
