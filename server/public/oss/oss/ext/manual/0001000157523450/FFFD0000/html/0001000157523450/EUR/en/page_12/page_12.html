Item Mode

Item Mode is identical to "Versus", with the addition that items can be collected throughout the levels. Each item has a different function. Most are helpful, but others are best avoided.

Manually activated items

Use the item button to activate the following items:

Gun: shoot the cars in front of you to slow them down.

Oil slick: you can leave 3 oil slicks on the track to thwart your opponents. Try to remember where you put them, or the joke could be on you.

Invisibility: your car won't be affected by your opponents' items for 5 seconds, but you won't be able to pick up items either.

Super speed: makes your car incredibly fast! But beware, your car returns to normal as soon as you hit something (including the brakes).
Automatically activated items

These items are automatically activated when you collect them:

Speed up: get a 10-second engine upgrade.

Random: changes all the collectible items on the track.

Reverse: your car will suddenly turn 180 degrees. Try to avoid this item!
