Investigating the Crime

 

Once you arrive in a location, take a good look around. To do this, move the cursor to where you want to go, and press .

 

The view of the street will pan right or left as your game character walks along.

 

If you meet someone on the street, move the cursor to the person to find out if he or she wants to say something to you. The cursor will change to a speech bubble.

 

  • Press once to see what he or she says.

     

  • Press twice to see the entire dialog at once.

     

  • Press three times to skip the dialog.

     

Sometimes the cursor will change to an arrow over a building. That means you need to take a look inside. You may find something important! Press to enter the building.
