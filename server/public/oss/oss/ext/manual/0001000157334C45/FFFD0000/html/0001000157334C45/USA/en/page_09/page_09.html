Traveling Around the World

 

To travel to a location, press to open the ACME Phone menu, move the cursor to Maps, and then press .

 

 

There are two tabs. On the World Map, you can choose a city to visit. On the City Map, you can choose a location in a city to visit.

 

To go to a city or location, place the cursor where you want to go, and then press .

New places will become available as you gather clues in your investigation.

 

If you haven't yet visited a city or location, its name and location will be displayed in blue.
