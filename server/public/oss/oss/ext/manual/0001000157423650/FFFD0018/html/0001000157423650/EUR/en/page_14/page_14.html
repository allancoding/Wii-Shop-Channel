Game interface
 

Cursor

The hand is your cursor. The number displayed in the hand indicates the Player (from Player 1 to Player 4). The hand indicates where the Wii Remote is pointing.

Note! In the Light Quiz, your cursor becomes a special colored ring:
*Orange for Player 1
*Blue for Player 2
*Green for Player 3
*Pink for Player 4
 

Time

This indicates the time remaining to answer the question.
 

Topic of the Quiz

This displays the topic of the question that you are currently answering.
 

Cash

This shows the amount of cash you will win for correctly answering the question first.
 

Question

This is where the current question of the quiz is displayed.
 

Number of Questions Answered

This displays the number of questions already answered out of a total of 7 questions per quiz.
 

Possible Answers

In the Classic Quiz, you will have 4 answers, located in blocks, to choose from. Pick the one you think is correct.
