Option Screens
 

After selecting a game, you’ll be brought to the Options Menu for that particular game.

 

 

Score (X01 & Poker) – This selection shows the starting score that players start at (301, 501, 701, or 901).

Score (Cricket) – In the game of Cricket, this selection turns the scoring option on and off.

 

Dbl in-out (X01) – This selection turns the

“Double In” / “Double Out” option on and off.

 

Mode

Normal – A standard game of turn based darts.

 

VS – A high speed game variation.  1-2 players compete at the same time, the standard game rules and options apply but all players play at once in a race to finish first. 

 

Scenery – Choose the location to have your competition (Pub, Lodge, or Lounge).

 

Players – Changes the player selection to CPU-Easy, CPU-Med, CPU-Hard, human Player, or off.

1-4 players can play in “Normal Mode”, while 1-2 players can play in “VS” mode.

 

Music - Turns the music on and off.

Announcer  – Turns the commentary on and off.

 

Back – Return to the Main Menu

Start – Start the game with the selections that you have made.

 

Tip - Synchronize 1-4 Wii Remotes that will be used for multiple players.  The Wii Remotes that are active are shown in the top-center of the screen.

 

 

In-game Options

 

To access the options screen during game play, press the + Button.  From this screen you can turn music & vocals on/off, change to the next song, restart the game, quit to the main menu, & instructions for the current game.

 

 

 

 

Darts Selection

During the first throw of each players turn, they must choose their Dart to play with.
