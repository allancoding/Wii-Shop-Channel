Ants, Colonies, and Enemies

 

Ants: Ants are generated by spending Food Points.

Ants level up by being trained. Ants will evolve in different ways based on how they are trained.

 

Colonies: You can build more chambers by completing certain missions.

Place the cursor over a colony, and shake the Wii Remote while holding the A Button to draw out ants.

 

Enemies: You may encounter ladybugs, spiders and other creatures.

They may eat your food or attack your ants.
