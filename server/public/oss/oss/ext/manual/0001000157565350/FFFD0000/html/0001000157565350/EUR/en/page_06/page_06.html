Introducing the game
Gods vs Humans
Genre: Strategy
Multiplayer: 2 players
Scenario
The Humans are attempting to overthrow the Gods by brazenly building Towers to reach their realm and invade it. However, the Gods are determined not to let this arrogance go unpunished!
Objective
Embody a God and destroy the Foundations or all the Levels of the Humans’ Towers before they can reach the gates to your realm.
The game
Humans: Humans are organized into different Units; each Unit must accomplish a very specific task to contribute to the Tower's construction. Humans work more or less efficiently according to their Mood.
Towers: they can be destroyed entirely and are comprised of Foundations, ten Levels and a Summit. Five different types of Levels are available.
Gods: you embody the major Gods of the Egyptian, Norse, Japanese and Greek & Roman Mythologies.
Powers: use your divine Powers to attack the Tower or distract the Humans from their task.
Respect gauge: your Respect gauge fills up more or less quickly according to the humans' Mood, and is emptied whenever you use your divine Powers.
Strategy: use your Powers to construct tactics and strategies, and unleash different attacks on the Tower according to the Human Units’ presence and the Levels that have been built. Then, choose how you wish to claim victory, according to the situation at hand – by destroying the Tower’s Foundations or all its Levels.
The game modes
Adventure: experience a divine adventure; discover four Mythologies, embody their major Gods and fight your way through 60 game levels!
Free Game: create a game that has no ties to the scenario by selecting a difficulty level, a Mythology and a God among those you have unlocked.
Duel: pit your might against another player’s on your console and be the first to destroy your Tower or defeat your opponent!
Challenges: alone or with another player, meet the Challenges that you are facing!
