Game Screen
World Map
Karma and Negative Power

The Karma and Negative Power (NP) in your possession.
Menu

● Start
Select a stage to attack and kick off the battle.

● Upgrade
Use Karma to raise the maximum level of the monsters in your army.

● Expand
Use Karma to expand your tower, increasing the maximum number of floors you can place during battle.

● Item
Look at the items currently in your possession.

● Clothing
Change the clothing Mira wears. Different clothing can affect her skills in different ways.
Stage Information

A display of the number and type of adventurers coming to invade your tower, as well as the amount of Karma or any special rewards (floors, monsters, items, etc.) you receive for winning the battle.
Cursor

Use this to select a stage to invade.
Stage
Stage Information

The name of the stage and the remaining number of invading adventurers.
Negative Power

The amount of NP remaining.
Menu

● Place Floor
Place a floor (with Artifact) in the tower.

● Summon
Summon monsters to the floors you have placed

● Power Up
Level up monsters you have upgraded in the world map. (You cannot level up monsters that have not been upgraded on the world map.)

● Use Item
Use an item in your inventory.

● Information
View information on the floors, monsters, and adventurers in the tower.

● Retreat
Halt the attack and return to the world map. You can also choose to restart the stage.
Incoming Adventurer Information

A report on invading adventurers, as well as the levels, jobs, and party information for the next wave of invading adventurers.
Tower Details

A display of the artifacts, monsters, and adventurers on all floors.
Tower Floor
HP and Action Wait Gauge

A gauge that shows the HP, level, tribe, and remaining wait time for Artifacts, monsters, and adventurers. When the gauge is full, an action will take place.
Battle Time Gauge

The remaining battle time for an adventurer. When this gauge reaches zero, the adventurer will move up to the next floor.
