Getting Started

Basic Rules

You control your tank's cannon while trying to stop the army of the enemy. The goal of the game is to defeat the enemy's armies, and to keep the number of soldiers that gets through your defense as little as possible. You have a variety of bonuses at your disposal that will help you with the task.

Victory/Defeat

There are two game modes: 'Campaign mode' and 'Survival mode'. Each of the modes is divided into three levels of difficulty. In 'Survival mode', the goal is to repeal enemy's army - The longer you succeed in doing it, the higher the score you get! 'Campaign mode' lets you play as a lone front tanker attacked by the army of the enemy.The player loses if too many enemy soldiers pass through the line of defence.

Multiplayer

'Survival mode' allows you to play up to 4 players, but it does not mean that it will be any easier. The enemies are stronger, and all the bonuses for all players act simultaneously. Like on a real battlefield, you will need good teamwork.
