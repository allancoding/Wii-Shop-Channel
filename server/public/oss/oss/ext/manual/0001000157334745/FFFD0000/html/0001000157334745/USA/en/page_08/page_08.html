The Game Menu

You can open the Game Menu by pressing .
Here, you can save your game or change your option settings. This menu is accessible whenever you are allowed to open the Court Record within the game.
Save

Save your progress.
Your progress will be saved in the Court Record file you chose at the beginning of the game.
Options

Change your game settings. Navigate your options with , and press to change the setting. Press to save your settings and return to the Game Menu.

  • You can adjust the volume for the Wii Remote speaker and the rumble feature through the Wii Remote Settings screen, which is accessible through the HOME Menu.
Motion Controls

Set whether or not you will use the motion sensors in the Wii Remote for specific controls in game.
Flashing Reduction

Turn the on-screen flashing reduction filter on or off.
Title Screen

Return to the Title Screen. (Your progress will not be saved.)
Back

Return to the game.
