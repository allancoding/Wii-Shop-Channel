The Wii Speak Channel

The Wii Speak™ Channel allows you to use the Wii Speak™ accessory to talk with friends around the world, send them voice messages, and more.

● In order to use the Wii Speak Channel, each household must have a broadband Internet connection, a Wii™ console, and a Wii Speak accessory, and must have downloaded the Wii Speak Channel. For details on how to connect and use your Wii Speak accessory, please refer to this Wii Speak manual.

● Before chatting, you and the person you want to converse with must register each other as Wii Friends. A Wii Friend is anyone whose Wii number you’ve registered in your Wii address book.
