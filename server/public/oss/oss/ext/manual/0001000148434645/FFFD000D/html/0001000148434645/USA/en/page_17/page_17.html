Parental Controls

Parental Controls can be used to limit a user’s access to content or to participate in features that parents or guardians may consider unsuitable. To use the Parental Controls to restrict the exchange of messages between Wii Friends using the Wii Speak accessory, please take the following steps.

  1. From the Wii Menu, select Wii, which will take you to the Wii Options menu.
  2. Navigate to Wii Settings, then Parental Controls, then choose Other Settings. From here, please set “Limited online use of Wii Message Board and access to certain user-generated content” to Restrict.
    *Depending on the Wii Menu version being used, the names of fields and buttons may be slightly different than what appears here.
