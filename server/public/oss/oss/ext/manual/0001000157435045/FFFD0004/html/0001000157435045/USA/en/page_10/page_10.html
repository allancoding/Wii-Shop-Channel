Copter Controls

  • Point the Wii Remote upwards to keep your copter level in flight.
  • Gently tilt the Wii Remote in the direction you want the copter to fly.
  • - Fire Missiles (this option is only available on missile-required levels)
  • - Hold to accelerate / Release to decelerate
  • - Display the Pause Menu

 

Wii Balance Board Controls

 

The Wii Balance Board must be used in conjunction with the Wii Remote.

 

When using the Wii Balance Board lean in the direction you want to fly. Lean forward to fly down to the canyon floor, and lean backward to fly upwards. At the same time use the standard acceleration and missile controls buttons while flying.

 

Instead of angling the Wii Remote, the Wii Balance Board will be used for controlling the copter's movement.
