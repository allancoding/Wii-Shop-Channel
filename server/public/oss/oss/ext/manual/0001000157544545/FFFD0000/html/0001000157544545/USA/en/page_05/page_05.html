World of the Slimes
Do you know the Slimes?
Dear players and parents, welcome to the weird and wonderful world of the Slimes. Somewhere deep in the heart of the universe floats a flourishing little green planet covered in forests, mountains and valleys. Riddled with huge caves where many large and powerful rivers flow, this planet is home to a small, yet remarkable, and peaceful people who live in harmony with it: the Slimes.
Who are the Slimes?
The Slimes are honest and brave little creatures with a friendly temperament. They are more or less round in shape and are unusual in that they move around by rolling or bouncing on the ground or by gripping onto the edges of rock with their four extendable arms.
Adventuring With the Slimes
Since time immemorial, the Slimes have lived peacefully on their planet, spending most of their time growing and harvesting their assorted produce, followed by well-deserved rest. The Axons may have disturbed their routines at the time, but that's all water under the bridge. So there the Slimes were, living life nice and easy, until one fateful day when the sky filled with dark, destructive rain clouds.
With Mooky the Watchman, set out on your exploration of the surprising planet of the Slimes, and discover the mysterious origin of the black clouds that have blocked out the sun, and of the rain that is poisoning the crops.
In your daring exploration, you will encounter a countryside full of traps to avoid and puzzles to solve. Eliminating the clouds and saving the harvests requires dexterity, speed, a sharp mind, patience, and perseverance. Alongside Mooky, discover and live moments crammed with suspense and action. Tales of Elastic Boy is a creative way to chill out and relax.
The Characters
  • Mooky :

Mooky is the hero of the adventure. Mooky is smart, brave, patient, quick and nimble, and he will lead you on a trail of traps and pitfalls across his planet. On his way he will have to demonstrate his best qualities to overcome challenges and restore radiant sunshine to his planet.

  • Slimy :

Slimy is the boss of the Slimes. He is a great sage who shows wonderful discernment in choosing Mooky the Watchman to solve the mystery of the black clouds. He is also an excellent teacher and expert in all techniques of movement and combat. He is the one who will train Mooky before he sets out.

  • Skamy :

Skamy is also a lookout guard, and is sent by Slimy on a reconnaissance mission to the old temple. Skamy is very nimble and has precious movement techniques that he'll teach Mooky when they meet.

  • Slinky :

Slinky is Slimy's right-hand man. He has a mysterious side and is very full of himself. He too, is sent by Slimy on reconnaissance. In return for new movement techniques which will come in very handy to Mooky in his quest, Slinky will hand over his mission to Mooky, because it's not really his cup of tea.
The Dangers
  • The Pinchu :

The Pinchu is a crab who'll keep to himself as long as he's not disturbed. If he's touched, he uses his pincers to grab the intruder and hurl him miles away. If Mooky doesn't wriggle from his clutches soon enough, he'll lose an energy bubble. The Pinchu is vulnerable to Hammer Attacks, but only to his head. This turns him into a nugget.

  • The Piku :

The Piku is a kind of big scaredy-pants butterfly that rolls up into a ball and bristles its spikes when it feels threatened. If Mooky touches it, he loses an energy bubble. When the Piku isn't rolled up in a spiky ball, it is vulnerable to a sudden blow, such as a Hammer Attack. When attacked this way, it will turn into a nugget.

  • The Scratchor :

This itchy-scratchy caterpillar is very aggressive and gets around on the silk thread it weaves and gets quite wrapped up in its work. The Scratchor is very sensitive to the vibrations of the thread and will pounce on any intruder who disturbs it. The caterpillar then stings its prey and paralyzes it, which could make Mooky lose an energy bubble. The Scratchor is invincible, so Mooky had better steer clear and not disturb it.

  • The Shaboom :

The Shaboom is a cannon-plant which hides away in the nooks and crannies of the walls. If an intruder enters its detection zone, it will aim its cannon at him, and launch an explosive spike ball which, if it hits its target, will make Mooky lose an energy bubble. It is possible to return the projectile back to sender with a well placed Punch. If you do this, the plant will be turned into an energy bubble.

  • The Gobble Plant :

The Gobble Plant sits in the cracks and hollows of tunnels and caves, and will catch Mooky with its extendible stem if he passes within its vicinity. When it does trap him, Mooky will have to fight like a demon to escape from the plant's mouth; otherwise he will dissolve and lose an energy bubble.

  • The Toxic Plant :

The Toxic Plant is to be found on the ground or hanging from the walls of caves and tunnels. If Mooky hits it too hard, it will open and eject a cloud of toxic pollen which slows down Mooky's abilities. But it will not make him lose energy.

  • The Spike-ball :

This dangerous protuberance is to be found hooked to the walls of tunnels and often gets in the way. Make sure Mooky doesn't touch it, or he'll lose an energy bubble.
