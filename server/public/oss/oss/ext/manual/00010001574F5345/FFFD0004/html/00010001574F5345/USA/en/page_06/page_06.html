Controls

●Slot Car Controls
If you press the A Button (or B Button)

the slot car will run.
If you let go it will stop.

If you have the Nunchuk, tilt the Control Stick

(or press the Z Button) to do the same motion.

●Course Out
When your speed is too

fast and you go into a curb,

you may fall off.
You will not be able to regain control until you are put back onto the course.
