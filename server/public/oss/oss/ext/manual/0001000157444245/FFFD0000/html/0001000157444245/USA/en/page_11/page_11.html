Dogs and doghouses
Dogs are categorized as shown below. An empty doghouse is required to have a new dog.
My Dog A dog that you have living in a doghouse. A new puppy will become a My Dog.
Rental dog A dog that you have living in a rental doghouse. A rental dog from the dog ranch “Doggybrook Pines”.
Two doghouses will be assigned to each handler at the beginning of the game. These two doghouses are free of charge, and thus referred to as “free doghouses”. Purchase additional doghouses as add-on content, and increase the number of doghouses to accommodate more dogs. Since eight rental doghouses are prepared for each handler, you can have eight rental dogs at the same time.

● If you have more dogs than you can handle...

You can hand over some of your retired dogs on hand to the dog ranch called “Doggybrook Pines” when the number of doghouses reaches a certain number. You can also send away retired rental dogs to make availability in the rental doghouses when the number of your rental dogs exceeds a certain limit. Keep in mind that once you send dogs away, you won’t be able to get them back later.
