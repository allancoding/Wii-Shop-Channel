One-dog race
This section explains how to play “One-dog race” where you will compete against the CPU.
Select a race
  1. Select a grade of the race first. You can only select a race of the same or lower grade than your dog. If you pick a race of a grade that is so much lower than your dog’s, you might get booed.
  1. Select a race next. Please be aware that some races are female-only.

● Button Controls

A Button Command your dog to “Go”. Press the A Button while swinging the Wii Remote to give a stronger command. It’s a command to give when you want your dog to run faster. It reduces the dog’s strength.
B Button Command your dog to “Jump”. Press the B Button while swinging the Wii Remote to give a stronger command. It’s a command to give when you want your dog to jump. It reduces the dog’s strength.
- Button Command your dog to go “Slow”. Press the - Button while swinging the Wii Remote to give a stronger command.
+Control Pad Moves the camera. Use left/right on the +Control Pad to move the camera to the sides of the dog. Use up/down on the +Control Pad to zoom in and out on the dog.
+ Button Display/Hide the minimap.
After a dog responds to your command, an icon will appear over the dog’s head. The higher the value for one of the dog’s abilities, “Friend”, is, the more responsive your dog is to your command.
