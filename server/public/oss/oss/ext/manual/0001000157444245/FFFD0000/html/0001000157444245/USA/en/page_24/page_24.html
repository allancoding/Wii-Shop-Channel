Purchase add-on content
Doghouses are available as add-on content. You can have a new puppy born through breeding live in a doghouse that you purchase.

※Note: You can purchase doghouses in the game. Wii Points are required to make a purchase.

Steps to purchase add-on content
  1. Visit the doghouse shop, “Hot dog”.
  1. Select a doghouse that you’d like to purchase.
