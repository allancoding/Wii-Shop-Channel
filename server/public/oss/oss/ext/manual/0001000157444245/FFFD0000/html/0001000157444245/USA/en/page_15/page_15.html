Training
You can increase the skill level of your dogs by training them. The following training menus are available, so choose one that satisfies your dog’s needs.
Disc Especially “Speed” and “Stamina” increase.
Jump rope Especially “Physical” and “Jump” increase.
Rabbit chase Especially “Acceleration” and “Turn” increase.
Chase Adjustability for grass, dirt and sand increases.
Walk Recovers from exhaustion more easily and “Friend” increases.

● Walk your dog to get him/her refreshed

Walking a dog is a special training where your dog can recover from fatigue. Take your dog to a walk after a series of races and trainings.
The training flow
  1. Select a training menu. Read the description of each menu carefully to pick the right training.
  1. You’ll start out with a demonstration.
  1. You can check the result of the training. Your dog’s abilities will change according to the result.
