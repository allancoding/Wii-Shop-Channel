House
The house is the start point of every action of your dog. Select an action from the menu shown below.
Race You can select from “Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection race with random player”, “Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection race with friends” or “One-dog race”.
Training You can select training from “Disc”, “Jump rope”, “Rabbit chase”, or “Walk”.
Retire You can get your dog to retire. This option becomes available once your dog goes through a couple of races and trainings.

● When you pet your dog...

Switch the pointer to the “hand pointer”, and pet your dog to raise its morale.
