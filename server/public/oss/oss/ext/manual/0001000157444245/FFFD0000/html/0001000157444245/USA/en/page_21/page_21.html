Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection race
You can enjoy race battles against your mutually-registered friends using Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. Up to eight players can participate in a race at the same time. There are two types of races: one hosted by you, and the one hosted by your friend.
When you host a race
You will host a race and have your friends participate in the race.
  1. Select “Host a race”.
  1. Select a grade like you did for “One-dog race”.
  1. Select a race.
  1. Set the standby time before the race begins.
  1. Wait for your friends to join the race.
  1. The race begins when all participants are ready, or the time comes.
Friend races
Joins races that your friends are hosting.
  1. Select the button "Participate in a race hosted by your friend".
  1. Select one of the races that your friends are hosting.
  1. Wait for others to join.
  1. Once the room is filled or it's time to start the race, the race will begin.
