Dog’s abilities
This section explains how to view each ability of your dog.
Speed The higher the value is, the faster a dog’s maximum speed is.
Acceleration The higher the value is, the quicker a dog reaches its maximum speed.
Physical The higher the value is, the less likely a dog loses balance upon contact with other dogs.
Turn The higher the value is, the easier a dog turns corners.
Jump The higher the value is, the higher a dog can jump.
Stamina The higher the value is, the less likely a dog’s strength decreases.
Friend The higher the value is, the easier it is to raise a dog’s morale, the better the dog’s response to commands is.
1. Power When the bar is filled, the power is at its highest level. As the power decreases, the bar gets shorter. A dog can run even when the power hits zero, but it might get slow, and might not respond to a handler’s command.
2. Spirit The more towards to the right the arrow ▲ is, the higher the spirit is and the more likely a dog responds to a handler’s commands.
There are grades for each dog: the grade ranges from ★3 (low) to ★7 (high). A dog can only participate in a race of lower or the same grade. Winning a race will increase the grade.
