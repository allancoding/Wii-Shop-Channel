Items
Items are placed throughout each map, and can be picked up by simply running over them.
Each item has its own unique capabilities, so choose wisely to best deal with the situation at hand.

* Only 1 item can be held at a time.

Bucket

Throw water from a bucket at your opponents.
Getting drenched by water from a bucket will inflict soak damage, and will also decrease movement speed and jump distance.
Water Bomb

Filled with water and ready to throw! Anyone hit with a Water Bomb will take soak damage and be temporarily stunned.
Bath Towel

Wipe off your shirt to recover from damage.
Skates

Wearing these will increase your movement speed and jump distance.
Chirpy Hammer

Your weapon will change into a Chirpy Hammer, and you'll be temporarily invincible. Anyone hit with a Chirpy Hammer will be temporarily stunned.
Raincoat

Put on a Raincoat and you'll be temporarily invincible.
Cork

Corks will plug weapons placed on the ground. Corked weapons are unable to fire for a certain amount of time.
Banana Peel

Throw a Banana Peel on the ground, and whoever steps on it will slip, temporarily losing control.
Plastic Bottle

Use this bottle of water as extra ammo for your water gun.
Umbrella

Open this umbrella and hold it over yourself, and it will start to rain, causing soak damage to everyone but you.
Sticky Juice

Fill up your water gun with this sticky stuff. Anyone hit by it will have their movement speed and jump distance decreased.
Surprise Device

This device triggers various effects.
The only way to see what it does is to try it out!
