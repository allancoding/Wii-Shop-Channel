Controls
There are 3 control styles available.
Nunchuk Style

*Pressing while tilting to the right or left will cause you to perform a Quick Dodge in the direction you're moving in.

Wii Zapper Style

*Pressing while tilting to the right or left will cause you to perform a Quick Dodge in the direction you're moving in.

Set the Wii Zapper option to ON at Controller Settings via Options.
Classic Style

*Pressing while tilting to the right or left will cause you to perform a Quick Dodge in the direction you're moving in.

● Menus
Select a menu or icon with the Pointer or Control Stick, then confirm your choice. Selecting the Cancel Button will take you back to the previous screen.

● During Gameplay
Use the Pointer or Control Stick to move your sight and character in a certain direction.
