Character Creation
When selecting "Create New" and pressing OK at the Character Selection Screen, you'llbetakentothe Character Creation Screen.
Hereyoucancreateacharacter by choosing from various hairstyles, clothes, etc. After this, press OK and you'll be taken to the Name Entry Screen. Finally, give your character a name, press OK, and you're finished.
By pressing "Change" you can pick a new hairstyle, clothes, etc. for a character you have already created. (Names cannot be changed.)
