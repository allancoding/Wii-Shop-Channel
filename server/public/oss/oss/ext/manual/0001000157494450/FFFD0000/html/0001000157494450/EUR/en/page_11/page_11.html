Enemies & Weaponry

 

Enemies

 
Every one of the Lord of the Undead’s minions is out to attack you. Prepare to be scared and amazed at what is headed your way!

 

Zombie
Your basic slowly walking undead, thirsting for blood.

 

Witch

Slow, immaterial specters which are hard to kill because of their insubstantial nature.

 

Blood sucker
Does exactly what it says on the tin - beware of these fleshy monsters.

 

Snow worm
Very fast, but weak. Yet they are very dangerous in large numbers!

 

Vampire bride
A strong undead foe, bitterly angered and ready to deal a lot of damage.

 

Mosquito spider
Very fast and small creatures that do quite some damage. Stay alerted because they are hard to target!

 

Werewolf
A lethal carnivore with a taste for human flesh. Medium speed, but great reactions.

 

Dracula
The omnipotent Lord of the Undead - he's seemingly immortal and very strong, with a distance-based lightning attack.

Weaponry

 
Enemies in Dracula have one thing in common. Undead, lycanthrope, mutant, and many more – they’re all after your flesh. Not to worry though – you come bearing “gifts”. Well, as long as you think of sharp, explosive, devastatingly destructive weaponry and ammo as “gifts”.

 

Pistol
Your basic weapon. Like other weapons, it has explosive bullets, so you can hit several enemies at once. Low damage and medium reload speed. This gun will provide you with an almost infinite amount of ammo.

 

Rifle
Double-barrelled gun, strong sawed-off shots. Medium damage and reload speed. Nicely strikes enemies back. Can be upgraded using the Rifle Master perk.

 

Arbalet
Fires poisonous bolts at large distance, lethal to undead enemies. If it doesn't kill instantly, slows enemies for a while. Can
be upgraded using Crossbow Master perk to shoot 3 bolts at once.

 

Grenade Launcher
Fires devastating grenades which explode on impact. Low range weapon and long reload time. Huge damage!

 

Machine Gun
A gatling weapon machine gun, fires at least 4000 shots per minute. Extremely effective!

 

Flamethrower
As simple as it is - burns the undead to bones, no one ever passed this. No reload required, however you will easily run out of ammo - use carefully. Easily could be the best weapon.

 

BFG
Simply the most advanced weapon ever! Fires plasma bolts which explode on impact. Great shooting rate, huge damage, big range - simply everything you need.
Saw

(Rush mode only) Close combat automatic saw, which shreds to pieces anyone who touches it. Extremely effective, but has a very limited range.
