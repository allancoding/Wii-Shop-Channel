Story

In a dimension far away, there is a world called the Little Realm.

This world is split into 4 Kingdoms, the Red Sands, the Blue Plains, the White Mountains and the Black Woodlands. These 4 kingdoms co-exist harmoniously, and the Little Realm is mostly peaceful.

However, every 10 years, in an abnormality of nature, a door to another dimension opens. This door leads to the Promised Lands, where legend has it that magic and treasure abound. The early rulers of the Little Realm set a rule that only the most deserving person shall have access to the Promised Lands. So a tournament was started; to seek and judge the strongest and the wisest warrior of them all.

In this tournament, each of the 4 kingdoms sends their most courageous Prince or Princess and their Elite Squad to battle over 9 Rounds. The condition for winning the Tournament is to win all the 9 Rounds. Losing in any of the 9 Rounds will mean defeat.

In the history of the Tournament, there has never been a team which won all 9 Rounds. This means there has never been a Tournament winner, nor has anyone gained access to the Promised Lands.

Game Objective

To win in the Tournament which comprises of 9 levels of progressive difficulty.
