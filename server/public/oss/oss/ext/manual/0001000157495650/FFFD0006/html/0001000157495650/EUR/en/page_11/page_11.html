Types of Attacks

There are 5 main modes of attack in Space Invaders.  These are called “Attack Types.”

Switch between Attack Types with and fire with.

Some attacks are more effective against certain enemies.  Use appropriate Attack Types for more points, and grab yourself a high score.
Each Attack Type requires a certain number of Invaders, so you’ll only be able to use those Attack Types that you have sufficient Invaders for.

Shoot

Invaders Required: 15

Invaders sparkle and charge at the enemy.
This attack can be used continuously, allowing very little room for the enemy to attack.  Although it is an attack that can be used in any situation, it’s not the most powerful option.
Homing

Invaders Required: 20

 

Invaders follow and attack the enemy leaving a trail of light.

This is effective against quick fighter planes, but not very effective against tanks on the ground.
Bomb

Invaders Required: 40

 

A group of Invaders plummet to the ground causing a large explosion.

Continuous attacks are not possible, but this is very effective against buildings and tanks.
Drill

Invaders Required: 20

 

Invaders pierce the enemy in a spiral formation.

Effective against bosses or powerful enemies.
Pogo

Invaders Required: 30

 

Invaders form a ball and automatically bounce around, crushing enemies.

This is a useful attack, but beware of destroying unintended targets.
