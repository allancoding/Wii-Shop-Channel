Special Attacks



In an emergency, press to call in the ”Mother Ship” to help you with its powerful laser beam. This is called a “Special Attack.”

When using a Special Attack, you will need Special Stock in your Special Attack Stock display located in the lower-left corner of the screen. You will lose one Special Stock for every Special Attack.

While using a Special Attack, your UFO and Invaders will be invulnerable to enemy attacks. After a Special Attack, your UFO will automatically regain its full complement of 100 Invaders.
