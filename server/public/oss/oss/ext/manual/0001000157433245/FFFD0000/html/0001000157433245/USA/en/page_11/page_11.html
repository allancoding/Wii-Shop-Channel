Jobs

Each unit type possesses different abilities. Deploy units in locations to best take advantage of those abilities. Gil is required to deploy a unit, as well as to increase one's level. This amount varies depending on job.

 

As the stages become more difficult, you will have to manage your battle funds wisely, lest your coffers run dry in the middle of a campaign.

 

Available Jobs

Depending on the map, not all of the following jobs may be available.

 

Fencer

Though combat range is limited, Fencers have exceptionally high attack power. Their attacks cannot reach aerial targets.

 

Black Mage

A Black Mage's spells can inflict magickal damage to multiple targets over a wide area. However, recast time is long.

 

Berserker

While weaker than Soldiers, Berserkers deal blows which damage multiple foes. Their attacks cannot reach aerial targets.

 

Hunter

Hunters can deal physical damage to both air and ground targets over a wide area.

 

Thief

Though Thieves cannot attack targets, if they are near one when it is defeated, their party will receive extra gil.

 

Time Mage

While not as powerful as Black Mages, Time Mages can wield wide-range spells which slow a target's movement.

Tinker

Through the use of their clockwork ability, Tinkers can stop monsters...and allies...dead in their tracks.
