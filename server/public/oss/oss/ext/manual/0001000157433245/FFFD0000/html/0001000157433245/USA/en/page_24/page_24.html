Ranking

Connect to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection and see how your scores stack up against other players from around the globe.

 

Look for the crown icon on the stage selection screen to find out which three stages are participating in the worldwide ranking. Upon completing one of these stages, you will have the option to upload your score to the worldwide ranking board. Both scores from the Easy and Normal difficulty settings are sent and ranked separately.

 

Worldwide ranking scores are saved separately from your in-game high scores, and are cleared from the worldwide ranking the first of every month.

 

There are no restrictions on when a score can be uploaded to the worldwide ranking board, as long as it has not been done so previously.

 

Update Rankings

This option uploads all of your in-game high scores (when applicable). Rankings can only be updated once every 10 minutes.

 

 

Edit Nickname

To upload your scores to the worldwide ranking board, you must first choose a nickname. Nicknames can be up to 12 characters long.

 

Top Defenders

Here you can view the highest ranking scores from around the world. The worldwide leaderboard is reset on the first of every month, 9:00 am (Japan Standard Time).
