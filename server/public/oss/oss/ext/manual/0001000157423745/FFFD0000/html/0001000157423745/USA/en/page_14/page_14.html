Play alone or with a friend!
  
Choose your level of difficulty: easy, medium or hard.
 
Use the left and right arrows on the screen to scroll through the characters, then select the OK icon to confirm your selection. In 2-player mode, Player 2 must also choose a character. In 1-player Arcade Mode, you can choose your opponent in the same way. If you can’t make up your mind, just choose Random for a surprise character.
 
After choosing the character(s), you can then choose a location in the same way.
