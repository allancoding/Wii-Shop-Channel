In-Game

Aiming Mode:

  
Wii Remote:   Move the Crosshairs cursor across the screen
Move the Wii Remote & hold the A Button:   Aim your shot (faster)
Left/Right on the +Control Pad:   Aim your shot (slower)
Up/Down on the
+Control Pad:		   Zoom the camera in or out
1 Button:   Switch to Effect Mode
2 Button:   Switch to or from Top View
B Button:   Switch to Shooting Mode
+ Button:   Open In-Game Menu
Move Wii Remote & Hold - Button:   Free view and zoom
HOME:   Open the HOME Menu

Shooting Mode:

    
Wii Remote:   Move towards the screen to take a shot
Left/Right on the +Control Pad:   Switch to Aiming Mode
Up/Down on the +Control Pad:   Zoom the camera in or out
1 Button:   Open Effect Mode window
A Button:   Switch to Aiming Mode
B Button:   Switch to Aiming Mode
+ Button:   Open In-Game Menu
HOME:   Open the HOME Menu
