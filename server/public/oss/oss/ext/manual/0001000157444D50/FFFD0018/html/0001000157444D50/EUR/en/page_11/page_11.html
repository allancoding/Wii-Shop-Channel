Basic Rules and Techniques

Dr. Mario and Germ Buster share the same basic rules and objectives. As the capsules enter the bottle and fall, line them up with like-coloured viruses in order to exterminate them. Once all viruses have been removed, a menu will appear and you may choose to proceed to the next stage, retry the same stage or quit and return to the Options Screen.

Note: The TRY AGAIN option will only appear in Dr. Mario 1-Player Classic Mode and Germ Buster.

● Eliminating Viruses

Rotate the capsules and drop them so they are next to, on top of or below viruses of the same colour. When you have vertically or horizontally aligned four or more capsule pieces or viruses of the same colour, they will all disappear.

● Chains and Combos

When viruses and capsules disappear, any remaining capsule pieces will drop, potentially causing more capsules and viruses to line up. These are called chains. When two or more sets disappear at the same time, they are called combos. Getting chains and combos in Battle Mode will send garbage to your opponent.

● Game Over

If capsules stack up to the top of the bottle, your game will end. You can then select TRY AGAIN or QUIT.
