Using WiiConnect24

You can use WiiConnect24 to send a demo version of Dr. Mario & Germ Buster to a friend who doesn’t have the game. This will allow you to play together via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. For details on how to set up WiiConnect24, refer to the Wii Operations Manual – Channels & Settings.

Note: Parental Controls must be turned off to use this function. There is no charge to receive the demo version.

● Registering a Wii Friend

In order for you to send the demo version, the recipient must be registered as a Wii Friend. Wii Friends are different from Friends registered in the game, and are registered through the Wii Message Board. For details, please refer to the Wii Operations Manual – Channels & Settings.

After you have registered each other, the colour of the Wii Friend’s nickname in your Wii Address Book will change from light grey to dark grey.

Attention

・If you are having trouble connecting to the Internet, please check any error codes that appear on-screen and refer to the troubleshooting section of the Wii Operations Manual.
