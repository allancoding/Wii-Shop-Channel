Game Modes
There are three different game modes in Bruiser and Scratch.

  1. Tutorial Mode: By selecting the Sleepy Owl of Education, you can play 11 instructional puzzles, complete with hints to teach you the rules of play. Players are strongly cautioned to play through as many tutorial puzzles as they can before trying other modes. If one is too easy or too hard, you may skip to the next one.

  2. Story Mode: By selecting the Book of Two Tails, you control the journey of Bruiser, and his feline friend Scratch, through 48 mind-bending puzzles on their quest to return home. In this mode, you will eavesdrop on many conversations and meet a number of characters who have their own stories to tell. Each puzzle must be completed in sequence to advance, and there are some real stumpers.

  3. Challenge Mode: If you are worthy, select the Golden Chalice and take your best stab at the most devious, confounding levels in the game. Challenge Mode brings 75 more puzzles of progressive difficulty to wrap your head around. You may play these in any order, so you can advance to the perfect level of intensity, right away.
