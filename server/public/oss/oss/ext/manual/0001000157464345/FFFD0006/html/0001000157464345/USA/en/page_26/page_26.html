　Add-on Content

You can purchase and download add-on content to enhance your play experience in FINAL FANTASY CRYSTAL CHRONICLES: My Life as a King.

You can access this feature by selecting "Purchase Add-on Content" from either the main menu or the in-game castle menu. Purchasing add-on content requires Wii Points.

If you delete add-on content at any time, games saved using the data in the add-on content will become inaccessible. You must download the deleted add-on content again to enable loading of those saved games. Downloading purchased add-on content will not require further Wii Points.

Note: Add-on content is purchased with Wii Points. Visit the Wii Shop Channel or the Nintendo web page for instructions regarding the purchase of Wii Points.

● Infinity Spire (Available: 6/30/08)
300 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 8
Adds 2 dungeons to the game, such as the Infinity Spire. The spire gains levels every time it is cleared, presenting a challenge to even the bravest champions of your realm.

● Castle Renovation (Available: 6/30/08)
100 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 8
Adds a dungeon to the game which adds a jukebox and the ability to rename your adventurers in the castle once cleared.

● Library (Available: 6/30/08)
200 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 8
Adds a dungeon to the game which adds libraries to the building menu once cleared. Libraries teach adventurers race-specific abilities.

● Fresh King (Available: 6/30/08)
100 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 3
Purchase an additional costume for the king--a casual outfit designed for warmer seasons. You can change costumes in the castle immediately after downloading.

● Summer Chime (Available: 6/30/08)
100 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 3
Purchase an additional costume for Chime--a cool outfit suited for tropical climates. You can change costumes in the castle immediately after downloading.

● Dungeon Pack (Available: 5/12/08)
300 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds 11 dungeons to the game. Clearing these dungeons raises the limit on weapon, armor, and item shops as well as taverns, parks, gaming halls, and houses.

 

● Triple Race Pack (Available: 5/12/08)
800 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds Lilty huts, Selkie dens and Yuke shacks to the building menu.

 

● Lilty Hut (Available: 5/12/08)
300 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds Lilty huts to the building menu. Lilties are naturally strong and make the best warriors.

● Selkie Den (Available: 5/12/08)
300 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds Selkie dens to the building menu. Selkies are naturally swift and make the best thieves.

● Yuke Shack (Available: 5/12/08)
300 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds Yuke shacks to the building menu. Yukes are naturally magic-savvy and make the best mages.

● The King's New Cloak (Available: 5/12/08)
100 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 3
Purchase an additional costume for the king--a cute yet regal design using black and white as its primary colors. You can change costumes in the castle immediately after downloading.

● Chime's Knell (Available: 5/12/08)
100 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 3
Purchase an additional costume for Chime--a dark outfit emphasizing Chime's charms. You can change costumes in the castle immediately after downloading.

● Luxurious House (Available: 5/12/08)
100 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds luxurious houses to the building menu. These houses recall an even larger family to the kingdom, boosting income of gil.


● Sacred Shrine (Available: 5/12/08)
200 Wii Points / Blocks Required: 1
Adds a dungeon containing the ability to build a sacred shrine. Sacred shrines built in the kingdom temporarily boost adventurers' stats.


Warning!
The add-on content described in this section is data used by the game FINAL FANTASY CRYSTAL CHRONICLES: My Life as a King. You cannot play using the add-on content alone.
