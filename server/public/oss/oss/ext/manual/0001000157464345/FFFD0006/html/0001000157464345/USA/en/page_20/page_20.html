Jobs
Adventurers can specialize in several different roles called "jobs." Aspiring adventurers become warriors when they are first sanctioned as adventurers.

As the game progresses, dungeons that require more than raw physical strength to conquer will appear. That would be a good time to ask some of the adventurers to change jobs.

Changing to a different job requires the appropriate structure to be built in the realm. Once that is done, you can post a job change behest to gather adventurers suitable for the new job.

Below are the four jobs and their respective structures.

● Warrior
A weapons expert, the warrior excels in physical combat.

A training hall is required to change an adventurer's job to warrior.

● White Mage
The quintessential healer, the white mage is the backbone of any successful adventuring party.

A white mage temple is required to change an adventurer's job to white mage.
● Black Mage
A master of elemental magic, the black mage excels in dispatching foes with offensive spells.

A black mage academy is required to change an adventurer's job to black mage.
● Thief
An explorer and treasure hunter, the thief can pick those pesky locked chests, and also allows parties to avoid unnecessary battles through stealth.

A gaming hall is required to change an adventurer's job to thief.
Adventurers who gather around a job change behest will ask you if you want them to take on the role posted.

Note that an adventurer will lose any abilities learned in the previous profession if they change jobs. Consider your choices carefully.
