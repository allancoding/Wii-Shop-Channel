Townsfolk and Adventurers

There are two types of citizens living in the realm: townsfolk and adventurers.

The townsfolk live in houses and offer the king gil in the form of tithes. Adventurers take on the king's behests and go explore dangerous dungeons in the name of the realm.


Whenever the king builds a house using architek, townsfolk are magically recalled to the realm from their exile. The type of house built determines the size of the families recalled to the kingdom. The more houses you build, the more townsfolk will live in the realm, and hence the more gil you will receive from tithes.

An aspiring adventurer will also be a member of the families you recall by building houses. These aspirants will present themselves at the castle entrance, waiting to be commissioned as sanctioned adventurers of the realm.
