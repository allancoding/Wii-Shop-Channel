Building and Dismantling

You can build structures on any of the glowing ground within the city walls. Summon Chime whenever you want to build a structure.

Note that all buildings require elementite to build.

１．Selecting a Place to Build
First, you must select the location where you want to place a building. Any of the glowing ground is available to build upon.

２．Selecting a Building Type
Next, summon Chime and select the "Let's build!" option from the menu. Then select the type of building you wish to build.


Note: You will only be able to build a few structures at the beginning of the game. More buildings will become available as you progress through the story.

There are several categories of buildings you can choose from, such as citizen shops, adventurer buildings, etc. You can switch between categories by pressing either +Control Pad left or right, and switch the type of building you wish to build by pressing +Control Pad up or down.

３．Building Facing
Some buildings occupy more space than others. You can change the facing of the building to fit the space available by pressing the +Control Pad. The arrow displayed during this phase indicates where the entrance to this building will be placed.

 

４．Using Elementite to Fuel Architek
Once you've selected the location, type of building, and the building's facing, you can now activate architek to finally place the building in your realm. Using architek requires elementite, a resource that is collected by adventurers from within dungeons.

Dismantling Buildings
When you reach a certain point in the game, you will be able to dismantle any buildings you've previously built. Summon Chime and choose "Dismantle" from the menu to perform this action. Any citizens who live in the dismantled building will temporarily move to vacant rooms in the castle. They will take residence in the next building of the same type that is newly constructed. Note that some family members may change during this process.


Note: All but one residential house and the original behest board may be dismantled.
