Building specific structures will give your adventurers access to new jobs and their corresponding abilities.

Warrior abilities focus on protecting allies and defeating foes with weapons. Mages wield spells that heal allies and damage foes. Thieves focus on utility, allowing parties to find shortcuts and sneak past enemies.

You can also unlock more powerful abilities for your adventurers by funding research in each job's respective training building.
