Using Wii MotionPlus
Connecting the
Wii MotionPlus Accessory

Please connect the Wii MotionPlus to the Wii Remote by following the below steps.

  1. Turn Wii MotionPlus so that you can see the arrow on the lock switch.
  1. Insert the Wii Remote into the jacket so that the tip fits snugly.
  1. Push the wrist strap through the opening in the bottom of the jacket. Next, pull the strap through until you see the cord.
  1. Pull the jacket over the bottom corners of the Wii Remote, gently moving the accessory back and forth.
  1. Press the buttons on both sides and insert the connector plug into the controller.
  1. Make sure that the jacket is not caught. If the strap cord gets caught, gently give the strap another tug.
  1. Be sure to slide the lock switch up in the direction of the arrow to lock it into place.
  1. Place your hand through the wrist strap and adjust the strap lock. If the strap lock has a lever, be sure to click it firmly in place.

For information on how to disconnect the Wii MotionPlus, or how to connect the Nunchuk controller, please refer to the Wii MotionPlus Operations Manual.

Precautions for Holding the
Wii Remote / Wii MotionPlus

When holding the Wii Remote / Wii MotionPlus with one hand, do not swing the Wii Remote by holding the Wii MotionPlus sensor unit.


If Wii MotionPlus Is Not Following
Your Motions Accurately

If the Wii Remote / Wii MotionPlus is not accurately following your motions, during the game place the Wii Remote facedown on a table or other stable, level surface and keep it still for a few seconds. Wii MotionPlus will calibrate automatically.
