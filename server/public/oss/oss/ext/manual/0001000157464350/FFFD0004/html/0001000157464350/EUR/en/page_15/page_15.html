Morning Reports
Chime will present you with the previous day's report every morning. There are two types of reports: adventurer reports and the financial report.

● Adventurer Reports
These are the reports of each adventurer's actions from the previous day.
You can also view each adventurer's status and items from this screen.

Select a line in the report and press to view detailed information on each adventurer's actions. These may provide you with useful hints on successfully clearing dungeons, adventurers' strengths and weaknesses, and many more.

● Financial Report
The previous day's financial transactions are detailed in this report. The amount of gil spent, realm income from tithes, elementite recovered from dungeons, and anything else you may need to plan your next day's spending is displayed.
