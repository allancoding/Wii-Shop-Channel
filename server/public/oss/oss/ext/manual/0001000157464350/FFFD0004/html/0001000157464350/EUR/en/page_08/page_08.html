Characters
● Leo
Race: Clavat

A young king who lost his queen mother at a very young age, and his kingdom shortly after. Chosen by the crystal and given the task to rebuild his kingdom in the new land, his task will be aided by the power of architek--a magical ability that has the power to turn memories into reality.
● Chime
Race: Half-Clavat, Half-Selkie

Leo's tutor, advisor, and best friend. As chancellor of the realm, she appears instantly at Leo's side whenever he rings his summoning bell, and helps him with every administrative task including building structures, monitoring the citizens' morale, and even enforcing the young king's bedtime!
● Hugh Yurg
Race: Lilty

A brave hero of countless battles who once led the crystal caravans in Leo's old kingdom. A great advisor on all things martial as well as culinary, he serves the young king with unwavering loyalty.
● Pavlov
Race: Unknown

This foul-mouthed creature suddenly appeared when the king and his retinue entered the new kingdom. While he appears no more than a talking, flightless avian, he seems to know much more about this land than he's letting on...
