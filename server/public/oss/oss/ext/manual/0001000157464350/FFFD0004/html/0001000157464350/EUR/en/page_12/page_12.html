Your Day as a King
Your goal in this game is to build structures and fulfill your people's desires in order to restore your once great kingdom. What you decide to focus on each day is, of course, up to you.

Each day passes in real time, as indicated by the clock at the upper left corner of the screen. The day ends at sunset, when Chime comes to tell the king that it's bedtime.

● Morning
Chime will present you with the previous day's reports each morning. Make sure to check each of them for hints on what the kingdom needs, and plan your day accordingly. At this time you may also post behests, which are orders such as exploring a dungeon or changing jobs, that can be assigned to your adventurers.
● Daytime
There are many things you can do in the castle town, such as selecting which adventurers you want send out on behests, constructing buildings, and chatting with the townsfolk. These chats may contain hints on important things that you need to do, so listen to your people carefully.

It's also fun to watch each citizen go about their daily business, as they act according to their immediate needs and desires.
● Sunset
Once it becomes dark, Chime will come fetch you and tell you to go to bed, effectively ending the day for you. You can also choose to go to bed early if you wish.

As the kingdom prospers and your citizens grow happier, you will be able to stay out longer.
