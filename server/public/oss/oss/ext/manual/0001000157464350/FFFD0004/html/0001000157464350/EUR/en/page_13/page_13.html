Saving Your Progress
Your progress is automatically saved at the end of each day, whether Chime comes to fetch you or you decide to end the day yourself.

Be aware that corrupted save files cannot be restored.
Saving Mechanism
Make sure to avoid the following actions when the game is being saved. Any of them may corrupt your save file. A broken save file cannot be restored.

● Do not press the Power Button on either the Wii console or Wii Remote while saving.

● Do not press HOME or RESET while saving.

● Do not unplug the AC connector while the Wii console is ON.
