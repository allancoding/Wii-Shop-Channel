Puzzle
In Puzzle, you must sink all of the balls on the table with a limited number of shots.
Each time you clear a puzzle, you will move on to the next one. You can create and save up to 32 puzzles, and play them later.

When you are working on a puzzle, you can save particularly good shots for later replay. You can watch the replays as many times as you like in Replay Mode. You can save up to 32 replays, and delete replays that you no longer wish to keep.
