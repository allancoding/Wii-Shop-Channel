General Elements


Time Orbs
By defeating enemies, Celia can use the time particles left behind to recover health and energy. There are two types:

  • Red: Allow Celia recover some of her ki.
  • Green: Allow Celia regain her health.

Closed Areas
On many occasions throughout the game Celia will be trapped in closed time spaces created by her enemies. When this happens, you will hear a ringing bell and the Closed Zone icon in Celiaâ€™s Bar will glow.
To get out of these spaces, Celia must defeat all enemies on the screen. Then you will hear a new ring and the Closed Zone icon will turn blue again.

Story Cutscenes
Sometimes the story of Ark of Sinners is told through various animated scenes. If you want to skip them, you can return to play at any time by pressing the A Button .
Knowledge Monoliths
Throughout the game there are many monoliths that tell what happened in Ark City in the past. Celia can gather that information simply by passing near them. You can see all the texts you have collected so far from the Menu Screen.

Statues
Throughout the game you will find numerous statues containing Time Orbs. Shatter them to restore your health and energy.
