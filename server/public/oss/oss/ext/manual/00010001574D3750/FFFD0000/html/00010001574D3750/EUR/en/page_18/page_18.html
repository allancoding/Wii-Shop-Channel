Tips


When you are fighting multiples enemies, always be sure that you never face directly against more than one at the same time. Since your attacks only affect one opponent, if you try a frontal attack on multiple enemies together they will end up hitting you. Instead, try to separate them by jumping over them or dodging their attacks.
If everything fails, do not hesitate to use Ki techniques.

Make sure to fully explore each section of Ark before leaving it. Otherwise, you must face again all its enemies when you want to return.

Crouching allows you to dodge most enemy beams attacks as well as the rays of the supernatural traps. When you think you are in danger, do not hesitate to quickly press down to avoid damage.
