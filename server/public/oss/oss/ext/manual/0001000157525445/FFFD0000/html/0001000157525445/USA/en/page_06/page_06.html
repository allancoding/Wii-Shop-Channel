Outside

 

You can enjoy a complete adventure mode that takes place in different locations around the planet, exploring different paths and discovering the many secrets hidden in the planet.

 

In these cases, use the Wii Remote horizontally.

 

You will start the adventure upon entering the planet's atmosphere by controlling the direction of the main character by tilting the Wii Remote from left to right to control the descent and landing.

 

Once on the surface, press the +Control Pad in the right direction to move through the different scenarios. However, this is not enough, as you must jump over obstacles using the 2 Button, which also allows you to swim, and defend yourself against various enemies with the laser by pressing the 1 Button.

 

You will obtain various secondary weapons throughout the adventure. To access them, simply press the A Button to remove them and/or store them and press the B Button to select them.
Once obtained, you can use them by shaking the Wii Remote.

 

You can access the interior of the exploratory probe whenever you want by pressing the -Button. By doing so, the control of the game will change, as is explained on the next page.

 

At special times (indicated on screen), you must hold the Wii Remote vertically, point at the screen and fire the laser by pressing the A Button.
