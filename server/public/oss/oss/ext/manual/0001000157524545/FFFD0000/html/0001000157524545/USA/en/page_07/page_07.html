Bonus boxes


When you pick up a bonus box, you randomly get one of the following bonuses: 

Vitamin can




When this bonus is activated, the vehicle accelerates quickly. 

Chewing gum




Chewing gum is thrown behind the car. When a vehicle rides over it, the maximum speed drops for a few seconds.

Bomb




A bomb is thrown behind the car. When a vehicle rolls over it, it explodes!

Soap bar




A soap bar is thrown behind the car. When a vehicle rides over it, the vehicles swerves like crazy.

Almighty




When this bonus is activated, the weapon loads automatically, which means that you can shoot continuously. This also deactivates all your opponents’ arms.

Automatic Drive




When this bonus is activated, the vehicle becomes unstoppable. It speeds up quickly and is in automatic drive for a few seconds.

Shield     




Once the shield is activated, the player is protected against attacks and bonuses for a few seconds.
