Hot Potato
Rules    
At the start of the game, the bomb appears randomly on one of the vehicles. The player with the bomb is invincible and his top speed is increased. The player then must transfer the bomb to an opponent by colliding with him. Each time the bomb explodes, a player is eliminated. The bomb is then randomly transferred to another vehicle. The last player remaining wins the round.
The match plays out in 3 rounds.
