Templar Bashing
  • Templar Bashing

    • In this reflexes/strategy competition, four Templar knights compete to see who survives the longest, dodging and hitting a giant spinning mace. The evil wizard Tom regulates the game, signals the beginning and end of rounds, and speeds up the spinning wheel. Goal: Survive as long as possible, dodging the spinning mace!

  • Rules

    • You will face wave after wave of competing knights, as the mace constanly accelerates. How long can you survive?

  • Controls

    • Swing the Nunchuk to jump, and swing the Wii Remote to hit the mace, bouncing it the other way around.

  • Hint

    • Try to surprise your opponents with sudden changes in strategy!
