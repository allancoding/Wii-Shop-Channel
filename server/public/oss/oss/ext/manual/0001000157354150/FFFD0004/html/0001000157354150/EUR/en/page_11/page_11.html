Jungle Pizza Delivery
  • Jungle Pizza Delivery

    In Jungle Pizza Delivery, your challenge is to keep the savages at bay, while dodging various traps and obstacles.

  • Rules

    • Each round has a minimum speed which you must keep up with, but you may also add pressure to your opponent Pizza Dudes by running faster! Hitting any obstacles will cause you to lose some speed, and if the savages reach you - it's game over!

  • Controls

    • Swing alternately the Wii Remote and the Nunchuk to run, press to jump and to duck/slide.

  • Hint

    • Don't get too far ahead, or it will be impossible to dodge the obstacles.
