Having Trouble Catching Fish?
  • Can't find the right fish?
    Different fish are found in different locations. If you aren't catching the right kind of fish, try casting in a different area. Try changing the depth of your lure. Some fish can only be found at specific depths!

  • Can't catch a big enough fish?
    Some types of fish can be found in a few different spots. If you're having trouble catching a fish of the right size, you might find bigger ones by casting in a different location.

  • Fish aren't biting?
    Check your tackle and make sure it appeals to the fish you're trying to catch. Make sure that you're reeling at the right speed. Remember, once you've got the attention of a fish, you should reel the line in slowly!

  • Keep losing fish during fights?
    Try increasing your line gauge to make your line more durable. Turn on the "Help" option for useful tips!
