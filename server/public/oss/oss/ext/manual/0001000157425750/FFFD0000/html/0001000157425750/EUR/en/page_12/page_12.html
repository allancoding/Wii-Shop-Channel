Scoring

Scoring is based on two things: the sizes of the different critters, and how many critters are fenced in together. The larger the critter, the more points they are worth. The key to getting high scores is getting multiples of the same critter species fenced in together. Each extra critter adds a multiplier to all critters in the same area.

 

For example, on a level with two pigs, if each pig is fenced in a separate pen, each will be worth 75 points.If both pigs are in the same fenced pen, EACH pig will be worth 150 points.

 

Tip - By strategically destroying and building new fences, you can connect two completed pens containing the same critter types together, boosting your score!

 

Bonus points for how quickly the level is completed and number of lives remaining are awarded at the end of the level. Trophies are awarded for high scores!
