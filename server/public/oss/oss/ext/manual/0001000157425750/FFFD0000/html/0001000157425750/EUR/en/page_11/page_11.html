Critter Behavior

Each critter has a mind of its own. Some are downright playful and cheery, while others are just a tad bit cranky and are likely to attack you or other nearby critters. There are two types of critters: Herbivores and Predators. But no matter the type of critter, one touch from any of them will cost you a life!

 

The Herbivores will generally not attack you and simply go about minding their own business. Be careful, though... if you get too close to one you might spook him! Spooked critters are unpredictable. They may just run away, or even jump over or claw their way through a fence! Some have other special abilities, so beware getting sprayed by a skunk or you'll be stunned! There are even Herbivores which lay eggs, creating new critters for you to deal with.

 

The Predators are the ones you really need to watch out for. Not only will they attack the poor Herbivores, but they won't hesitate in chasing you down either.

 

Tip - If you've got a hungry wolf on your tail, lead him to a nearby Herbivore, and he might go chase the critter instead of you.
