Maze Selection

Once you have selected "Play" from the Main Menu you will be presented with a listing of all of the mazes that are included in the game. You'll notice at first that only the first level "Play" option will be active. However, as you complete each level more options will be unlocked.

 

Once you have completed your way through a maze, the game will automatically save your position should you wish to return to complete the level at a later time.

 

The top five High Scores will be displayed on screen when you move your cursor over a specific level.


Challenge Mode

Selecting "Challenge" on the "Maze Selection" screen will allow you to play The Incredible Maze in a whole new way! You'll now have to collect gems that are strewn across the level before you'll be able to complete the maze. Look carefully, they can be hidden! Try to complete the maze as quickly as you can to put up a High Score.
