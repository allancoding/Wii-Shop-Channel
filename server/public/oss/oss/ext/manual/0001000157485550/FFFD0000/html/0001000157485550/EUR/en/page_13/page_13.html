Cells
The cells are positioned on the On-Board Game Map in Adventure mode.

Crossroad Cell


When players pass this kind of cell, the current player must choose a direction.

Repair Cell


If the car has been damaged and players pass by this cell, the players can choose to pay 500 coins to repair the car.

On-Board Challenge Cell


When the car lands on this type of cell, players will enter an On-Board Challenge.

Scroll Cell


When the car passes by this cell, the players will be asked if they want to buy a scroll.

Game Time Cell


When the car lands on this cell, the players will play a mini-game together.

Enchanter Ghost Cell


This cell is only found at the entrances to some special areas. When the car passes by it, the Enchanter Ghost will appear and shrink everyone. Then the players can access the special area. When the players have already been shrunk, passing this cell will change them back to their normal sizes.

Nothing Cell


When the car lands on this, nothing will happen.

-10 Cell and -20 Cell


When the car lands on one of these cells, players will be scared by a ghost that appears suddenly and everyone loses either 10 or 20 coins.

+20 Cell, +30 Cell and +50 Cell


When the car lands on one of these cells, all players will be given either 20, 30 or 50 coins.
