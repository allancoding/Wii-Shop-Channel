Game Modes
            

            

 

Choose between "Multi-Player" and "Single Player" depending on how many people are playing with you.

 

When playing with 2-4 players, select "Multi-Player" and when playing alone, select "Single Player".

 
            

            

 

- Multi-Player

Select between Grand Prix and Single Match Modes.

 

 

Grand Prix

Compete with your opponents for the highest cumulative score after four race tracks.

 

Single Match

Select a specific course and compete with your rivals.

 
            

            

 

- Single Player

Select between Grand Prix and Time Attack Modes.

 

Grand Prix

Compete against 5 other CPU opponents for the highest cumulative score after four race tracks.

 

Time Attack

Challenge specific courses without other cars on the

track to hone your skills and gain an edge on the

competition.
