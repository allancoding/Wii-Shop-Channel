7
Items
While in possession of items, press any direction on the the +Control Pad to use them.

 

【Acceleration】

 

<Take your speed to the limit!>

Accelerate to speeds beyond the limit of the cart for a short period of time.

 

 

【Super Barrier】

 

<Reflect attacks with a barrier!>

Put up a barrier around the car for a short period of time, negating opponents' attacks.

 

 

【Lightning】

 

<Catch up in the blink of an eye!>

For a short period of time, advanced computers will take over the controls of the go-kart, navigating the course at blinding speeds.

 

 

【Bowling Bomb】

 

<Aim at rivals...then strike!?>

Launch a "bowling bomb" at opponents. If it lands, it will explode, and the victim will lose control.

 

 

【Oil Spill】

 

<Send opponents out of control!>

Spray oil behind the car. If any car runs over the oil spills, then it will spin out of control.

 

 

【Psychic Power】

 

<Strange powers take over opponents!?>

Use this and a strong psychic force will infiltrate opponents' minds, stealing control of their machines for a short period of time.
