Abilities

In And Yet It Moves you are a paper man and have the familiar abilities to run left, right and jump. But this time you’re cooler than that! You also have the power to turn the world, at any time you choose.

This has clear implications on the game environment: walls become ﬂoors, tunnels can suddenly be used as slides, or a stone resting harmlessly on the ground may shift position, becoming either a dangerous obstacle or useful object for catapulting.

To successfully navigate through a level, you must consider not only your own place in this rotating world, but also other objects like stones, drops of water and tree branches, as well as living creatures such as bats, monkeys and many more.
