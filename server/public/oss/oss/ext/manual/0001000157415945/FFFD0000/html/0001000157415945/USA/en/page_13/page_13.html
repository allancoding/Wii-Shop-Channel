General Control Settings

There are four different ways to control your character and the world in And Yet It Moves. Different people prefer different ways to play, so we encourage you to try them all out and see which one fits your playing style the best!   

Each control method has low, medium and high sensitivity settings.
These determine how much the movements of the Wii Remote influence the world’s rotation. A low sensitivity setting requires bigger movements to rotate, while a high sensitivity setting means that small movements will be enough.

In addition, you can always invert the world’s rotation. You can activate this setting if you feel that the world always rotates in the direction opposite how you want it to.
