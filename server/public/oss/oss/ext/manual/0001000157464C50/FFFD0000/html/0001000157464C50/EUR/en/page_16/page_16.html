Tips and Tricks
Here are a number of little techniques that’ll help you become a pro in no time:
  • Water form
Gather after jumping It’s often a good idea to gather once you’ve performed a jump and landed on a platform, so that you can quickly pull your water back together.
Don’t forget to pool It’s important to keep your water together to stop any from separating and then evaporating. Whenever you’ve just traversed some bumpy terrain, take a second to pool all your water back together before proceeding.
  • Water and ice forms
Double jump Experiment with timings, and you’ll find that you can double-jump while in the water or ice forms.
High jumps If you need to jump high while in either the water or ice forms, use a ramp to build up speed and jump as you near the top of the ramp.
  • Ice form
Jump while still stuck You can jump while you’re stuck to a wall without having to let go of the Stick button first – but you’ll need to stick again at the top of the jump! You can also jump away from a wall: simply tilt the Wii Remote and jump before you become unstuck.
Brakes To quickly bring your ice to a halt, either fully tilt in the opposite direction or use the Stick ability.
  • Cloud form
Use the wind channels Want to move around the environment quickly? Consider using the wind channels to your advantage...
Avoiding the cloud busters Don’t forget that holding down while in your cloud form will not only suck in balloons but also slow your movement – this’ll come in handy when you need to work your way through dangerous areas carefully.
