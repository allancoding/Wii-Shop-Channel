Cloud Form
The final form – but by no means the least powerful – is the cloud form. No longer restricted by gravity, the cloud can soar into the sky and reach previously unreachable areas.
  • Controlling the cloud
    The cloud can be moved to the left and right in the same way that you move the water and ice forms: through tilting the Wii Remote in the appropriate direction.
To make the cloud ascend, tilt the Wii Remote towards you. To make the cloud descend, tilt the Wii Remote away from you.
Note: These controls can be reversed – so that tilting the Wii Remote towards you makes the cloud descend and vice versa – by selecting INVERTED for the Cloud Up & Down Controls setting in the Options Screen (see “9. Starting the Game”).
  • Rain
Simply shake the Wii Remote to ‘rain’ your cloud into a puddle of water – but watch where it goes!
  • Suction
By holding , you can suck balloons inside the cloud to carry them with you. It will also make the cloud slower, which helps when you need to navigate more carefully through an area.
  • Gust
If you press a direction on while using the Suction ability, you will expel a gust of wind in that direction. Gusting has a number of uses:
Boosting Gust in the opposite direction to the direction you’re moving in to get a nice speed boost. For example, if you’re travelling to the right, gust to the left and you’ll be there in no time.
Moving objects You can use Gust to make small objects move, even if you can’t reach them.
Goop mist You’ll discover areas throughout the Aquaticus where the Influence has formed into clouds that block your progress – but they won’t stand up to a good old Gust!
  • Lightning
Fancy giving something a bit of a shock? Hold down to charge up a lightning bolt, then release to fire it beneath you. You can use this to get rid of enemies and also to charge up machinery.
