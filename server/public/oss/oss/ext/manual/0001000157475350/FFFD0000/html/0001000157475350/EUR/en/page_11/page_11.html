Add-On Content
The following add-on content will be available for this game.
Episode 5: "Rise from the Ashes"
Episode 5 from the original "Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney".
Episode 5: "Rise from the Ashes" can be purchased through the "Buy Episode 5" button on the Court Record Selection Screen.
Wii Points are additionally required to download Episode 5: "Rise from the Ashes". (This involves a fee)
Available on: May 14, 2010
Price: 100 Wii Points Number of blocks required: 1
  • Episode 5: "Rise from the Ashes" is the add-on content used by "Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney".
  • You cannot play using Episode 5: "Rise from the Ashes" alone.
  • Free space is required in Wii system memory to download Episode 5: "Rise from the Ashes". If there is not enough free space, either delete unnecessary data saved in Wii system memory or move it to an SD Card.
