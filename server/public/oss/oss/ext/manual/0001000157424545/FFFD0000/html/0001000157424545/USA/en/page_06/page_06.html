Game Play
 
Throwing the Ball
Hold the Wii Remote up.
Tilt the Wii Remote backward to adjust your angle.
Flick your wrist forward in a smooth motion.
 

Meters
When throwing the ball, you can track and adjust your throw using the Power and Angle Meters. Your previous throw is displayed with a gray arrow.

 

Projected Arc - Shows the throw distance at the strength noted by the blinking arrow.

The Bonus shows your current score multiplication. An "X" bonus is awarded for every consecutive cup made.

 

Aiming

To change the cup your aiming for, press the +Control Pad left and right.
 

Scoring
The base scoring pyramid is 3000 for the rear cups, 2000 for the outside and 1000 for the inside. For every consecutive cup made a "2x" bonus is awarded.

 

Example - If you made 3 cups without missing, your next shot would be "8x". The possible points will be displayed over the cups, 24000 for the rear and 16000 for the sides.
